HAT:
Size: 1/3-6/9-12/18 months (2-3/4-5/6) years
Head circumference in cm: 
40/42 - 42/44 - 44/46 (48/50 - 50/52 - 52/54)

50-50-50 (50-100-100) g colour no 3620, red

DROPS CROCHET HOOK size 4.5 mm – for crochet border.

NECK WARMER:
Size: 1/3-6/9-12/18 months (2-3/4-5/6) years 
Materials: DROPS ALPACA
50-50-50 (50-100-100) g colour no 3620, red. 
50 g for all sizes colour no 1101, white

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40 cm) size 5 mm – or size needed to get 17 sts x 22 rows in stocking st with 2 threads Alpaca = 10 x 10 cm.

INCREASING TIP: 
Inc 1 st inside 1 edge st by making 1 YO. On next row work YO into back of loop to avoid a hole. 
GARTER ST (back and forth on needle): K all rows.
GARTER ST (in the round):
K 1 round, P 1 round. 
--------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Beg with the ear flaps, worked back and forth on double pointed needles, then cast on sts for the hat and work hat in the round on double pointed needles. 

EAR FLAP: 
Worked back and forth on double pointed needles. Cast on 6-6-6 (6-6-7) sts on double pointed needles size 5 mm with 2 threads white. Work GARTER ST – see above – back and forth on needle (first row = RS). AT THE SAME TIME on row 3 inc 1 st each side - See INCREASING TIP – and repeat the inc on every other row a total of 5-6-7 (7-8-8) times = 16-18-20 (20-22-23) sts. After the last inc piece measures approx 3.5-4-4.5 (4.5-5-5) cm. Work 1 row from WS, AT THE SAME TIME inc 2-2-2 (4-4-4) sts evenly = 18-20-22 (24-26-27) sts. Slip sts on a stitch holder and knit one more ear flap. 

HAT:
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 6-6-5 (5-4-4) sts on double pointed needles size 5 mm with 2 threads white, work 1 ear flap in on double pointed needles (= 18-20-22 (24-26-27) sts), cast on 17-18-18 (19-20-20) sts (= front), work the other ear flap in on double pointed needles (= 18-20-22 (24-26-27) sts) and cast on 6-6-5 (5-4-3) sts = 65-70-72 (77-80-81) sts on needle. Insert a marker, and now measure piece from here. Continue in GARTER ST – see above – until piece measures 5-5-6 (6-7-8) cm from marker. Now continue in stocking st with 2 threads red. When piece measures 9-10-11 (13-15-18) cm from marker insert 5-5-6 (7-8-9) new markers in piece with 13-14-12 (11-10-9) sts between each. Now dec 1 st after each marker by K2 tog (i.e. = 5-5-6 (7-8-9) dec sts per round). Dec like this on every other round a total of 11-12-11 (10-9-8) times = 10-10-6 (7-8-9) sts left on needle. Now K2 tog all the way round = 5-5-3 (4-4-5) sts. Cut the thread, pull it through remaining sts, tighten and fasten. Hat measures approx 19-20-21 (22-23-24) cm from marker mid back.

CROCHET BORDER: 
Crochet a border round the edge of hat from RS with crochet hook size 4.5 mm and 2 threads white as follows: 1 dc at the point of one ear flap and continue as follows: * 3 ch, 1 tr in the first of the 3 ch, skip approx 1 cm, 1 dc in next st *, repeat from *-* and finish with 3 ch, 1 tr in the first of the 3 ch and 1 sl st in dc from beg of round. Cut and fasten thread. 

POMPOM: 
Make 2 pompoms with a diameter of approx 5 cm with white. Sew 1 pompom to the point of each earflap. 
--------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER: 
Worked in the round on circular needle from top down. Cast on 54-66-72 (84-96-102) sts on circular needle size 5 mm with 2 threads red. Work 2 rows GARTER ST – see above – and continue in rib K3/ P3. When piece measures 5-6-7 (7-8-8) cm inc 1 st in each P-section by working 2 sts in the first P-st = 63-77-84 (98-112-119) sts. Repeat the inc in the last and first P-st alternately in each P-section on every 2-2-2 (3-3-3) round another 6-6-6 (5-5-5) times = 117-143-156 (168-192-204) sts.
Continue with K3/P10-10-10 (9-9-9) until piece measures 11-12-13 (15-17-18) cm. Now change to 2 threads white. Work 2 cm garter st and cast off. 

CROCHET BORDER: 
Crochet a border round bottom edge from RS with crochet hook size 4.5 mm and 2 threads white as follows: 1 dc, * 3 ch, 1 tr in the first of the 3 ch, skip approx 1 cm, 1 dc in next st *, repeat from *-* and finish with 3 ch, 1 tr in the first of the 3 ch, skip approx 1 cm and 1 sl st in dc from beg of round.
