Knitting Tension: 23 sts x 32 rows on needles size 3 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm, 20 sts x 20 rows in garter sts on needles size 4.5 mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Pattern: See diagrams M.1 and M.2, the diagrams show 1 repeat. 

------------------

Cast on 96-108-120 sts on needles size 3 mm with Karisma Superwash. Knit M.1 and increase on the last round as shown in diagram = 112-126-140 sts. Continue to knit M.2. Remember the knitting tension! When the piece measures 17-19-21 cm knit the 4 sts in each cable tog 2 by 2 = 96-108-120 sts. 
Knit 3 rounds with K2, P2 and then knit the P2’s tog to P1 = 72-81-90 sts. Knit 3 rounds with K2, P1, and then knit the K2’s tog to K1 = 48-54-60 sts. Knit 3 rounds with K1, P1 and then knit all sts tog 2 by 2 = 24 - 27-30 sts. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts, pull tight and sew the thread. 
The hat now measures 21-23-25 cm.

NECK WARMER:

Knitting Tension: 20 sts x 20 rows in garter sts on needles size 4.5 mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.

Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.
------------------

Work begins at the bottom of the front piece and you bind off at the bottom of the back piece, and knit the neck itself last.

Front piece: Cast on 42-46-50 sts on needles size 4.5 mm with Karisma Superwash and knit garter sts. Remember the knitting tension! When the piece measures 7-9-10 cm bind off the centre 14-16-18 sts for the neck. Knit each shoulder piece separately.
1st shoulder piece: = 14-15-16 sts. Continue in garter sts – at the same time bind off at the neck side on every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 3 times = 6-7-8 sts. When the piece measures 11-13-15 cm insert a marking thread (= the middle of the shoulder) – continue to measure from this point. On the next row cast on 2 new sts at the neck side and put sts on a thread.
2nd shoulder piece: Knit as the 1st shoulder piece.
Back piece: Put sts from one of the shoulder pieces back on  needle, cast on 26-28-30 sts and put sts from the other shoulder piece on the same needle = 42-46-50 sts. Continue in garter sts. When the piece measures 11-13-15 cm from the shoulder, bind off.
Neck: Pick up approx 76-86 sts  (divisible by 4) around the neck on double pointed needles size 3 mm. Purl 1 round, and Knit 1 round. At the same time inc number of sts evenly distributed on round to 92-98-102. Continue in Rib. When the neck measures 7-8-9 cm bind off in Rib.
