Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: 23 sts x 32 rows on needles size 3 mm and stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*

Pattern: See diagram M.1, the pattern is knitted in Rib.
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Knit the sock in stripes or one colour.  
Cast on 44-48-52 (56-60-68) sts with Karisma on double pointed needles size 3 mm. Knit 11-12-13 (14-15-16)cm Rib as described in M.1. Continue in light brown - at the same time after M.1, knit 1 round displacing the rib 1 st to the left as follows: Over (K2, P2) knit P1, K2, P1, * knit 3 rounds with K over K and P over P, and displace the rib 1 st to the left*, repeat from *-* until the piece measures 27-30-33 (36-39-42) cm. Knit all P sts together 2 by 2, knit another 2 rounds with K over K and P over P and then knit all K sts tog 2 by 2. Knit another 2 rounds with K over K and P over P and then knit all sts tog 2 by 2 = 11-12-13 (14-15-17) sts left. Cut the thread and pull through the remaining sts, sew tight. The sock measures approx 29-32-35 (38-41-44) cm.
