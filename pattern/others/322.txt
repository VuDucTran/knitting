Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

50-100-100 g colour no 50, dark brown

Size: approx. 14x120 – 17x140 – 20x160 cm.

Knitting Tension: 16 sts x 20 rows on needles size 5.5 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib:* K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 72-80-84 sts on double pointed needles size 5.5 mm. Knit 6 rounds Rib and continue in stocking sts, at the same time dec 2-3-0 sts evenly on first round of stocking sts = 70-77-84 sts. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 10-11-12 cm insert 7 marking threads with 10-11-12 sts between each thread. Now dec 1 st to the right of all marking threads by knitting tog 2 sts on every 4th round 3 times and on every other round 5-6-7 times = 14 sts left on round. Knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. Sew. The hat measures approx 19-21-23 cm. 


SCARF:
Knitting Tension: 14 sts on needles size 7 mm in garter sts = width 10 cm.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows.

Cast on 20-24-28 sts on needles size 7 mm with light brown and knit garter sts. When piece measures 20 cm knit 4 rows in off-white and continue in light brown until piece measures 98-118-138 cm. Knit another 4 rows in off-white and finish the scarf in light brown. Cast off when scarf measures 120-140-160 cm.
