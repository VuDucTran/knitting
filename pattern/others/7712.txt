See diagram A.1. Work entire pattern in stocking st. 

GARTER ST (back and forth on needle): 
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.
----------------------------------------------------------

Worked in the round on circular needle and then on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 104-116-128 sts on circular needle size 3 mm with beige. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 3 cm, rounds beg mid back. Switch to circular needle size 3.5 mm and continue in diagram A.1 – AT THE SAME TIME on 1st round, dec 8-8-8 sts evenly = 96-108-120 sts (= 8-9-10 repetitions of A.1). REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When A.1 has been worked 1 time vertically, continue until finished with beige. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 14-16-18 cm, insert 8-9-10 markers in the piece, 12 sts apart. On next round, dec 1 st after every marker by K 2 tog. Repeat dec every other round 6 more times and then every 4th round 4 times = 8-9-10 sts remain on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts and tighten tog. The hat measures approx. 20-22-24 cm vertically.
