PATTERN: 
See diagram A.1. Work entire pattern in stocking st.

DECREASE TIP:
Dec 1 st after marker as follows: Slip 1 st as if to K, K 1, psso.
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle, switch to double pointed needles when needed. Cast on 112-116-120 sts on circular needle size 2.5 mm with light grey. K 1 round. Then work rib = K 2/P 2 for 3-4-4 cm. Switch to circular needle size 3 mm. K 1 round while dec 8 sts evenly = 104-108-112 sts. Then continue pattern in the round according to diagram A.1. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When A.1 has been worked 1 time vertically, work in stocking st with dark blue, AT THE SAME TIME on 1st round dec 5-9-4 sts evenly = 99-99-108 sts. When piece measures 14-16-18 cm, insert 9 markers in the piece, 11-11-12 sts between each marker. On next round dec after every marker - SEE DECREASE TIP (= 9 sts dec). Repeat dec every other round a total of 7 times = 36-36-45 sts remain on needle. * K 1 round, on next round K all sts tog two by two *, repeat from *-* 1 more time = 9-9-12 sts remain on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. The hat measures approx. 19-21-23 cm vertically.

POMPOM:
Make a pompom with dark blue and an approx. of diameter 4-5 cm and fasten it at the tip of the hat.


----------------------------------------------------------
NECK WARMER:

GARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.

ENGLISH RIB (back and forth on needle incl. 1 edge st in each side):
ROW 1: 1 edge st in garter st, * K 1, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P *, repeat from *-* until 2 sts remain, finish with K 1 and 1 edge st in garter st.
ROW 2: 1 edge st in garter st, * 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P, K tog YO and slipped st *, repeat from *-* until 2 sts remain, finish with 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P, 1 edge st in garter st.
ROW 3: 1 edge st in garter st, * K tog YO and slipped st, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P *, repeat from *-* until 3 sts remain, K tog YO and slipped st, 1 edge st in garter st.
Repeat 2nd and 3rd row upwards.
----------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 37-41-45 sts on needle size 3 mm with Merino Extra Fine. Work 2 ridges in GARTER ST - see explanation above. Switch to needle size 4 mm and work ENGLISH RIB - see explanation above. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When piece measures approx. 45-46-47 cm (adjust so that next row is worked from RS), switch to needle size 3 mm again. Work 2 ridges back and forth over all sts and cast off K from RS.
