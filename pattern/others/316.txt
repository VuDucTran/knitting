HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: DROPS Karisma Superwash from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no 60, turquoise 
50-50-50 g colour no 45, light olive
50-50-50 g colour no 13, pink
50-50-50 g colour no 11, orange 
50-50-50 g colour no 10, mustard
50-50-50 g colour no 01, off-white

DROPS pointed and double pointed needles size 4 mm
DROPS crochet hook size 3.5mm

Knitting Tension: 21 sts x 28rows on needles size 4 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm,
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows
Garter sts (round on double pointed needles): knit 1 round, purl 1 round
Pattern: See diagrams M.1 to M.4. The pattern is knitted in stocking sts. 
Decreasing tips (applies to earflaps): dec on both sides of the middle st. 
Dec as follows before the middle st: slip 1 st as if to knit, K1, psso
Dec as follows after the middle st: K2 tog.

Earflaps: knitted from top down and sewn on to hat afterwards. Cast on 9-13-13 sts on needles size 4 mm with turquoise. Knit M.1 – begin at row for appropriate size – at the same time inc at sides and dec in the middle as follows:  Inc 1 st each side on every other row by knitting 2 sts in the first and last st from the right side a total of 4 times. Dec in the middle – see decreasing tips above – on every other row until there are 3 sts left. Cast off, earflap measures approx 5-6.5-6.5 cm. Now pick up approx 22 to 30 sts along the 2 sides on double pointed needles size 4 mm with orange. Insert a marking thread at the top (point). 
Read all of the following before continuing!
Continue as follows in garter sts:  2 rows orange, 2 rows pink, 2 rows orange, 2 rows pink and 2 rows turquoise, at the same time inc 1 st on both sides of the marking thread on every other row = 10 inc sts. Cast off loosely.

With crochet hook size 3mm and turquoise crochet as follows in the cast-off row: 1 dc, *3 ch, 1 tr in the first ch just crochet (= 1 picot), skip 2 sts, 1 dc in next st*, repeat from *-* all the way round.
The earflap is now approx 10-12-12 cm wide and 8-9.5-9.5 cm long. Knit another earflap in the same way.

Hat: Cast on 88-96-104 sts on double pointed needles size 4mm with turquoise. Knit 6 rounds in garter sts. Continue in stocking sts and M.2, M.3 (see arrow for appropriate size) and then M.4. After M.4 finish the hat in turquoise. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 13 cm dec 3-1-4 sts evenly on round = 85-95-100 sts. Now insert 5 marking threads with 17-19-20 sts between each thread and dec by knitting tog 2 sts to the right of all marking threads on every other row until there are 10 sts left.  Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts, pull tight and sew. The hat now measures approx 24-25-26 cm.

Assembly:  sew an earflap to each side of the hat with approx 14-14-16 cm between the flaps mid front and approx 8-8-10 cm mid back. Place the earflap within the cast-on row on hat and sew on with neat sts.
