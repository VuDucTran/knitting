1/3 - 6/9 - 12/18 months (2 - 3/4 - 5/6) years

Inc 1 st by picking up 1 st from previous round and knitting it.

DECREASE TIP:
Dec as follows before marker: K2 tog.
Dec as follows after marker: Slip 1 st as if to K, K1, psso. 
----------------------------------------------------------

PANTS:
Worked in the round on circular needle from waist down. 
Cast on 114-120-120 (126-132-138) sts on circular needle size 3.5 mm with 2 threads Delight. Work rib = K3/P3 for 8-9-9 (10-10-10) cm. Switch to circular needle size 4 mm and K 1 round – AT THE SAME TIME dec 22-24-20 (22-24-26) sts evenly = 92-96-100 (104-108-112) sts. Insert a marker mid front and a marker mid back (= 46-48-50 (52-54-56) sts between markers). Continue in stocking st until piece measures 12-14-15 (15.5 - 16 - 17) cm. Now inc 1 st on each side of the 4 sts mid front (2 sts on each side of marker) – SEE INCREASE TIP. Repeat inc on every other round a total of 8-9-9 (10-11-11) times = 108-114-118 (124-130-134) sts. After all inc the piece measures approx. 18-21-22 (23-24-25) cm. On next round cast off 6 sts mid front and mid back (i.e 3 sts on each side of marker) = 48-51-53 (56-59-61) sts on each leg. 

LEG: 
Slip sts from one leg on a stitch holder and sts from the other leg on double pointed needles size 4 mm = 48-51-53 (56-59-61) sts. Work stocking st in the round – insert a marker at beg of round = inside of leg. AT THE SAME TIME when the leg measures 4-4-4 (5-6-6) cm dec 1 sts on each side of marker – SEE DECREASE TIP. Repeat dec every 2-3-4 (5-6-8) cm a total of 4 times = 40-43-45 (48-51-53) sts. Switch to double pointed needles size 3.5 mm when leg measures 12-15-18 (23-28-33) cm. K 1 round – AT THE SAME TIME inc 8-5-9 (6-9-7) sts evenly = 48-48-54 (54-60-60) sts. Continue in rib = K3/P3 for 10 cm. Then loosely cast off sts with K over K and P over P. 
The entire pants measures 40-46-50 (56-62-68) cm. 
Slip sts from stitch holder back on needle and knit another leg. 

ASSEMBLY: 
Sew tog the opening between the legs. 
Fold the legs at the bottom if needed.
