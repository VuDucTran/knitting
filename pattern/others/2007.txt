Crochet Tension: 9.5 tr x 6 rows on hook size 7mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Crochet info: Replace the first tr on each round with 3 ch, and finish each round with a sl st in the 3rd ch from beginning of round. 

Read crochet info!
Crochet 3 ch on hook size 7 mm and make a loop with a sl st.
1st round: Crochet 4 tr around the loop.
2nd round: Crochet 2 tr in each tr from previous round = 8 tr
3rd round: *1 tr in the first tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 12 tr
4th round: *1 tr in the first tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 18 tr
5th round: *1 tr in the first 2 tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 24 tr
6th round: *1 tr in the first 3 tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 30 tr
7th round: *1 tr in the first 2 tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 40 tr

Finish the increases here for size 3/5 years

8th round:
Size 6/9 years: *1 tr in the first 9 tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 44 tr
Size 10/14 years: *1 tr in the first 4 tr, 2 tr in the next tr*, repeat from *-* = 48 tr

All sizes: Continue to crochet 1 tr in each tr until the hat measures approx. 23-25-27 cm from the top.

Earflap: Crochet loosely 5-5-6 ch (inclusive of 3 ch to turn with) on hook size 7 mm.
1st row: Crochet 1 tr in each ch = 3-3-4 tr (1st tr = 3 ch), cut the thread.
2nd row: Turn the piece, crochet 3 ch and crochet 1 tr in between each tr from previous row. Around the 3 ch at the end crochet 7 tr and continue to crochet tr’s in between each tr from the 1st the row without turning the piece. Finish with a tr in the last tr from previous row = 13-13-15 tr around the “bend”, cut the thread.
3rd row: Turn the piece, 3 ch, 1 tr in between each tr from previous row – but at each side of the centre tr at the top of the “bend” crochet 2 tr, finish with a tr in the last tr from previous row = 16-16-18 tr
Size 3/5 years: Cut the thread

Size 6/9 and 10/14 years: 
4th row: Turn the piece, 3 ch, 1 tr in between each tr from previous row – but in between the 4 centre tr at the top of the “bend” crochet 2 tr, finish with a tr in the last tr from previous row = 20-22 tr

All sizes: The earflap measures approx. 8-10-10 cm in the width at the top, and 6.5-8-8.5 cm in the height. Crochet one more flap. Sew 1 flap each side of the hat, leaving a gap of approx. 14-15-17 cm at the centre front – sew the flaps edge by edge with the hat.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MITTENS:
Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm

Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*. 

Decreasing tips: Dec as follows before the marking thread: K2 tog. Dec as follows after the marking thread: Slip 1 st as if to knit, K1, psso.

Cast on 16-18-22 sts with red Eskimo on double pointed needles size 8 mm. Knit 5-6-7 cm Rib and continue in stocking sts. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 7-8-10 cm knit next round as follows: Insert a marking thread (= side), knit 1 st, put the next 2-3-3 sts on thread for thumb, knit 5-5-7 sts, insert a marking thread (= side), knit the remaining 8-9-11 sts. On next row cast on 2-3-3 new sts over the thumb sts. Continue in stocking sts. When piece measures 5-7-8 cm from the thumb opening dec on each side of both marking threads – see Decreasing tips above – on every other row a total of 3-3-4 times = 4-6-6 sts left. Cut the thread and pull through remaining sts, sew tight. The mitten measures approx 16-19-23 cm.
Thumb: Put the 2-3-3 sts from thread back on needle size 8mm, pick up 2-3-3 sts behind the opening and 1 st on each side = 6-8-8 sts. Knit stocking sts round on needles. When thumb measures 3.5-4.5-5.5 cm, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts, sew tight.

Knit another mitten but with the thumb the opposite side.
