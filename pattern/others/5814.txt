DROPS design: Pattern no U-054-bn
Yarn group B
---------------------------------------------------------
Size: 3/5 - 6/9 - 10/12 years
Head circumference: 52/53 - 54/55 - 55/56 cm 
Height: approx. 44-46-49 cm

Materials: DROPS KARISMA from Garnstudio
50 g colour no 64, grey purple
50 g colour no 40, light old pink 

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 4.5 mm - or size needed to get 20 sts x 26 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 4 mm – for rib

You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comSTRIPES:
Work * 6 rounds light old pink, 6 rounds grey purple *, repeat from *-*.

DECREASE TIP: 
Dec 2 sts before marker as follows: Work until 3 sts remain before marker, slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, (marker) = 2 dec sts. 
---------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. Cast on 72-80-88 sts on double pointed needles size 4 mm with light old pink. 
ROUND 1 to 2: K all sts. 
ROUND 3 to 6: * K 1, P 1 *, repeat from *-* the entire round. 
Then switch to double pointed needles size 4.5 mm and continue in stocking st and with STRIPES - read explanation above - until finished measurements.
When 12 stripes have been worked vertically, insert 4 markers 18-20-22 sts apart. Then dec on 1st round in next stripe as follows: Dec 2 sts before every marker - READ DECREASE TIP = 8 sts dec on round. Repeat dec on 1st round of every stripe (= dec every 6th round) until 8 sts remain on round (= 20-21-22 stripes in total), cut the thread. Pull thread through all sts and tighten tog.
Make 1 pompom of approx. 5 cm in diameter with 1 thread in each colour and 1 pompom with light old pink, fasten them at the top of hat.
