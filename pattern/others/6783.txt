DROPS design: Pattern no pe-002-bn
Yarn group E or C + C
-----------------------------------------------------------
Size: Child - Teen - Adult
Head circumference: 48/50 – 55/56 – 58/60 cm
Materials: 
DROPS PEAK from Garnstudio
100 g in all sizes in colour no 08, neon pink
50 g in all sizes in colour no 09, neon yellow
50 g in all sizes in colour no 07, neon green

Or use:
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100 g in all sizes in colour no 26, hot pink
50 g in all sizes in colour no 24, yellow
50 g in all sizes in colour no 25, green

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES and CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40 cm) SIZE 8 mm - or size needed to get 11 sts x 15 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.PATTERN: 
See diagram A.1. Pattern is worked in stocking st. 
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle, switch to double pointed needles when needed. Cast on 48-56-60 sts on circular needle size 8 mm with neon pink / hot pink. Work rib K 2/P 2 until piece measures 6 cm. Then work in stocking st – AT THE SAME TIME dec 0-2-0 sts evenly on first round = 48-54-60 sts. When piece measures 7-9-10 cm, continue with colours and pattern according to diagram A.1 over all sts. When A.1 has been worked 1 time vertically, insert 6 markers 8-9-10 sts apart. Continue with neon yellow / yellow and in stocking st – AT THE SAME TIME on next round dec 1 st at every marker as follows: * Work until 2 sts remain before marker, K 2 tog *, repeat from *-* the entire round = 42-48-54 sts. Repeat dec on every round until 6 sts remain. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. Piece measures approx. 21-23-25 cm vertically.

POMPOM:
Make 1 pompom of approx. 10 cm in diameter with neon pink / hot pink. Sew the pompom on the top of the hat. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
