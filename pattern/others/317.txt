Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: Before felting: 11 sts x 15 rows using needle size 8mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. After felting: approx 13.5 sts x 28 rows = 10 x 10 cm.

The bootie in knitted in one piece and sewn together mid front and mid back. Start at the top of leg. Loosely cast on 18-19-20 (21-22-23) sts and knit stocking sts. When piece measures 15-15-17 (17-17-19) cm cast on new sts for foot on one side on every other row as follows: 2 sts 2-2-3 (5-6-7) times, 1 st 4-5-5 (3-3-2) times = 26-28-31 (34-37-39) sts. When piece measures 40-43-47 (50-53-55) cm dec for leg on the same side as you cast on, on every other row as follows: 1 st 4-5-5 (3-3-2) times and 2 sts 2-2-3 (5-6-7) times = 18-19-20 (21-22-23) sts. Cast off loosely when piece measures 62-67-75 (78-82-86) cm. 

Fold bootie double, wrong side against wrong side and sew together mid back (in outer loops of the outermost sts). Sew together mid front in the same way but leave approx 7 cm at the top for leg. Turn bootie inside out - this is the right side. 

If desired, make 2 pompoms in a contrast colour and attach to top front. 



Knit another bootie. 

Felt the booties in washing machine at 40 deg with 1 tablespoon of powder directly in the machine. When felted, fold the leg approx 4 cm, shape the bootie and leave to dry.
