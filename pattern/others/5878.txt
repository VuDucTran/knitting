DROPS design: Pattern no bf-010-bn
Yarn group C
-----------------------------------------------------------
Measurements: approx. 86 x 86 cm

Materials: DROPS BIG FABEL from Garnstudio
300 g colour no 677, green/turquoise
300 g colour no 522, turquoise/blue

Or use:
DROPS FABEL from Garnstudio
250 g colour no 677, green/turquoise
250 g colour no 522, turquoise/blue

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (80 cm) size 5.5 mm – or size needed to get 16 sts x 30 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm.STRIPES: 
* K 2 rows in green/turquoise and K 2 rows in turquoise/blue *. 
Repeat from *-* until finished measurements.
----------------------------------------------------------

BLANKET:
Cast on 3 sts on needle size 5.5 mm with 1 thread green/turquoise Big Fabel or 2 threads green/turquoise Fabel. 
Work STRIPES - see explanation above, AT THE SAME TIME inc 1 st at beg of every row by making 1 YO inside outermost st, on next row K YO twisted. Repeat inc until piece measures 61 cm / 24" measured from cast on edge and straight up to needle (= half the blanket is made). 
Continue with stripes as before while AT THE SAME TIME dec 1 st at beg of every row by K 2 tog. Work until 3 sts remain, loosely cast off and fasten the threads.
