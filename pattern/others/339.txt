Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm.

Garter sts (back and forth on needles): Knit all rows

Stripy pattern: See diagram M.1, if more space when finish with the pattern continue with red until finish.
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Earflaps: Cast on 4-4-5 sts with red and needles size 8 mm. K 1 row from the wrong side and continue with M.1 ( see explanation above) in stocking sts and 1 edge sts in garter sts each side - at the same time inc 1 st each side from the right side as follows: Make a yo inside the edge st each side –twist and knit the yo on the returning row, to avoid a hole – inc 1 st each side on every other row 4 times = 12-12-13 sts. Remember the knitting tension. Continue in stocking sts and 1 edge st in garter st each side until the flap measures 6-7-8 cm. Put aside and knit another one.

Hat: Knit in one of the earflaps on needle size 8 mm, cast on 8-10-11 new sts (= the back) and knit in the other earflap on to the same row = 32-34-37 sts. Knit the next row from the wrong side as follows (continue with M.1): 1 garter st, P 10-10-10 sts, K 10-12-13 sts, P 10-10-10 sts and 1 garter st. Continue in stocking sts with 1 edge st each side in garter sts – at the same time inc 1 st each side as done for the earflaps, a total of 2 times = 36-38-41 sts. On the next row cast on 12-14-15 sts in between the earflaps for the front part = 48-52-56 sts. Now do all measures from this point onwards. Now knit the hat round on double pointed needles size 8 mm as follows: On the 1st round P the new 12-14-15 sts at the front (continue to knit the other sts in stocking sts), then continue to knit in stocking sts over all sts. When the piece measures 13-15-17 cm bind off evenly distributed on every other row: 6-6-6 sts 3 times and 6-7-8 sts 4 times = 6 sts left, cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts. Sew tight. The hat measures approx. 21-23-25 cm from the centre front and to the top.

Sew red slanting sts along the bottom edge of the hat – each stitch should be 2 sts in the width and 4 rows in the height.

Pompons: Sew 1 red pompom at the top of each earflap. The pompoms should be approx. 6 cm in diameter.

---------------------------------------------------------------------

SCARF:
Knitting Tension: 9 sts x 22 rows on needles size 8 mm in English rib = 10 x 10 cm.

English rib: 1st row (right side): 1 edge st in garter sts, *K1, make a yo, slip 1 st as if to purl*, repeat from *-* until 2 sts left on row, finish with K1 and 1 edge st in garter sts.
2nd row (wrong side): 1 edge st in garter sts, *make a yo, slip 1 st as if to purl, K the next st and the yo tog.*, repeat from *-* until 2 sts left on row, finish with a yo, slip 1 st as if to purl and 1 edge st in garter sts.
3rd row (right side): 1 edge st in garter sts, * K the next st and the yo tog, make a yo, slip 1 st as if to purl*, repeat from *-* until 2 sts left on row, finish with K tog the next st and the yo and 1 edge st in garter sts.
Repeat the 2nd and 3rd row.

Scarf: 
Cast on 13-15-17 sts on needles size 8 mm and lilac. Knit English rib – see explanation above. Knit 5 stripes each measuring 22-26-30 cm in the following order: lilac, red, orange, olive and turquoise. The scarf measures approx. 110-130-150 cm, bind off loosely.
