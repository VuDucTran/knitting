Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 10 sts x 14 rows on needles size 9 mm in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.

Decrease tips: Dec inside 2 garter sts from the right side.
Dec after 2 garter sts: Slip a st as if to knit, K1, psso
Dec before 2 garter sts: K2 tog.

VEST

Front piece:
Cast on 27-30-33 (36-39-42) sts ( includes 1 edge st each side) on needles size 9 mm. Knit 4 rows of garter sts and continue in stocking sts. REMEMBER THE TENSION!
Inc 1 st each side when the piece measures 10 cm and then again at 18 cm = 31-34-37 (40-43-46) sts.
When the piece measures 25-26-27 (28-29-30) cm purl 4 sts each side from the right side (continue in stocking sts over the remaining sts). On the next row cast off 2 sts each side for armhole and then continue dec 1 st each side on every other row inside the 2 garter sts – see dec tips above - 0-1-2 (3-4-5) times = 27-28-29 (30-31-32) sts.
When the piece measures 31-33-35 (36-38-40) cm purl the middle 7-8-7 (8-7-8) sts from the right side. On the next row cast off the middle 3-4-3 (4-3-4) sts for neck. Work each shoulder separately and continue with 2 sts in garter sts at the neck side – at the same time dec 1 st each side on every other row – see dec tips: 3-3-4 (4-4-4) times = 9-9-9 (9-10-10) sts left for shoulder.
Cast off when the piece measures 39-41-43 (45-47-49) cm.

Back piece:
Cast on and work as the front piece.
Bind off for the armholes as done for the front piece and continue until the piece measures 37-39-41 (43-45-47) cm.
Purl 1 row (seen from the right side) over the mid 13-14-15 (16-15-16) sts.
On next row cast off the middle 9-10-11 (12-11-12) sts for neck = 9-9-9 (9-10-10) sts left for each shoulder. Continue with 2 sts in garter sts at the neck side and cast off when the piece measures 39-41-43 (45-47-49) cm.

Assembly:
