See diagram A.1(size 3/7 years) or A.2 (size 8/12 years), work entire diagram in stocking st.

HAT:  
Worked in the round on circular needle and then on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 104-120 sts on circular needle size 3 mm with grey blue. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 3 cm. Switch to circular needle size 3.5 mm and continue in stocking st – AT THE SAME TIME on 1st round dec 4-10 sts evenly = 100-110 sts. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! After round with dec work A.1 (size 3/7 years) or A.2 (size 8/12 years) (= 5 repetition on round). 
Continue with stocking st and grey blue until piece measures 14-16 cm, insert 7 markers in piece as follows:
3/7 years: 14-14-15-14-14-15-14 sts between each.
8/12 years: 16-16-15-16-16-15-16 sts between each.
On next round, dec 1 st after every marker by K 2 tog. Repeat dec every other round 4-6 more times and then every round 7-7 times = 16-12 sts remain on needle. Then K 2 and 2 tog until 8-6 sts remain.
Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts and tighten tog. The hat measures approx. 19-22 cm vertically.

Embroider on eyes and snout with duplicate sts according to diagram.
