DROPS design: Pattern no me-022-bn
Yarn group B
----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 20/21 - 22/23 - 24/25 - 26/28 – 29/31 – 32/34
Foot length: 12 - 13 - 15 - 17 - 18 - 20 cm  
Materials: 
DROPS MERINO EXTRA FINE from Garnstudio
50 g for all sizes in colour no 30, mustard

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES AND STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 3.5 mm - or size needed to get 21 sts x 28 rows in rib = 10 x 10 cm. You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 2.80£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comSLIPPER:

LEG: 
Cast on 34-38-42-46-50-54 sts on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm with Merino Extra Fine. Work rib K 1/P 1 in the round until piece measures approx. 5 cm. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION

FOOT: 
Now keep the first 13-13-13-15-15-15 sts on the round on the needle, slip the remaining 21-25-29-31-35-39 sts on 1 stitch holder. Continue rib back and forth over the 13-13-13-15-15-15 sts, AT THE SAME TIME on 1st row cast on 1 new st in each side (edge st) = 15-15-15-17-17-17 sts. When 7-7½-8½-9-9½-10½ cm have been worked on foot, dec 1 edge st in each side = 13-13-13-15-15-15 sts. Then slip the sts from stitch holder back on needle and pick up 15-16-18-19-20-22 sts on each side of middle part (inside 1 edge st) = 64-70-78-84-90-98 sts on needle. NOW MEASURE PIECE FROM HERE. Insert 1 marker mid front of toe and 1 marker mid back on heel. Continue in the round with rib as before for 2 cm. Now finish in stocking st - AT THE SAME TIME K 2 tog on each side of every marker on every other round (= 4 sts less per round), continue dec until piece measures 3-3-4-4-5-5 cm - cast off. Sew seam under foot in outer loops of edge sts to avoid a chunky seam.
