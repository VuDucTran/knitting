K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.

HEAD BAND:
Cast on 24-28-32 sts on needles size 5.5 mm with beige. Work in GARTER ST - see explanation above. When piece measures approx. 42-44-48 cm, loosely cast off. Sew tog cast on edge and cast-off edge, sew edge to edge to avoid a chunky seam. 

CROCHET EDGE: Work an edge along each side of head band with hook size 4 mm and off white, work in outermost st. Work 1 dc in first st, 2 ch, * skip approx. 1 cm, work 1 dc and 2 ch *, repeat from *-* the entire round but make sure to avoid a tight edge. Finish with 1 sl st in first dc. Fasten off. Fold head band double. Can also be used as an open hat without fold.

SCARF:
Cast on 18-21-24 sts on needles size 7 mm with beige. Work 10 ridges in GARTER ST - see explanation above. * Switch to off white and work 10 ridges, switch to beige and work 10 ridges *, repeat from *-* until piece measures approx. 120-135-150 cm or desired length, adjust so that scarf ends with off white. Cast off and fasten the yarns.

FRINGES: Fasten fringes in both ends of scarf. Use off white in the end with beige and beige in the end with off white. Cut strands with a length of approx. 28-30 cm. Distribute them in 3 bundles and fold them double. Thread loops inn through the bottom ridges on scarf, place ends through loops and tighten loops.
