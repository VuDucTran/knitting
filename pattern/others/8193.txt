Yarn group C or A + A

Sizes: 3/5 - 6/8 - 9/12 years
Length: 24 - 26 - 28 cm
Materials: 
DROPS BRUSHED ALPACA SILK from Garnstudio (belongs to yarn group C)
25-25-50 g colour 13, jeans blue

The piece can also be knitted with yarn from:
"Alternative yarn (Yarn Group C)" – see the link below.

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 5 MM – or the size needed to get 17 stitches and 22 rows stocking stitch on 10 cm in width and 10 cm in height.

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 4.5 MM for rib - or the size needed to get 18 stitches and 23 rows stocking stitch on 10 cm in width and 10 cm in height.

You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comLEG WARMER:
The piece is worked in the round with double pointed needles.

Cast on 44-48-52 stitches with double pointed needles size 4.5 mm and Brushed Alpaca Silk. Work rib (= knit 2 / purl 2). Work until the rib measures approx. 4 cm. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Change to double pointed needles size 5 mm. Continue by working stocking stitch until the piece measures 20-22-24 cm. Change to double pointed needles size 4.5 mm and work rib (= knit 2 / purl 2). Cast off with knit over knit and purl over purl when the piece measures 24-26-28 cm (the rib measures approx. 4 cm). Work one more leg warmer in the same way.
