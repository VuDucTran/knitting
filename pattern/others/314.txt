Knitting tension: 16 sts x 20 rows on needles size 5.5mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows.
Garter sts (round on double pointed needles): knit 1 round, purl 1 round.
Pattern: See diagram M.1, the pattern is knitted in stocking sts.

Earflap: Cast on 5 sts on needle size 5mm with 1 thread ice blue + 1 thread grey blue Alpaca (double thread). Knit garter sts. After 2 rows in garter sts inc 1 st each side from the right side by making a yo within 2 sts. On next row purl the yo into back of st to avoid creating a hole. Inc on every 3rd row a total of 6-7-7 times = 17-19-19 sts. Continue in garter sts until earflap measures 6-7-8 cm. Put the piece aside and knit another earflap. 

Hat: Put one earflap on needle size 5mm. Cast on 10-10-13 new sts (= back) and put the other earflap on needle = 44-48-51 sts. Knit 4 rows garter sts over all sts, at the same time inc 1 st each side as described for earflap on every other row 2 times = 48-52-55 sts. On next round cast on 22-23-25 sts between earflaps at the front = 70-75-80 sts. Measure piece from here from now on! 
Continue round on double pointed needles size 5mm and change to 1 thread off-white + 1 thread ice blue and knit 6 rounds garter sts over all sts. Cut the thread, change to double pointed needles 5.5 mm and start mid back. Knit M.1 – see arrow for appropriate size. NB! Make sure threads at the wrong side of pattern don’t pull. After M.1 continue in stocking sts and 1 thread off-white + 1 thread ice blue. When piece measures 11-12-13 cm insert 5 marking threads with 14-15-16 sts between each thread and dec on every other round by knitting tog 2 sts to the right of all marking threads. Continue to dec until there are 10 sts left, cut the thread, pull it through remaining sts and sew. The hat measures approx 23-25-27 cm from mid front to the top. 

Pleat: Cut 15 threads in grey blue Alpaca measuring 50 cm each. Fold them double, pull the loop through cast on row on one earflap and pull the ends through loop. Separate the threads into 3 sections and pleat loosely. Tie a knot at the end – the pleat measures approx 15-18 cm. Repeat for other earflap. 
---------------------------------------------------------------------

SCARF:
Knitting tension: 14 sts x 16 rounds on needles size 7mm in garter sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows.

Loosely cast on 17-20-23 sts with 2 threads ice blue Alpaca. Knit 2 rows garter sts, change to orange Alaska and continue in garter sts until scarf measures 89-109-129 cm. Change back to 2 threads ice blue Alpaca, knit 2 rows garter sts and cast off loosely.

Tassels: Attach 4 tassels each end. 1 tassel = 12 threads of grey blue Alpaca, each measuring 12 cm. Fold threads double and pull loop through the last row on scarf. Pull ends through loops.
