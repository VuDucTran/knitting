Drops pointed and double pointed needles size 8 mm

Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows in stocking sts on needles size 8 mm = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Garter sts (back and forth): Knit on all rows.

Knit from the neck and down.
Cast on 32-36-40 sts with red and needles size 8 mm. Knit 7-8-9 cm Rib, put half of the sts on a thread and the other half on another thread.

Front piece: Cast on 3 sts with orange and needles size 8 mm, knit in the sts from one of the threads and cast on another 3 sts for the other side = 22-24-26 sts. Knit in stocking sts with 2 sts of garter sts each side until the piece measures 9-11-13 cm from the shoulder. Knit 4 rows of garter sts over all sts, bind off.

Back piece: Knit as front piece.

Assembly: Sew a couple of sts on each shoulder.
