PATTERN: 
See diagram A.1.

DECREASE TIP:
Dec 1 st after marker as follows: Slip 1 st as if to K, K 1, psso.
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle, switch to double pointed needles when needed. Cast on 64-72-72-80 sts on circular needle size 4 mm with Nepal. K 1 round. Then work pattern according to diagram A.1 for 5-6-7-8 cm. Switch to circular needle size 5 mm and work in stocking st. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When piece measures 14-15-16-17 cm, insert 8 markers in the piece, 8-9-9-10 sts between each marker. On next round dec 1 st after every marker - SEE DECREASE TIP (= 8 sts dec). Dec like this every other round a total of 3 times and then every round 3 times in total = 16-24-24-32 sts remain on needle. * K 1 round, on next round K tog all sts 2 by 2 * = 8-12-12-16 sts. Repeat from *-* one more time in size 7/10 = 8-12-12-8 sts remain on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. The hat measures approx. 19-20-21-22 cm vertically.
----------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER:
Worked in the round on circular needle. Cast on 66-72-78-84 sts on circular needle size 5 mm with Nepal. K 1 round. Then work pattern as follows: * Work A.1 (= 4 round vertically), 2 rounds in stocking st *, repeat from *-* until neck warmer measures approx. 12-14-16-18 cm – finish after 4 rounds in moss st, then cast off with K over P and P over K.
