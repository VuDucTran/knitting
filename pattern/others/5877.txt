DROPS design: Pattern no bf-009-bn
Yarn group C
-----------------------------------------------------------
Measurements: approx. 84 x 84 cm

Materials: DROPS BIG FABEL from Garnstudio
300 g colour no 161, pink dream
300 g colour no 900, pink/yellow/green

Or use:
DROPS FABEL from Garnstudio
250 g colour no 161, pink dream
250 g colour no 911, fun lilac

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (80 cm) SIZE 5.5 mm - or size needed to get 16 sts x 30 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CROCHET HOOK size 5.5 mmSTRIPES: 
* K 2 rows in pink dream and K 2 rows in pink/yellow/green *. 
Repeat from *-* until finished measurements.
----------------------------------------------------------

BLANKET:
Cast on 3 sts on needle size 5.5 mm with 1 thread pink dream Big Fabel or 2 threads pink dream Fabel. Work STRIPES - see explanation above, AT THE SAME TIME inc 1 st at beg of every row by making 1 YO inside outermost st, on next row K YO twisted. Repeat inc until piece measures 58 cm measured from cast on edge and straight up to needle (= half the blanket is made).
Continue with stripes as before while AT THE SAME TIME dec 1 st at beg of every row by K 2 tog. Work until 3 sts remain, loosely cast off and fasten the threads.

CROCHET EDGE: 
Crochet an edge around the entire blanket with 1 thread pink/yellow/green Big Fabel or fun lilac 2 threads Fabel on hook size 5.5 mm. Beg in one corner, work 1 dc, * 3 ch and 1 tr in the first of the 3 ch, skip approx. 1 cm and work 1 dc *. Repeat from *-* and make sure to avoid a tight edge. Finish with 1 sl st in first dc.
