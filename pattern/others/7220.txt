DROPS design: Pattern no pe-007-bn
Yarn group E or C + C
----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 20/21 - 22/23 - 24/25 - 26/28 – 29/31 – 32/34
Foot length: 12 - 13 - 15 - 17 - 18 - 20 cm  

Materials: 
DROPS PEAK from Garnstudio
100-100-100-150-150-200 g colour no 07, neon green
Or: DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-100-100-150-150-200 g colour no 25, green  

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES AND STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 22 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.GARTER ST (in the round on double pointed needles):
* K 1 round and P 1 round *, repeat from *-*. 1 ridge = 2 rounds. 

GARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K. 
--------------------------------------------------------

SLIPPER:

LEG: 
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. Cast on 28-32-36-36-40-44 sts on double pointed needles size 5 mm with Peak or Eskimo. K 1 round. Then work rib with K 2/P 2 until piece measures approx. 8-9-10-11-12-13 cm. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION

FOOT:
Now keep the first 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts on the round on the needle, slip the remaining 16-20-24-22-26-30 sts on 1 stitch holder. Continue in GARTER ST - see explanation above - back and forth over the 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts - AT THE SAME TIME on 1st row cast on 1 new st in each side (edge st) = 14-14-14-16-16-16 sts. When middle piece measures 4-4-5-6-6-7 cm, dec 1 edge st in each side = 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts. Then slip the sts from stitch holder back on needle and pick up 7-7-9-10-10-12 sts on each side of middle part (inside 1 edge st) = 42-46-54-56-60-68 sts on needle. NOW MEASURE PIECE FROM HERE. Insert 1 marker mid front of toe and 1 marker mid back on heel and work in the round in GARTER ST - see explanation above. When piece measures 2 cm, K 2 sts tog on each side of every marker on every other round (= 4 sts less per round) continue dec until piece measures 3-3-4-4-5-5 cm. Cast off all sts and sew seams under foot in outer loops of edge sts to avoid a chunky seam.
