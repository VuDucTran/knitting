DROPS design: Pattern no pe-005-bn
Yarn group E + E or F
-----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 3/6 - 7/10 - 11/12 years
Head circumference: 52-54-55 cm 
Materials: 
DROPS PEAK from Garnstudio
50 g in all sizes in colour no 08, neon pink
50 g in all sizes in colour no 05, powder pink

Or use:
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
50 g in all sizes in colour no 26, hot pink
50 g in all sizes in colour no 51, powder pink

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 10 mm - or size needed to get 9 sts x 15 rows in moss st with 2 strands = 10 x 10 cm.MOSS ST: 
Row 1: * K 1, P 1 *, repeat from *-*. 
Row 2: K over P and P over K. Repeat 2nd row.
----------------------------------------------------------

HEAD BAND: 
Worked back and forth on needle. Loosely cast on 7-9-10 sts on needle size 10 mm with 1 strand neon pink/hot pink and 1 strand powder pink/powder pink (= 2 strands). Work MOSS ST - see explanation above! REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Work until piece measures approx. 46-48-49 cm or desired measurements. Loosely cast off all sts. Sew cast-on edge and cast-off edge in outer loops of edge sts. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
