HAT:
Work in the round from bottom up. Cast on 48-56-60 sts with amethyst on circular needle size 8 mm. Work rib K 2/P 2 until piece measures 6 cm. Continue in stocking st AT THE SAME TIME on first round dec 2 sts evenly in the middle size = 48-54-60 sts. Continue in stocking st until piece measures 8 cm. Then work in stocking st with colours and pattern according to diagram A.1. After A.1 insert 6 markers in the piece, 8-9-10 sts apart. Continue with white AT THE SAME TIME dec 1 st at every marker as follows: * Work until 2 sts remain before marker, K 2 tog *, repeat the rest of round = 6 sts dec. Change to double pointed needles when needed, and continue to dec this way on every round until 6 sts remain. Cut the yarn, use a needle and baste the yarn through the last sts a couple of times before fastening on the inside. Hat measures approx. 22-23-24 cm.

POMPOM:
Make a pompom with white with an approx. diameter of 10 cm. Sew the pompom on the top of the hat.
