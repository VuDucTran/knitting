LEG WARMER: 

Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 

Cast on 64 sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with light pearl grey. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4 cm. Switch to double pointed needles size 3.5 mm and K 1 round while at the same time dec 4 sts evenly = 60 sts. Continue with A.1. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When A.1 has been worked 1 time vertically, K 1 round with light pearl grey AT THE SAME TIME inc 4 sts evenly = 64 sts. Switch to double pointed needles size 3 mm, continue with rib = K 2/P 2 and light pearl grey for 4 cm, cast off with K over K and P over P. Piece measures approx. 22-25-28 cm.
