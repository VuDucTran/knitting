* K 3, P 2 *, repeat from *-* the entire round. 
Pattern is over 5 sts and no of sts are divisible by 5 minus 1. 
When working in the round, the pattern will displace 1 st towards the left on every round and forms a spiral pattern.

DECREASE TIP:
Dec 1 st before every marker. Work until 2 sts remain before marker, slip 1 st as if to K, K 1, psso.

GARTER ST (in the round on circular needle):
* K 1 round and P 1 round *, repeat from *-*. 1 ridge = 2 rounds. 
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle from bottom up. Cast on 74-79-84 sts on circular needle size 5.5 mm with 2 strands Baby Alpaca Silk. Slip tog and work SPIRAL PATTERN - see explanation above! Continue working spiral pattern this way until piece measures approx. 14-15-16 cm. NOTE! Make sure not to stretch piece when measuring, the hat will contract vertically and in width because of pattern. 
On next round work pattern as before but dec in every other K section by K 2 tog (K the first 2 tog). Work next round with spiral pattern as before but there are now alternately 2 and 3 K sts between every P section in every repetition. 
On next round work all sections with 3 K sts K 2 tog = 59-63-67 sts. 
Work 1 round spiral pattern as before but with K 2 and P 2. Work 1 ridge in GARTER ST - see explanation above and insert 6 markers in piece as follows:
3/5 years: 10-10-10-10-10-9 sts between every marker.
6/9 years: 11, 10, 11, 10, 11, 10 sts between every marker.
10/12 years: 12, 11, 11, 11, 11, 11 sts between every marker.
Then work in garter st until finished – AT THE SAME TIME DEC – READ DECREASE TIP at every marker on every round worked K 8-9-10 times. After dec there are 11-9-7 sts on needle. 
Cut the yarn, use a needle and baste the yarn through the last sts a couple of times before fastening. 
Piece measures approx. 20-21-22 cm.
