Knitting Tension: 16 sts x 20 rows on needles size 5.5 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib:* K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.
Stripes: *6 rounds of dark olive, 6 rounds of light brown, 6 rounds of dark brown*, repeat from *-*.

Hat: Cast on 72-80-84 sts on needles size 5.5 mm with dark brown. Knit 6 rounds of Rib, continue with stripes in stocking sts  - see explanation above – until finished measurements. At the same time on the 1st round after the Rib, bind off 2-3-0 sts evenly distributed on round = 70-77-84 sts. Remember the knitting tension! 
hen the piece measures 10-11-12 cm, insert 7 marking threads in piece (= 10-11-12 sts between each thread). Now bind off at the right side of each marking thread as follows: K2 tog on every 4th round a total of 3 times and then on every other round 5-6-7 times = 14 sts left on round. Knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull it through remaining sts, pull tight and sew the thread. The hat measures approx. 19-21-23 cm.

SCARF:
Knitting Tension: 20 sts x 17 rows on needles size 7 mm in Rib = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib:* K4, P4*, repeat from *-*.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.

Scarf: Cast on 24-28-32 sts on needles size 7 mm with dark olive and knit Rib with 4 sts of garter sts each side, as follows: *18-20-22 cm of dark olive, 18-20-22 cm of dark brown, 18-20-22 cm of light brown*, repeat from *-* 2 times. Remember the knitting tension! The piece measures approx 108-120-132 cm, bind off in Rib
