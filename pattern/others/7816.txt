DROPS design: Pattern no me-030-bn
Yarn group B
----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 2/4 - 5/9 years
Height: approx. 20-23 cm from top and down.
Head circumference: approx. 48/55 cm (the texture makes the hat very elastic, hat will therefore fit head circumference from approx. 48 to 55 cm).
Materials:
DROPS MERINO EXTRA FINE from Garnstudio
100 g for both sizes in colour no 31, forest green

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES and CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40 cm) SIZE 3.5 mm - or size needed to get 22 sts x 30 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40 cm) SIZE 2.5 mm for rib - or size needed to get 24 sts x 34 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CABLE NEEDLE - for cables.

HAT:
Worked in the round on a short circular needle, switch to double pointed needles when needed. Cast on 112 sts in both sizes on circular needle size 2.5 mm with Merino Extra Fine. Work rib in the round according to diagram A.1 (= 7 repetitions of 16 sts). REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When piece measures approx. 4 cm, switch to circular needle size 3.5 mm. Then work pattern according to diagram A.2 (= 7 repetitions of 16 sts - NOTE: Choose diagram for correct size). When A.2 has been worked 2-3 times in total vertically, piece measures approx. 15-19 cm. Now work and dec according to A.3 (= 7 repetitions of 16 sts – choose diagram for correct size). When A.3 has been worked, 21 sts remain on needle in both sizes. P 1 round where all sts are worked P tog 2 by 2 = 11 sts remain on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. The hat measures approx. 20-23 cm vertically.
