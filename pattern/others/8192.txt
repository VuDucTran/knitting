MOSS STITCH: 
ROW 1: * Knit 1, purl 1 *, work from *-*. 
ROW 2: Knit over purl and purl over knit. 
Repeat row 2.
----------------------------------------------------------

LEG WARMER:
The piece is worked in the round with double pointed needles.

Cast on 56-60-64 stitches with double pointed needles size 3.5 mm and Puna. Work rib (= knit 2 / purl 2) for 4 cm. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Change to double pointed needles size 4 mm. Knit 1 round where you decrease 8 stitches evenly on round = 48-52-56 stitches. Continue working MOSS STITCH – see description above. Change to double pointed needles size 3.5 mm when the piece measures 18-20-22 cm. Work rib (= knit 2 / purl 2) for 4 cm. Cast off with knit over knit and purl over purl. The leg warmer measures approx. 22-24-26 cm. Work another leg warmer in the same way. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
