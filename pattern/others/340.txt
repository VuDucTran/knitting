HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: Eskimo
100-100-100 g colour no. 04, orchid + a remainder of orange and red 
Drops pointed and double pointed needles size 8 mm You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 15.30£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Garter sts (back and forth on row): Knit all rows
Stripy pattern: See diagram M.1. If you finish M.1 before finished measurements, continue in red.

Earflaps: Cast on 4-4-5 sts with orchid and needles size 8 mm. K 1 row from the wrong side and 1 edge st of garter sts each side - at the same time inc 1 st each side from the right side as follows: Make a yo inside the edge st each side –twist and knit the yo on the returning row to avoid a hole,  – inc 1 st each side on every other row 4 times = 12-12-13 sts. Remember the knitting tension. Continue in stocking sts and 1 edge st each side until the flap measures 6-7-8 cm. Put aside and knit another one.

Hat: Knit in one of the earflaps on needle size 8 mm, cast on 8-10-11 new sts (= the back) and knit in the other earflap on the same row = 32-34-37 sts. Knit the next row from the wrong side as follows: 1 garter st, P 10-10-11 sts, K 10-12-13 sts, P 10-10-11 sts and 1 garter st. Continue in stocking sts with 1 edge st each side in garter sts – at the same time inc 1 st each side as done for the earflaps, a total of 2 times = 36-38-41 sts. On the next row cast on 12-14-15 sts in between the earflaps for the front part = 48-52-56 sts. Now do all measures from this point onwards. The hat is from now on knitted on double pointed needles size 8 mm as follows: On the 1st round P the new 12-14-15 sts at the front (continue to knit the other sts in stocking sts) , then continue to knit in stocking sts over all sts. When the piece measures 10-12-14 cm dec evenly distributed on every other round: 5-5-6 sts 4 times and on every round 5-6-6 sts 4 times = 8 sts left, cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts. Sew tight. The hat measures approx. 17-19-21 cm from the centre front and to the top.

Sew red slanting sts in to the bottom edge of the hat – each stitch should be 2 sts in the width and 4 rows in the height.

Flower: Embroider a flower at the centre front of the hat.
