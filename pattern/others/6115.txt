Dec by K 2 tog.

BERET:
Work in the round on circular needles, change to double pointed when needed.
Cast on 88-92-96 sts on circular needle 4 mm with powder pink.
Work in rib K2 /P2 until piece measure 3-3.5-4 cm.
K 1 round – AT THE SAME TIME dec 3-2-1 sts evenly = 85-90-95
sts. Place 5 markers on round with 17-18-19 sts between each and continue in stocking st.
On next round inc 1 st – READ INC TIPS! Before each marker, and repeat inc on every other round 7-
8-9 more times = 125-135-145 sts. 
Now dec 1 sts – READ DEC TIPS! after each marker,
repeat dec on every other round 8-9-10 more times = 80-85-90 sts.
Continue dec on every round 10-11-12 more times = 30 sts.
K 2 tog around. 
Cut yarn and pull end through remaining sts, tighten and fasten.
Make a small pom pom with a 4 cm diameter and attach to top of beret. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
