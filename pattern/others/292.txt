HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years

Materials: DROPS Angora-Tweed from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no 01, off-white 
DROPS pointed and double pointed needles size 4mm and 4.5mm

SCARF:
Measurements: 14x140 cm – 17 x160 cm – 20 x180 cm

Materials: DROPS Vienna from Garnstudio
100-100-100 g colour no 21, off-white
DROPS pointed needles size 6mm
HAT:
Knitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4.5mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Garter sts  (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.
Garter sts  (round on double pointed needles): 1st round: knit, 2nd round: purl.

Back piece inclusive ear flaps: 
Cast on 54-62-70 sts on needle size 4 mm. 
Read all of the following section before knitting it!
Knit 1 row garter sts over all sts, *knit 1 row garter sts over the first 12-14-16 sts, turn the piece and knit garter sts back to edge, turn, knit 1 row garter sts over all sts, turn and knit 1 row garter sts over the first 12-14-16 sts (now the other side), turn the piece and knit garter sts over all sts back to edge, knit 1 row garter sts over all sts*, repeat from *-*, but knit 2 less sts each side for ear flap each time (i.e. next row knit over the first 10-12-14 sts each side etc) until there are no sts left on ear flap. 

Hat: 
Cast on 26-28-30 sts between the ear flaps mid front on double pointed needles size 4 mm = 80-90-100 sts. 
Now knit round on double pointed needles. 
Knit 6 rounds garter sts (see above), cut the thread and change to double pointed needles size 4.5mm. 
Begin mid back, knit 2 rounds and purl 1 round and finish the hat in stocking sts. 
At the same time when piece measures 8-9-10 cm mid front insert 5 markers in piece (16-18-20 sts between each marker). 
Now dec by knitting tog the first 2 sts after each marker on every other row a total of 10 times and then on every 4th-6th-8th row =  10 sts left. Cut the thread, pull it through remaining sts and sew. 
The hat measures approx 17-19-21 cm from mid front to the top. 

SCARF:
Knitting tension: 18 sts x 16 rows on needles size 6mm in Rib = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.

SCARF: 
Loosely cast on 26-32-38 sts on needle size 6mm with Vienna. Knit Rib with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. Cast off loosely in Rib when piece measures 140-160-180 cm.
