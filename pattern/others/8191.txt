GARTER STITCH (back and forth):
1 ridge = knit 2 rows. 

PATTERN: 
See diagrams A.1 and A.2. Diagrams show all rows in pattern seen from the right side.
----------------------------------------------------------

HEAD BAND:
Work piece back and forth on needle. Cast on 18 stitches on needle size 5 mm with Air. Work 1 ridge in GARTER STITCH - see explanation above. Then work A.1 (= 18 stitches) over all stitches on needle. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Continue until piece measures approx. 42-44-46 cm or desired measurements. Work 1 ridge. Sew together edge to edge mid back.
----------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER:
Work piece back and forth on needle. Cast on 26 stitches on needle size 5 mm with Air. Work 1 ridge in GARTER STITCH - see explanation above. Then work A.2 (= 26 stitches) over all stitches on needle. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Continue until piece measures approx. 46-49-52 cm. Work 1 ridge. Sew together edge to edge mid back.
