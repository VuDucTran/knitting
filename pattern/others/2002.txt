HAT and neck warmer:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: Alaska
Hat: 50-100-100 g colour no 60, turquoise  
Neck warmer: 50-100-100 g colour no 60, turquoise 
Both: 100-150-150 g colour no 60, turquoise
Drops pointed and double pointed needles size 5 mm You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 3.20£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comHAT:
Knitting Tension: 17 sts x 22 rows on needles size 5 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows.

Cast on 78-84-90 sts on needles size 5 mm with Alaska. Knit 3 cm Rib. Continue with K1, P5 (make sure the K1 is above the K1 in Rib). When piece measures 13.5-15.5-17.5 cm dec all P5 to P4 = 65-70-75 sts. Knit 2 rounds, on next round dec all P4 to P3 = 52-56-60 sts, knit 2 rounds, on next round dec all P3 to P2 = 39-42-45 sts, knit 2 rounds, on next round dec all P2 to P1 = 26-28-30 sts. Knit 2 rounds and knit all sts tog 2 by 2 = 13-14-15 sts left. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts, pull tight and sew the thread. The hat now measures 19-21-23 cm.

NECK WARMER:
Knitting Tension: 17 sts x 22 rows on needles size 5 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.

Loose neck: Begin at the bottom of the front piece and cast off at the bottom of the back piece, and knit the neck itself last.

Front piece: Cast on 38-41-44 sts on needles size.5 mm with Alaska and knit 4 rows garter sts. Continue in stocking sts with 2 sts each side in garter sts. Remember your knitting tension! When the piece measures 7-9-10 cm cast off the centre 12-15-16 sts for neck. Knit each shoulder piece separately.
1st shoulder piece: = 13-13-14 sts. Continue in stocking sts and 2 sts each side in garter sts – at the same time cast off to shape the neckline on every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times = 6-6-7 sts. When the piece measures 11-13-15 cm insert a marking thread (= the middle of the shoulder) – continue to measure from this point. On the next row cast on 2 new sts towards the neck side and put sts on a thread.
2nd shoulder piece: Knit as 1st shoulder piece.
Back piece: Put sts from one of the shoulder pieces back on  needle, cast on 22-25-26 sts and put sts from the other shoulder piece on the same needle = 38-41-44 sts. Continue in stocking sts with 2 sts each side in garter sts. When the piece measures 10-12-14 cm from shoulder knit 4 rows garter sts and cast off. 
Neck: Pick up approx 60-70 round the neck on double pointed needles size 5 mm. Purl 1 round, knit 1 round and continue in Rib. Cast of in Rib when neck measures 7-8-9 cm.
