LEG WARMER: 
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 56-64-68 sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with medium purple. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4-5-6 cm. Switch to double pointed needles size 3.5 mm and continue in diagram A-1 – AT THE SAME TIME on 1st round, dec 2-4-2 sts evenly = 54-60-66 sts. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures approx. 21-25-29 cm (finish with 1 whole border in pattern), K 1 round with medium purple while at the same time inc 2-4-2 sts evenly = 56-64-68 sts. Switch to double pointed needles size 3 mm, continue with rib = K 2/P 2 and medium purple for 4-5-6 cm, cast off with K over K and P over P. 
Knit another leg warmer.
