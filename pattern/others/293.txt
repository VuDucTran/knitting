HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 - 10-14 years

Materials: Alaska
50-50-50 g colour no 02 off-white
100-100-100 g colour no 40, rose
DROPS double pointed needles size 4.5mm and 5.5mm

Knitting tension: 16 sts x 20 rows on needles size 5.5mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K3, P3*, repeat from *-*.
Sewn on flowers: See diagram 1.

The hat is knitted from top down. Cast on 8 sts on double pointed needles size 5.5 with rose and knit stocking sts as follows:
1st round: Inc 1 st in each st = 16 sts (inc 1 st by picking up st from previous row and knit it)
2nd round: Inc 1 st in every other st = 24 sts
3rd round: Inc 1 st in every 3rd st = 32 sts
Continue the increases until and inclusive the 10th round with 1 extra st between each inc = 88 sts.
Now inc on every other round as follows:
12th round: Inc 1 st in every 11th st = 96 sts
14th round: Inc 1 st in every 12th st = 104 sts
16th round: Inc 1 st in every 13th st = 112 sts
NB: discontinue inc for size 3-5 years.
18th round: Inc 1 st in every 14th st = 120 sts
NB: discontinue inc for size 6/9 years
20th round: Inc 1 st on every 15th round = 128 sts
NB: discontinue inc for size 10/14 years
After all inc are complete piece measures approx 8-9-10 cm and there are 112-120-128 sts on round. Knit 6-7-8 rounds in stocking sts without inc and now dec as follows: *knit 12-13-14 sts, K2 tog*, repeat from *-*. Repeat the dec on every other row another 3 times with 1 less st between each dec = 80-88-96 sts. On next round dec 14-16-18 sts evenly on round = 66-72-78 sts. Change to off-white and double pointed needles size 4.5mm. Knit 1 round, purl 1 round, knit 1 round and continue in Rib until the off-white piece measures 4 cm. Cast off in Rib. The hat measures approx 19-20.5-22 cm. 

Sew flowers on hat with off-white as described in diagram 1.
