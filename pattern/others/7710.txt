See diagram A.1 - NOTE: Choose diagram for correct size. Work entire pattern in stocking st.
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle from the bottom up, switch to double pointed needles when needed.
Cast on 100-112 sts on circular needle size 3 mm with cerise. K 1 round. Then work rib = K 2/P 2 for 3-4 cm. Switch to circular needle size 3.5 mm and K 1 round while at the same time dec 16 sts evenly = 84-96 sts. Then work pattern according to diagram A.1 (= 12 repetitions of 7-8 sts). Inc and dec as shown in diagram. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When diagram A.1 has been worked, 48-48 sts remain on needle. Continue with red as follows: * K 1 round, on next round K all sts tog 2 by 2 *, repeat from *-* 1 more time = 12-12 sts remain on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten.
