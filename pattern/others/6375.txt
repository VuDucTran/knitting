HAT:
Worked in the round on circular needle, switch to double pointed needles when needed. Cast on 72-78-84 sts on circular needle size 4 mm with medium brown. Work rib = K 3/P 3 for approx. 4 cm. Switch to circular needle size 5 mm and continue in stocking st until finished – AT THE SAME TIME on first round dec 2-1-0 sts evenly = 70-77-84 sts. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! 
When piece measures 10-11-12 cm, insert 7 markers in the piece, 10-11-12 sts apart. 
On next round dec on right side of all markers by K 2 tog. Repeat dec on every 4th round 2 more times. Then dec on every other round 5-6-7 more times = 14 sts remain on needle. 
Work all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the yarn and pull yarn end through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. Hat measures approx. 19-21-23 cm.

Embroider Alpaca with camel according to diagram A.1 mid front on hat with duplicate stitches. Beg with pattern on 1st round after rib and make sure that head on the Alpaca is between two markers.
