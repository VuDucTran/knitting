HAT:
Cast on 84-90-96-102-108 sts with orange on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm. P 1 round, then work in stocking st. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 6 cm, insert 1 marker (= rolling edge), now measure the piece from here and work according to diagram A.1. 
When piece measures 7-8-9-10-12 cm, dec 3-0-6-3-0 sts evenly = 81-90-90-99-108 sts. 
Then insert 9-10-10-11-12 markers in the piece 9 sts apart. First st after first marker = first st in A.2. 
Now work A.2, but continue with K and P as in A.1 in the orange section. 
After A.2 work with forest green until finished - AT THE SAME TIME dec every other round: 1 st on right side of all markers until 9-10-10-11-12 sts remain on needle. 
Switch to double pointed needles size 2 mm and work 3 cm over these sts for stem. Pull yarn through the remaining sts and fasten.
