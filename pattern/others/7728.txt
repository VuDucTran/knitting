DROPS design: Pattern no cm-010-bn
Yarn group B
----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 0/1 - 1/3 - 6/9 - 12/18 months (2 - 3/4) years
To fit foot length: 9-10-11-12 (14-16) cm 
Materials: 
DROPS COTTON MERINO from Garnstudio
50-50-50-100 (100-100) g colour no 18, medium grey

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE SIZE 3 mm - or size needed to get 22 sts x 45 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm. You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 3.10£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.
GARTER ST (in the round on double pointed needles):
* K 1 round and P 1 round *, repeat from *-*. 1 ridge = 2 rounds. 
----------------------------------------------------------

SLIPPER: 
Worked back and forth on circular needle.
Cast on 32-36-36-40 (44-48) sts on needle size 3 mm with Cotton Merino. K 1 round, and continue with rib (= K 2/P 2) for 8-8-9-9 (10-10) cm – adjust so that next row is worked from WS. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION P 1 row from WS while AT THE SAME TIME dec 4-6-4-6 (6-6) sts evenly on row = 28-30-32-34 (38-42) sts. On next row work eyelet holes as follows from RS: K 1, * K 2 tog, 1 YO *, repeat from *-* until 1 st remains and finish with K 1. P 1 row from WS, cut the yarn.

Slip the outermost 11-11-12-12 (13-14) sts in each side on 1 stitch holder = 6-8-8-10 (12-14) sts remain on needle (= mid on top of foot). Work 6-8-8-10 (12-14) ridges back and forth over the middle sts (= 12-16-16-20 (24-28) rows and approx. 3-4-4-5 (6-7) cm in garter st). Cut the yarn.
Work next row as follows from RS: Work the 11-11-12-12 (13-14) sts from stitch holder in first side of piece, pick up 1 st in every ridge along the side of mid piece (= 6-8-8-10 (12-14) sts), work the 6-8-8-10 (12-14) sts at the front of foot, pick up 1 st in every ridge along the other side of mid piece (= 6-8-8-10 (12-14) sts and work the 11-11-12-12 (13-14) sts from the last stitch holder = 40-46-48-54 (62-70) sts. K 1 row from WS. Insert 1 marker on each side of the middle 6-8-8-10 (12-14) sts at the front. Then work in garter st back and forth AT THE SAME TIME on 1st row inc 1 st inside the 2 markers so that there are 8-10-10-12 (14-16) sts mid front = 42-48-50-56 (64-72) sts on needle. When 2½-3-3½-4 (5-6) cm have been worked from markers at the front, continue like this:
ROW 1 (= RS): K 3, slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K 10-12-13-15 (18-21) sts, K 3 tog, K 4-6-6-8 (10-12), slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K until 6 sts remain on row, K 3 tog and finish with K 3 (= 8 sts dec, 34-40-42-48 (56-64) sts on needle).
ROW 2 (= WS): K all sts.
ROW 3 (= RS): K 2, slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K 8-10-11-13 (16-19) sts, K 3 tog, K 2-4-4-6 (8-10), slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K until 5 sts remain on row, K 3 tog and finish with K 2 (= 8 sts dec, 26-32-34-40 (48-56) sts on needle).
ROW 4 (= WS): K all sts.
ROW 5 (= RS): K 1, slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K 6-8-9-11 (14-17) sts, K 3 tog, K 0-2-2-4 (6-8), slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K until 4 sts remain on row, K 3 tog and finish with K 1 (= 8 sts dec, 18-24-26-32 (40-48) sts on needle).
ROW 6 (= WS): K all sts. 
Stop here in size 0/1 + 1/3 + 6/9 months.

SIZE 12/18 months + 2 years + 3/4 years: 
ROW 7 (= RS): Slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K 9 (12-15) sts, K 3 tog, K 2 (4-6), slip 1 st as if to K, K 2 tog, psso, K until 3 sts remain on row, K 3 tog (= 8 sts dec, 24 (32-40) sts on needle).
ROW 8 (= WS): K all sts.

ALL SIZES:
K 2 row.

ASSEMBLY:
Distribute sts on 2 needles (= mid under foot), place double and sew tog alongside with grafting/kitchener sts.

TIE:
Cut 2 strands of 1 meter. Twine them together until they resist, fold the strand double and let it twine together again. Make a knot in each end. Tread it through the eyelet row.
