Knitting tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*

Loosely cast on 38-42-46 sts with Eskimo on double pointed needles size 8 mm. Knit 15-17-18 cm Rib, bind off loosely in Rib.



SCARF:
Knitting tension: 9 sts x 18 rows on needles size 10 mm in garter sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Garter sts (Back and Forth on row): Knit all rows

Loosely cast on 11-13-15 sts with Eskimo on needles size 10. Knit in garter sts until the scarf measures 85-100-120 cm, bind off loosely.
