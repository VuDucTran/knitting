DROPS design: Pattern no EE-016-bn 
---------------------------------------------------------
Size: 23/25 -26/28 -29/31 -32/34 -35/37 
To fit foot length: 15-17-18-20-22 cm.

Materials: DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-100-100-100-100 g colour no 06, olive
50-100-100-100-100 g colour no 35, lime

+ some left-over thin wool yarn in black, white, and red.

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 9 mm - or size needed to get 10 sts x 20 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm. You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 5.10£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comSLIPPER: 
Worked back and forth in one piece and sewn tog mid under foot when finished.
The whole slipper is worked K on all rows (= garter st). 
Cast on 28-32-36-40-42 sts on needle size 9 mm with lime. K 3-4-5-6-6 rows. Insert a marker in middle of piece. 
Work next row as follows: K until 1-1-1-2-2 sts remain before marker, make 1 YO, K 2-2-2-4-4 sts and make 1 more YO, K the rest of row. On next row K the YOs twisted to avoid holes (i.e. work in back loop of st) – continue the inc with 1 YO on each side of the middle 2-2-2-4-4 sts on every row until there are 50-56-62-68-74 sts on needle. 
Switch to olive. Work 4 rows without inc. Then dec 1 st at the beg of the next 4-4-4-6-6 rows (i.e. dec a total of 2-2-2-3-3 sts in each side) = 46-52-58-62-68 sts remain on needle. Cast off the remaining sts. 
Loosely sew tog edge to edge under foot and mid back (avoid a thick edge).    

EYES: 
Cast on 2 sts with olive on needle size 9 mm. 
Row 1: K 2 sts in each st = 4 sts. 
Row 2: P all sts.
Row 3: K 2 sts in every st = 8 sts.
Row 4-6: Work in stocking st without inc.
Row 7: K all sts tog 2 by 2 = 4 sts.
Row 8: P all sts.
Row 9: K all sts tog 2 by 2 = 2 sts.
Cut the thread approx. 20 cm, pull it through the remaining sts. A circle has now been worked, baste the thread around the circle and tighten tog. 
Work another eye and sew them on top of the slipper. 

FELTING:
Place the slippers in the washing machine with a detergent without enzymes and optical bleach. Wash at 40 degrees with normal spin but no pre-wash. After wash shape the slippers to the right size while still wet and leave to dry flat. At subsequent wash, wash the slippers at regular wool programme.
