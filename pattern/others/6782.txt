DROPS design: Pattern no pe-001-bn
Yarn group E or C + C
-----------------------------------------------------------
Size: Child - Adult
Head circumference: approx. 48/52 - 54/58 cm
Materials: 
DROPS PEAK from Garnstudio
100 g in both sizes in colour no 09, neon yellow
50 g in both sizes in colour no 04, electric blue
50 g in both sizes in colour no 07, neon green (if you want pompom)

Or use:
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100 g in both sizes in colour no 24, yellow
50 g in both sizes in colour no 15, dark blue
50 g in both sizes in colour no 25, green (if you want pompom)

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 8 mm - or size needed to get 11 sts x 15 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.SPIRAL PATTERN: 
* K 4, P 4 *, repeat from *-* in the round. Pattern is over 8 sts and no of sts are divisible by 8 + 7. When working in the round, the pattern will displace 1 st on every round and form a spiral pattern.
----------------------------------------------------------

HAT:
Worked in the round on double pointed needles.
Cast on 47-55 sts on double pointed needles size 8 mm with electric blue / dark blue. Switch to neon yellow / yellow and work SPIRAL PATTERN - see explanation above. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures 13-18 cm, K 1 round AT THE SAME TIME insert 5-6 markers as follows:
Size child: 9-10-9-10-9 sts between every marker.
Size adult: 9-9-9-9-9-10 sts between every marker.
Continue in stocking st AT THE SAME TIME on next round dec 1 st on right side of every marker by K 2 tog, repeat every other round 4-5 more times = 22-19 sts. On next round K sts tog 2 by 2 = 11-10 sts on needle. Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten.

POMPOM:
Make 1 pompom of approx. 15 cm in diameter with neon green / green. Sew the pompom on the top of the hat. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
