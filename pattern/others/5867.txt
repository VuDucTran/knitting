Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comINCREASE TIP 1 (applies to neck warmer): 
Inc 1 P st in every P-section as follows: * P 1, 1 YO, K 2 *, repeat from *-* the entire round. On next round P the YO twisted.

INCREASE TIP 2 (applies to head band): 
Inc from RS 1 P st as follows: P 2, K 1, 1 YO, P 0-2-4, K 1, P 2. On next row K YO twisted (i.e. work in back loop of YO instead of front) to avoid holes. 
On next inc, inc directly before K 1 and P 2 at the end of row so that inc are done alternately on the right and left side of the middle P sts.

DECREASE TIP (applies to head band): 
Dec from RS 1 P st in the middle P-section on head band as follows: P 2, K 1, P the next 2 tog, work the rest of row. 
On next dec, dec directly before K 1 and P 2 at the end of row so that dec are done alternately on the right and left side of the middle P sts.
--------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER:
Worked in the round, top down. Loosely cast on 33-36-39-42 sts on double pointed needles size 8 mm with Eskimo (switch to circular needle size 8 mm when needed). Work rib: * P 1, K 2 *, repeat from *-* the entire round = 11-12-13-14 P-sections. Work K over K and P over P until piece measures 7-8-9-9 cm. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION.
Then inc for shoulder as follows: Inc 1 st in every P-section - READ INCREASE TIP 1 - the entire round. Repeat inc in every P-section directly before 2 sts K every 3rd-3rd-4th-4th round until piece measures approx. 16-18-20-21 cm. K 1 round and P 1 round over all sts, loosely cast off. Piece measures approx. 17-19-21-22 cm. 
--------------------------------------------------------

HEAD BAND:
Worked back and forth on needle. Loosely cast on 6-8-10 sts on double pointed needles size 8 mm with Eskimo. Work 1st row (= from RS) as follows: P 2, K 1, P 0-2-4, K 1, P 2. Work K over K and P over P for 1-1-3 more rows = 2-2-4 rows in total. On next row from RS inc 1 st – SEE INCREASE TIP. Repeat inc every other row a total of 4 times, then every 4th row a total of 2 times = 12-14-16 sts (6-8-10 P sts between 2 P + 1 K in each side of piece).
Work K over K and P over P until piece measures approx. 32-34-35 cm. Then dec 1 st in the middle P-section - READ DECREASE TIP. Repeat dec 1 more time after 3 rows (= 2 dec every 4th row). Then continue dec in the middle P-section every other row a total of 4 times = 6-8-10 sts remain. Work K over K and P over P for 2-2-4 more rows, piece measures approx. 44-46-48 cm, loosely cast off all sts. Sew the short sides tog.
