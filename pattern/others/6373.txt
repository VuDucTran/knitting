DROPS design: Pattern no as-001-bn
Yarn group C and C
-----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/12 years
Head circumference: 50/52 – 52/54 – 54/58 cm
Materials: 
DROPS BRUSHED ALPACA SILK from Garnstudio
25-25-25 g colour no 08, heather

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES OR DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES SIZE 6 mm - or size needed to get 13 sts x 17 rows in stocking st with 2 strands = 10 x 10 cm.

 You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 2.35£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comENGLISH RIB (back and forth on needle):
ROW 1: 1 st in garter st, * K 2 tog, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P *, repeat from *-* until 3 sts remain, finish with K 2 tog and 1 st in garter st (= 13 sts).
ROW 2: 1 st in garter st, * 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P, K tog YO and slipped st *, repeat from *-* until 2 sts remain, finish with 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P, 1 st in garter st.
ROW 3: 1 st in garter st, * K tog YO and slipped st, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P *, repeat from *-* until 3 sts remain, K tog YO and slipped st, 1 st in garter st.
Repeat 2nd and 3rd row upwards.
----------------------------------------------------------

HEAD BAND:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 19 sts on needle size 6 mm with 2 strands Brushed Alpaca Silk. Work ENGLISH RIB - see explanation above. When piece measures approx. 40-42-44 cm (or desired length) adjust after 3rd row. Then work next row as follows: 1 st in garter st, * P 1, K tog YO and slipped st *, repeat from *-* until 2 sts remain, finish with P 1 and 1 st in garter st. Loosely cast off with K over K and P over P.
