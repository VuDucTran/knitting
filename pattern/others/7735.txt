LEG WARMERS: 
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 64 sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with Baby Merino. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4 cm. Switch to double pointed needles size 3 mm. Work 1 ridge in GARTER ST - see explanation above - on first round dec 7 sts evenly = 57 sts. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION
Then work according to A.1 (= 3 repetitions in width). When piece measures approx. 20 cm, work 1 ridge while inc 7 sts evenly = 64 sts. Switch to double pointed needles size 2,5 mm, continue with rib = K 2/P 2 for 4 cm, cast off with K over K and P over P.
