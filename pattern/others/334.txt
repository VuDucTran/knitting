HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: Karisma Superwash
100-100-100 g colour no. 55, light brown

DROPS double pointed needles size 3 mm

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: 23 sts x 32 rows on needles size 3 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 104-112-120 sts with Karisma on double pointed needles size 3 mm. Knit Rib over all sts until the hat measures 24-26-28 cm. Now knit all purl sts tog 2 by 2, and on the next round knit all knitted sts tog 2 by 2, and then all sts 2 by 2 = 26-28-30 sts. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts, pull tight and sew. Fold the bottom 6 cm towards the right side.

The hat measures approx. 19-21-23 cm in the height.
