Yarn group E
--------------------------------------------------------
Size: 3/5 - 6/9 - 10/12 years
Head circumference: 50/52 – 52/54 – 54/58 cm 

Materials: 
DROPS ANDES from Garnstudio
100-100-100 g colour no 5310, light brown
And use:
DROPS ALPACA BOUCLÉ from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no 2020, light beige 

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES AND CIRCULAR NEEDLES (40 CM) SIZE 8 mm - or size needed to get 11 sts x 15 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CROCHET HOOK SIZE 7 mm - for flap and crochet edge

Work 1 dc but wait with last pull through (= 2 sts on hook), then work next dc but on last pull through, pull yarn through all sts on hook = 1 dc dec.
--------------------------------------------------------

EAR FLAP:
Cast on 4-4-5 sts on double pointed needles size 8 mm with light brown Andes. Work in stocking st back and forth with 1 edge st K in each side (1st row = WS). 
On next row from RS inc 1 st in each side by making 1 YO inside 1 edge st, on next row P YO twisted to avoid hole. Inc like this every row from RS 3 more times = 12-12-13 sts in total on row. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! 
Continue in stocking st until flap measures 6-7-8 cm. Put piece aside and knit another ear flap.

HAT:
Work one ear flap on to circular needle size 8 mm, cast on 8-10-11 new sts (= back) and work the other ear flap on to same needle = 32-34-37 sts turn. P next row from WS, turn. Continue in stocking st back and forth and 1 edge st K in each side - AT THE SAME TIME inc in each side the same way as on ear flap: 1 st 2 times = 36-38-41 sts. At the end of next row from RS cast on 12-14-15 sts at the front between ear flaps = 48-52-56 sts. NOW MEASURE PIECE FROM HERE!  
Join and work hat in stocking st in the round until piece measures 10-12-13 cm. 
Now insert 6 markers in the piece as follows: 
3/5 years: 8 sts between each. 
6/9 years: 9-8-9-9-8-9 sts between each.
10/12 years: 10-9-9-10-9-9 sts between each. 
Then dec 1 st by K 2 tog after every marker every other round 4-4-5 times in total and then every round 3 more times = 6-10-8 sts remain on needle.
Cut the yarn and pull it through the remaining sts, tighten tog and fasten. Hat measures approx. 17-19-22 cm from mid front and up to top.

CROCHET EDGE: 
Work with 3 strands Alpaca Bouclé on hook size 7 mm. Beg before left ear flap at opening mid front as follows: 1 dc, * 2 ch, skip approx. 1 cm, 1 dc *, repeat from *-* around one ear flap, along edge at the back of neck, around the last ear flap. Then work back and forth along opening mid front to make a flap as follows:
ROW 1 (RS): Work dc in each of the 12-14-15 sts at the front, 1 sl st in first dc from round around the hat. Turn piece and work 1 ch.
ROW 2 (WS): Work 1 dc in every dc, turn piece and work 1 ch.
ROW 3: Work 1 dc in every dc BUT work the first 2 and the last 2 dc tog - read DECREASE TIP above. Turn piece and work 1 ch.
ROW 4-7: Repeat ROWS 2-3 Fasten off.


ASSEMBLY:
