Yarn group E or C + C

Size: 20/21 - 22/23 - 24/25 - 26/28 – 29/31 – 32/34
Foot length: 12 - 13 - 15 - 17 - 18 - 20 cm 
Materials: 
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-100-150-150-150-200 g colour no 54, medium purple

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40, 60 or 80 cm) SIZE 5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 33 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.

PICOT EDGE (back and forth on needle):
ROW 1 (= RS): K all sts.
ROW 2 (= WS): K all sts. 
ROW 3 (= RS): K until 4 sts remain, work these sts as follows: K 2 tog, 1 YO, K 2 sts in each of the last 2 sts = 2 new sts on row.
ROW 4 (= WS): Slip 1st st as if to K, K 1, psso, K 1 and slip the last st on right needle over the foremost st, 2 sts dec - then K the remaining sts. Repeat rows 1 to 4.
----------------------------------------------------------

SLIPPER:
Worked back and forth on needle.

FOLDING EDGE:
Cast on 14-14-16-16-18-18 sts on circular needle size 5 mm with Eskimo. Insert 1 marker inside the last 4 sts on row in left side of piece (seen from RS). Work in GARTER ST - see explanation above, AT THE SAME TIME work PICOT EDGE over the last 4 sts - see explanation above. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION Continue until 28-32-34-38-42-44 ridges have been worked in total (i.e. 56-64-68-76-84-88 rows, piece measures approx. 17-19-21-23-25-27) cm, cast off.

FOOT:
Pick up 1 st in every ridge along the long side without picot edge = 28-32-34-38-42-44 sts. Work in garter st for 2 cm, last row = from WS. 
Work the first 8-10-11-12-14-15 sts in garter st and slip them on a stitch holder, inc 1 st, work the next 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts in garter st, inc 1 st, work the last 8-10-11-12-14-15 sts and slip them on a stitch holder. Continue in garter st over the remaining 14-14-14-16-16-16 sts. When middle piece measures 4-4-5-6-6-7 cm, dec 1 edge st in each side from WS = 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts, slip sts on 1 stitch holder. Cut the yarn. Work next row as follows (= RS): Work sts from stitch holders AT THE SAME TIME pick up 7-7-9-10-10-12 sts on each side of middle piece inside 1 edge st = 42-46-52-58-62-68 sts. NOW MEASURE PIECE FROM HERE! Continue back and forth in garter st. Insert 1 marker mid front on toe. When piece measures 2 cm, K 2 sts tog on each side of marker and K 2 tog inside 1 edge st in each side, repeat dec every other row (= 4 sts dec in total every other row). Continue dec until piece measures 3-3-4-4-5-5 cm, cast off. Sew seams under foot in outer loops of edge sts to avoid a chunky seam.
