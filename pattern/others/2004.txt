Size: 3/5 - 6/9 - 10/14 år

Materials: DROPS Ull-Flamé or Eskimo 
100-100-100 gr colour no 15, grey/beige

DROPS pointed and double pointed needles size 8mmHAT:

Knitting Tension: 
11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm. 
11 sts x 23 rows on needle size 8 mm in garter sts = 10 x 10 cm.

Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.
Garter sts (in the round on circular needles): K 1st row, P 2nd row.

Back piece including ear-flaps: Cast on 31-35-39 sts on needles size 8 mm with 2 threads of grey beige. Remove one of the threads and continue to knit as explained below:
Please read all of the next section before continuing:
K 1 row over all sts,* K 1 row over the first 3-4-5 sts, turn and K backwards, K 1 row over all sts, turn and K 1 row over the first 3-4-5 sts (opposite side), turn and K backwards, K 1 row over all sts*, repeat from *-* but K 1 st less each side  for the earflap each time (i.e. at the next row K over the first 2-3-4 sts, and so on) until 0 sts remaining for the flap. Knit 12-16-20 rows of garter sts each outer side of the ear flaps.

Hat: cast on 14-15-16 sts in between the earflaps at mid front = 45-50-55 sts. Distribute all sts on 4 double pointed needles and continue to knit the hat round on needles. Knit 6 rounds of garter sts and continue in stocking sts until finished measurements. Remember the knitting tension! At the same time, when the hat measures 9-10-11 cm from mid front, insert 5 marking threads with 9-10-11 sts between each thread. Continue to bind off as follows: K2 tog after each marking thread on every other round 6-6-7 times and then on every round 2-3-3 times = 5 sts left. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts, sew the thread. The hat measures approx. 17-19-21 cm from mid front and to the top.
