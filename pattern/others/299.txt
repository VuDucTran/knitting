HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: Camelia (discontinued , use Alpaca instead)
50-50-50 g colour no 0100 off-white
50-50-50 g colour no 6205, light blur 
Drops double pointed needles size 4mm and 5mm

SCARF: see pattern no 12-01		
HAT:
Knitting Tension: 17 sts x 22 rows with 2 threads of Alpaca on needles size 5 mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 80-84-88 sts with 1 thread of each colour (double thread) on double pointed needles size 4mm. Knit 7-8-9 cm Rib. Change to double pointed needles size 5mm and continue in stocking sts, at the same time dec 10-9-8 sts evenly on first round = 70-75-80 sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 9-11-12 cm dec 4-5-6 sts evenly on round, repeat the dec on every 4th round another 2 times = 58-60-62 sts. When hat measures 13-15-16 cm change to double pointed needles size 4mm and knit 2 cm Rib. Cast off in Rib.
