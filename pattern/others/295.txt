HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years

Materials: DROPS Alpaca
50-50-50 g colour no 3770, heather 
and use: DROPS Eskimo or Ull-flame 
100-100-100 g colour no 01, off-white

DROPS pointed needles size 10mm 

SCARF:
Measurements: 13x95 cm – 16 x115 cm – 19x135 cm

Materials: Puddel
100-100-100 g colour no 01, off-white
DROPS pointed needles size 12mm

Knitting tension: 8 sts x 21 rows with 1 strand of Ull-flame and 1 strand of Alpaca on needles size 10mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K3, P3*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 36-36-42 sts on needle size 10mm with 1 strand of Eskimo/Ull-flame and 1 strand Alpaca(= double thread) and knit 4 cm Rib. Continue in stocking sts, at the same time dec 4-0-2 sts on the first row = 32-36-40 sts. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 10-12-14 cm dec 4-4-5 sts evenly on row. Repeat this dec when piece measures 14-16-18 cm and again at 18-20-22 cm. Cast off when piece measures 21-23-25 cm. Sew together mid back in the outer loop of the outermost st in order the seam doesn’t become too heavy. Put hat flat on table with seam in the middle and sew together at the top. 

SCARF:
Knitting tension: 7 sts = width 10 cm on needles size 12mm in garter sts. 
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): knit all rows.

Loosely cast on 9-11-13 sts on needle size 12mm with 2 strands of Puddel, remove 1 strand and knit garter sts. Cast off loosely with 2 strands when piece measures 95-11-135 cm.
