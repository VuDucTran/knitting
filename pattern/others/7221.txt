DROPS design: Pattern no pe-008-bn
Yarn group E or C + C
----------------------------------------------------------
Size: 20/21 - 22/23 - 24/25 - 26/28 – 29/31 – 32/34
Foot length: 12 - 13 - 15 - 17 - 18 - 20 cm  

Materials: 
DROPS PEAK from Garnstudio
100-100-150-150-200-200 g colour no 08, neon pink 
or:   DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-100-150-150-200-200 g colour no 26, hot pink 

DROPS DOUBLE POINTED NEEDLES AND STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 22 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CEDAR BUTTON, NO 511: 4 pieces for all sizesGARTER ST (in the round on double pointed needles):
* K 1 round and P 1 round *, repeat from *-*. 1 ridge = 2 rounds. 

GARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K. 

PATTERN: 
See diagram A.1. The diagram shows all rows in pattern seen from RS. 
--------------------------------------------------------

SLIPPER:

LEG: 
Cast on 28-31-34-37-41-44 sts on double pointed needles size 5 mm with Peak or Eskimo. Work in GARTER ST in the round - see explanation above - until piece measures approx. 4 cm, finish after 1 round P. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION 

FOOT:
Now keep the first 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts on the round on the needle, slip the remaining 16-19-22-23-27-30 sts on 1 stitch holder. Continue in garter st back and forth over the 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts - AT THE SAME TIME on 1st row cast on 1 new st in each side (edge st) = 14-14-14-16-16-16 sts. When middle piece measures 4-4-5-6-6-7 cm, dec 1 edge st in each side = 12-12-12-14-14-14 sts. Then slip the sts from stitch holder back on needle and pick up 7-7-9-10-10-12 sts on each side of middle part (inside 1 edge st) = 42-45-52-57-61-68 sts on needle. NOW MEASURE PIECE FROM HERE. Insert 1 marker mid front of toe and 1 marker mid back on heel. Continue in the round in garter st. When piece measures 2 cm, K 2 sts tog on each side of every marker on every other round (= 4 sts less per round) continue dec until piece measures 3-3-4-4-5-5 cm - cast off. Sew seams under foot in outer loops of edge sts to avoid a chunky seam.


LOOSE LEG WORKED ACROSS: 
Cast on 13 sts on needle size 5 mm with Peak or Eskimo. Work 2 ridges in GARTER ST back and forth - see explanation above - on last row inc 4 sts evenly = 17 sts. Work according to A.1 (= 17 sts) until piece measures approx. 18-20-22-24-26-28 cm, on last row dec 2 sts over every cable in A.1 = 13 sts. Work 2 ridges and cast off. Place cast-on edge over cast-off edge and sew on 2 buttons for decoration through the leg. Pull leg 2 cm down on foot with buttons on the outside of slipper and sew leg to cast-on edge on slipper from WS with invisible stitches.
