Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*. 

Cast on 38-42-46 sts on needles size 8 mm with Eskimo. Knit 7-8-9 cm Rib and cast off in Rib. 


MITTENS:
Knitting Tension: 11 sts x 15 rows on needles size 8mm in stocking sts. = 10 x 10 cm
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*. 
Decreasing tips: Dec as follows before the marking thread: K2 tog. Dec as follows after the marking thread: Slip 1 st as if to knit, K1, psso.

Cast on 16-18-22 sts with Eskimo on double pointed needles size 8mm. Knit 5-6-7 cm Rib and continue in stocking sts. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 7-8-10 cm knit next round as follows: Insert a marking thread (= side), knit 1 st, put the next 2-3-3 sts on thread for thumb, knit 5-5-7 sts, insert a marking thread (= side), knit the remaining 8-9-11 sts. On next row cast on 2-3-3 new sts over the thumb sts. Continue in stocking sts. When piece measures 5-7-8 cm from the thumb opening dec on each side of both marking threads – see Decreasing tips above – on every other row a total of 3-3-4 times = 4-6-6 sts left. Cut the thread and pull through remaining sts, sew tight. The mitten measures approx 16-19-23 cm.
Thumb: Put the 2-3-3 sts from thread back on needle size 8mm, pick up 2-3-3 sts behind the opening and 1 st on each side = 6-8-8 sts. Knit stocking sts round on needles. When thumb measures 3.5-4.5-5.5 cm, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts, sew tight.

Knit another mitten but with the thumb the opposite side.
