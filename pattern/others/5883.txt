GARTER ST (back and forth on needle): 
1 ridge = 2 rows K.
-------------------------------------------------------

HAT:  
Worked in the round on circular needle and then on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 104-116 sts on circular needle size 3 mm with medium purple. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 3 cm, rounds beg mid back. Switch to circular needle size 3.5 mm and continue diagram A-1 – AT THE SAME TIME on 1st round dec 8 sts evenly = 96-108 sts. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION!  When A-1 has been worked 1 time vertically, continue with medium purple until finished measurements. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 14-16 cm, insert 8-9 markers in the piece, 12 sts apart. On next round, dec 1 st after every marker by K 2 tog. Repeat dec every other round 6 more times and then every round 4 times = 8-9 sts remain on needle. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts and tighten tog. The hat measures approx. 20-22 cm vertically.

EAR FLAPS:
Worked back and forth on 2 double pointed needles.
Count 12 sts from mid back and out to one side - insert a marker. Knit up with medium purple on needle size 3.5 mm: 1 st in each of the next 22-26 sts along cast on edge. 
Work in GARTER ST back and forth - see explanation above - there should be 4 rows in garter st in medium purple and 4 rows in cerise alternately.
When ear flap measures approx. 30-33 cm, finish with medium purple – AT THE SAME TIME dec 1 st in each side by K the 2 next to last sts tog - repeat dec every other row 4-5 more times and then every row 4-5 times = 4 sts remain. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts and tighten tog.  

Knit another ear flap on the other side of hat - make sure they are the same length. There should be 24-24 sts between ear flaps at the back and 36-40 sts between ear flaps at the front.

POMPOM: 
Make 3 dense, nice pompoms with Symphony or Melody with an approx. diameter of 5 cm. Fasten 1 at the bottom of each ear flap and 1 at the top of hat.
-------------------------------------------------------

MITTEN: 
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 32-36-40 sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with medium purple. Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4-5-6 cm. Switch to double pointed needles size 3 mm and work 1 round in stocking st while at the same time inc 4-6-8 sts evenly = 36-42-48 sts. Continue with diagram A-1. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION!
When 3 rounds in A-1 have been worked, beg inc for thumb gusset – inc on each side of second st on round – inc by making 1 YO, on next round K YOs twisted to avoid holes. Repeat inc every 4th round 2-3-3 more times (inc on each side of all thumb sts, i.e. there are 2 sts more between YOs every time) – thumb sts are worked in pattern when possible – NOTE: Make sure that the pattern is not displaced on the rest of mitten. After all inc are done, there are 7-9-9 thumb sts and 42-50-56 sts in total on needle. 
When piece measures a total of 10-12-14 cm, slip the 7-9-9 thumb sts + 1 st on each side of these on a stitch holder. Cast on 3 new sts behind sts on stitch holder = 36-42-48 sts on needle. Continue pattern up to arrow in diagram for your size, then finish the piece with medium purple. 
AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 15-17-19 cm, insert 6 markers in the piece, 6-7-8 sts apart. On next round, dec 1 st after every marker by K 2 tog. 
Repeat dec every other round 2 more times and then every round 2-3-4 times = 6 sts remain on needle. Cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts and tighten tog. 
Mitten measures approx. 17-19-22 cm.

THUMB: 
Slip the 9-11-11 sts from stitch holder over thumb gusset back on double pointed needles size 3 mm, knit in addition up 5 sts with medium purple in the edge behind the thumb sts = 14-16-16 sts. Work in stocking st in the round with medium purple for 3½-4½-5½ cm. On next round, K all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread, pull it through the remaining sts, tighten and fasten.

Knit another mitten.
