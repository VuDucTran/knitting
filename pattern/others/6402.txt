DROPS design: Pattern no ee-485
Yarn group E
-----------------------------------------------------------
NECK WARMER:
Size: 3/6 - 7/10 - 11/12 years
Materials: 
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-150-150 g colour no 56, Christmas red
and use:
DROPS PUDDEL from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no 01, off white

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 9 mm - or size needed to get 10 sts in moss st with 1 strand Eskimo = width 10 cm.
DROPS CROCHET HOOK SIZE 6 mm - for edge

HEAD BAND:
Size: 3/6 - 7/10 - 11/12 years
Head circumference: 50-52-54 cm
Materials: 
DROPS ESKIMO from Garnstudio
100-100-100 g colour no 56, Christmas red
and use:
DROPS PUDDEL from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no 01, off white

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 8 mm - or size needed to get 9 sts in moss st with 2 strands Eskimo = width 10 cm.
DROPS CROCHET HOOK SIZE 6 mm - for edge.

You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comMOSS ST: 
Row 1: * K 1, P 1 *, repeat from *-*. 
Row 2: K over P and P over K. Repeat 2nd row.

----------------------------------------------------------

NECK WARMER:
Worked back and forth on needle. 
Cast on 20-25-25 sts on needle size 9 mm with Eskimo. Work in moss st - see explanation above. Continue like this until piece measures approx. 70-75-80 cm, cast off.

CROCHET EDGE:
Work with 2 strands Puddel on hook size 6 mm along all sides on neck warmer as follows: 1 dc in first st, * 2 ch, skip approx. 2 cm, 1 dc *, repeat from *-*, finish with 1 sl st in first dc.

ASSEMBLY:
Sew cast-off edge, edge to edge with the end on one long side to avoid a chunky seam (opposite long side now forms a tip towards mid front).

----------------------------------------------------------

HEAD BAND: 
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 7-9-10 sts on needle size 8 mm with double yarn in Eskimo. Work in moss st - see explanation above. Work until piece measures approx. 48-50-52 cm or desired measurements. Loosely cast off all sts. Sew cast-on edge and cast-off edge in outer loops of edge sts.

CROCHET EDGE:
Work with 2 strands Puddel on hook size 6 mm along both sides on head band as follows: 1 dc in first st, * 2 ch, skip approx. 2 cm, 1 dc in next st *, repeat from *-*, finish with 1 sl st in first dc.
