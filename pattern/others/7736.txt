DROPS design: Pattern no z-042-bn
Yarn group A + A or C

Size: 3/5 - 6/8 - 9/12 years
Length: 22-24-26 cm
Materials: 
DROPS ALPACA from Garnstudio
50 g for all sizes in 3112, powder pink
DROPS KID-SILK from Garnstudio
25 g for all sizes in colour no 03, light pink

DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLE SIZE 5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 34 rows in garter st with 1 strand of each quality (= 2 strands) = 10 x 10 cm. 
DROPS STRAIGHT NEEDLES SIZE 4.5 mm – for rib You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 6.70£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K. 
-------------------------------------------------------

LEG WARMER: 
Worked back and forth on needle. 
Cast on 36-40-44 sts on needle size 4.5 mm with 1 strand Alpaca and 1 strand Kid-Silk (= 2 strands). Work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4 cm. Switch to needle size 5 mm. Work in GARTER ST – see explanation above – until piece measures 18-20-22 cm. Switch to needle size 4.5 mm and work rib = K 2/P 2 for 4 cm. Cast off with K over K and P over P. 

ASSEMBLY:
Sew tog leg warmer in outer loops of edge sts.
