Knitting tension: 17 sts x 22 rows on needles size 5 mm and stocking sts  = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Front and back piece: Cast on 120–132–144 (156–168–180) sts with Alaska on circular needles size 4 mm. Knit 4-4-4(5-5-5) cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 5mm and continue as follows: K 5-6-6 (7-7-8) sts , * P 10-11-12 (13-14-15) sts, K 10-11-12 (13-14-15) sts* , repeat from *-* a total of 5 times, finish with P 10-11-12 (13-14-15) sts and K 5-6-6 (7-7-8) sts. Insert a marking thread in the middle of the knitted part each side. Remember the knitting tension! When the piece measures 25-27-29 (31-32-33) cm bind off 4-5-6 (7-6-7) sts each side (in the middle of the knitted part) for armholes = 56-61-66 (71-78-83) sts for each front and back piece. Now finish each part separately.
Front piece: = 56-61-66 (71-78-83) sts. Bind off for armholes on every other row as follows: 2 sts 0-1-1 (2-2-3) times and 1 st 3-2-3 (3-5-4) times = 50-53-56 (57-60-63) sts.  When the piece measures 34-37-40 (43-44-46) cm bind off the middle 8-9-10 (11-12-13) sts for neck. Continue to bind off each neck side on every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times = 14-15-16 (16-17-18) sts left each for each shoulder. Bind off when piece measures 40-43-46 (49-51-53) cm.
Back piece: = 56-61-66 (71-78-83) sts. Bind off for armholes as done for the front piece = 50-53-56 (57-60-63) sts. When the piece measures 38-41-44 (47-49-51) cm bind off the middle 20-21-22 (23-24-25) sts for neck. Continue to bind off each neck side 1 st and bind off remaining sts when piece measures 40-43-46 (49-51-53) cm.
Sleeve: Cast on 34-36-38 (38-40-42) sts with Alaska on double pointed needles size 4 mm. Knit 6-6-6 (7-7-7) cm rib. Change to double pointed needles size 5mm and continue in stocking sts. At the same time when the piece measures 8 cm, inc 2 sts centred under the sleeve on every 3rd cm (approx every 6th and 7th round alternately) a total of 8-9-10 (11-12-13) times = 50-54-58 (60-64-48) sts. When the piece measures 31-34-37 (41-44-47) cm bind off 4 sts under the sleeve and continue to knit forwards and backwards. Bind off 3 sts each side on every other row a total of 2-2-1 (0-0-0) and then 2 sts each side until piece measures 35-39-43 (48-52-56) cm. Now binf off 3 sts each side 1 time and bind off remaining sts when the piece measures approx 36-40-44 (49-53-57) cm.
Assembly: Sew the shoulder seams
Neck: Pick up approx 60 – 80 sts around the neck (divisible by 2) with double pointed needles size 4mm in Alaska. Knit 4-4-5 (5-6-6) cm rib and bind off loosely in rib. Sew in the sleeves.

HAT:

Knitting tension: 11 sts x 17 rows on needles size 7 mm with Highlander and stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 104-114-124 sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with Karisma. Knit 8 cm rib and change to turquoise blue Highlander and double pointed needles size 7 mm. Continue in stocking sts – at the same time on the 1st round knit all sts tog 2 by 2 = 52-57-62 sts. On the next round bind off 2 sts evenly distributed = 50-55-60 sts. Remember the knitting tension! When the hat measures 16-17-18 cm insert 5 marking threads with 10-11-12 sts between each thread. Continue to bind off as follows: K2 tog on the right side of each marking thread on every 4th round a total of 3-3-3 times and then on every other round 3-4-5 times = 20 sts left. Continue to knit the sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull it through the remaining sts and sew it tight. The hat measures approx 25-27-29 cm. Sew slanting sts at the bottom of the hat, each st should be approx 4 sts in the width and 5 rounds in the height.  

SCARF:

Knitting tension: 10 sts x 16 rows on needles size 8 mm with Highlander in stocking sts  = 10 x 10 
cm.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): K all rows.

Cast on 14-17-20 sts on pointed needles size 8 mm with Highlander. Knit 4 rows of garter sts, and continue in stocking sts with 2 sts of garter sts each side. Remember the knitting tension! When the piece measures 118-138-158 cm, knit 4 rows of garter sts over all sts and bind off loosely.
