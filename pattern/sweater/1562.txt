Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 20 sts x 26 rows on larger needle in pattern = 10 x 10 cm .

Pattern: See chart (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is shown from the right side.

Rib: * K 2, P 2 *

Body: Cast on 136-144-156 (164-176) sts on smaller circular needles and knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger circular needles and knit pattern, increasing 24-24-28 (28-32) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 160-168-184 (192-208) sts. When the work measures 26-28-31 (32-33) cm  divide it for fronts and back, 40-42-46 (48-52) for the front, 80-84-92 (96-104) sts for the back, 40-42-46 (48-52) for the front. Knit the rest of each part separately. 

Front: = 40-42-46 (48-52) sts. When the work measures 40-43-48 (50-52) cm , bind off for the neck every other row: 14-14-15 (15-16) sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Bind off all sts when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57) cm .

Back: = 80-84-92 (96-104) sts. When the work measures 43-46-51 (53-55) cm , bind off the center 34-34-36 (36-38) sts for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off all sts when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 32-32-32 (36-36) sts on smaller double pointed needles; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger double pointed needles, increasing 8-8-12 (12-12) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 40-40-44 (48-48) sts, and knit pattern (see that you get a repeat directly centered on the sleeve). After the rib, inc 1 st each side of marker 18-20-22 (22-24) times:
Size 3/4: every other row
Sizes 5/6 + 7/8: alternately every 2nd and 3rd row
Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: every 3rd row 
= 76-80-88 (92-96) sts. When the work measures 21-25-28 (32-35) cm , bind off 3 sts each side of marker and knit the rest back and forth on needles. Then bind off on each side every other row: 5 sts 3 times, bind off all sts. The work measures approx. 24-28-31 (35-38) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 96-100-110 (120-125) sts along the left front on smaller circular needles and knit rib back and forth for 2.5 cm . Repeat along the right front, but after 1 cm  make 5-5-5 (6-6) buttonholes evenly distributed on the buttonband - 1 buttonhole = bind off 2 sts and increase 2 new sts on the next row. Pick up approx. 80-96 sts around the neck on smaller circular needles and knit 2.5 cm  rib, bind off all sts. Sew on the sleeves. Sew on buttons.
