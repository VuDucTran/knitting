Garter st back and forth: Knit all rows.

Back piece: Cast on 48-52-56-56-60 sts (includes 1 edge sts on each side) on needle size 6 mm with 1 thread of each quality yarn (= 2 threads). 
Knit 1 row garter st from WS. 
Knit next row as follows from RS: 1 edge st, * K2, P2 * repeat from *-* and finish off with 2 garter sts and 1 edge st. 
Repeat this until piece measures 2 cm. Continue in stocking sts. 
When piece measures 5-6-7-8-9 cm cast off for armhole on each side on every other row as follows: 3 sts 1 time, 1 st 1-2-3-2-3 times = 40-42-44-46-48 sts. When piece measures 17-19-21-23-25 cm cast off middle 16-18-18-18-20 sts for neck. Continuing to cast off 1 st on next row at neckline = -11-11-12-13-13 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 19-21-23-25-27 cm. 

Right front piece: Cast on 25-25-29-29-29 sts (incl 1 edge sts on each side) on needle size 6 mm with 1 thread of each quality yarn (= 2 threads). Knit 1 row garter sts from WS. 
Knit next row as follows from RS: * K2, P2 * repeat from *-* and finish off with 1 edge st until piece measures 2 cm. 
Continuing to knit stocking sts. When piece measures 3-4-5-6-7 cm cast off for v-neck mid front as follows: 1 sts on every 2nd cm.  5-6-6-6-9 times in total and 1 st on every cm 5-3-5-5-1 times in total at the same time when piece measures  5-6-7-8-9 cm cast off for armhole like back piece  = 11-11-12-13-13 sts. 
Cast off when piece measures 19-21-23-25-27 cm. 

Left front piece: Knit like right piece but reversed. 

Sleeve: Cast on 26-26-30-30-30 sts (incl 1 edge sts on each side) on needle size 6 mm with 1 thread of each quality yarn (= 2 threads). Knit 1 rows stocking sts. Continue in rib * K2, P2 * repeat from  *-* with 1 edge st on each side until piece measures 3 cm. Continue in stocking sts at the same time inc 1 sts on each side on every 7-6-7-7-6 cm 5-6-6-6-7 times in total = 36-38-42-42-44 sts. When piece measures  33-36-40-42-44 cm cast off to shape sleeve cap on each side on every other row as follows: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1-2-3-4-5 times. Continuing cast off 2 sts on each side until piece measures 37-41-46-49-52 cm, then cast off 3 sts 1 time on each side. Cast off when piece measures approx 38-42-47-50-53 cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up 74-98 (dividable by 6+8) sts on needle size 6 mm along one front piece, round neckline and down along other front piece. Knit 1 row garter sts from WS. Knit next row as follows from RS: 2 st garter st, * K4, P2 * repeat from *-* and finish off  K4  and 1 sts garter st. Knit until edge measures 3 cm. Slip first 8 sts on 1 thread or stitch holder. Cast off remaining sts until 8 sts are left on row. 8 sts are left at the bottom of each front piece that are to be knitted for ties from RS as follows: 2 sts garter st, 4 sts stocking st and 2 sts garter st. Cast off when ties measures approx 14 cm. Sew in sleeves. Sew sleeve and side seams in one within 1 edge st.
