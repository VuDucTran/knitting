Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 20 sts and 26 rows in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm 

Pattern: See chart. The Pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette stitch. 

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter st, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Body: Cast on 160-172-184 (196-208-220) sts on circular needles with blue. Join, being careful not to twist the sts. Place a marker at the join and knit 6 rows Garter Stitch. Then knit in Pattern. When the work measures 29-31-34 (35-36-38) cm , knit the next row as follows: Bind off 2 sts for armhole, 76-82-88 (94-100-106) sts for the front, bind off 4 sts for armhole, 76-82-88 (94-100-106) sts for the back, bind off 2 sts for armhole. Knit the front and back separately from this point.

Front: 76-82-88 (94-100-106) sts. Bind off for armholes every other row: 2 sts 2-2-2 (3-3-3) times, 1 st 3-3-3 (3-3-4) times. 62-68-74 (76-82-86) sts. When the work measures 42-45-49 (51-53-56) cm , bind off the center 28-28-30 (30-32-32) sts for the neck. Then bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm . 

Back: 76-82-88 (94-100-106) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front. 62-68-74 (76-82-86) sts. When the work measures 43-46-51 (53-55-58) cm , bind off the center 34-34-36 (36-38-38) sts for the neck. Bind off 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm . 

Sleeves: Cast on 36-40-44 (44-46-46) sts on double pointed needles with blue. Join, being careful not to twist the sts. Place a marker at the join and knit 6 rows Garter Stitch. Then knit the pattern. At the same time, when the work measures 4 cm , inc 1 st each side of the marker 14-14-16 (18-19-21) times:
Size 3/4 years: alternately every 2rd and 3th row. 
Sizes 5/6 + 7/8 + 9/10 years: alternately every 3rd and 4th row. 
Sizes 11/12 + 13/14 years: every 4th row. 
= 64-68-76 (80-84-88) sts. When the work measures 22-25-29 (33-36-39) cm , bind off 2 sts each side of the marker and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off at each side for sleeve caps every other row: 3 sts 6 times. Bind off when the work measures 28-31-35 (39-42-45) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 96-100-100 (100-104-108) sts around the neck on circular needles with blue; join and knit 6 rows Garter Stitch. Then knit the work back and forth from the center front for the hood. Knit in stockinette stitch with 3 sts of Garter Stitch on each side at the center front. When the hood measures 30-31-31 (32-32-33) cm , knit 2 rows Garter Stitch and bind off. Sew the top of the hood together. 
If a hood is not desired knit as follows: Pick up approximately 78-90 sts with blue around the neck. Join and knit 6 rows Garter Stitch and bind off. 
Sew on sleeves.
