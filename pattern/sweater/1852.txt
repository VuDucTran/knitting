In case you find the chart confusing, here are the patterns written out:
Pattern 1 (8 rows): 4 rows stockinette st in Pelliza, 2 rows stockinette st in Angora-Tweed, then P 2 rows in Angora-Tweed.
Pattern 2 (8 rows in Angora-Tweed):
Repeat the following 4 rows twice:
Rows 1 and 3: Knit
Row 2: * P 3, K 1 *, repeat from * - *.
Row 4: P 1, K 1, * P 3, K 1 *, repeat from * - *.
Pattern 3 (8 rows in Angora-Tweed):
Rows 1-3: stockinette st
Row 4: * P 3, K 1 *, repeat from * - *.
Rows 5-7: stockinette st
Row 8: P 1, K 1, * P 3, K 1 *, repeat from * - *.
Pattern 4 (6 rows): P 2 rows in Alpaca, K 1 row in Pelliza, P 3 rows in Angora-Tweed

Seed Stitch (on an even number of sts):
Row 1: * K 1, P 1 *
Row 2: * P 1, K 1 *
Repeat Rows 1-2.

Back: Cast on 75-79-87-95 sts on smaller needles with 2 strands Pelliza. Knit Pattern 1 (knit the first 4 rows with 2 strands Pelliza, then change to larger needles and continue with 1 strand of Angora-Tweed). After Pattern 1 repeat Pattern 4 a total of 5-5-6-6 times (30-30-36-36 rows). Then knit Pattern 2 twice (16 rows), then repeat Pattern 3 to finished dimensions. At the same time when the piece measures 24-26-27-29 cm  bind off for armhole at each side every other row: 3 sts 1-1-1-1 time, 2 sts 1-1-2-3 times and 1 st 4-4-4-4 times = 57-61-65-69 sts. When the piece measures 37-40-43-46 cm bind off the center 25-25-27-27 sts for the neck. Then bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row = 14-16-17-19 sts remain on each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 39-42-45-48 cm.

Left front: Cast on 38-42-46-50 st on smaller needles with 2 strands Pelliza. Knit pattern as on back. When the piece measures 24-26-27-29 cm bind off for armhole at side as on back = 29-33-35-37 sts. When the piece measures 35-37-40-42 cm bind off 8-10-11-11 sts at front edge for neck. Then bind off for the neck every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times = 14-16-17-19 sts remain on each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 39-42-45-48 cm ].

Right front: Cast on and knit the same as the left reversing all shaping.

Sleeve: Cast on 40-42-44-46 sts on smaller needles with 2 strands Pelliza. nit Pattern 1 (knit the first 4 rows with 2 strands Pelliza, then change to larger needles and continue with 1 strand of Angora-Tweed). After Pattern 1 repeat Pattern 4 a total of 5-5-6-6 times (30-30-36-36 rows). Then knit Pattern 2 twice (16 rows), then repeat Pattern 3 to finished dimensions.
At the same time when the piece measures 7-7-8-8 cm inc 1 st at each side 10-13-16-17 times:
Sizes 3/4 and 5/6: alternately every 5th and 6th row Sizes 7/8 and 9/10: alternately every 4th and 5th row
= 60-68-76-80 sts - knit the increased sts into the pattern as you go along. When sleeves measures 30-34-36-39 cm [ bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 3 sts 2 times, then 2 sts at each side until the piece measures 36-40-43-47 cm. Bind off the remaining sts.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams.

Button band: Pick up approx. 76 to 92 sts along left button band on smaller needles with Angora-Tweed. Knit seed st for 3 cm , bind off. Repeat along right button band, but after 1 cm  make 6 buttonholes evenly distributed. 1 buttonhole = bind off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts on the next row. The bottom buttonhole should be 1 cm from bottom edge and the top buttonhole 0.5 cm from top edge.

Hood: Start to pick up sts around the neck approx. 6 cm in from right front edge and stop approx. 6 cm before left front edge. Pick up approx. 44 to 60 sts on smaller needles with Angora-Tweed. Knit Pattern 2 over all sts for 27-28-29-30 cm, then join top of hood together with Kitchener stitch. Pick up along front edges of hood approx. 100 to 120 sts on smaller needles with 2 strands Pelliza. Knit stockinette st for approx. 10-12 cm, bind off. Fold Pelliza edge double against wrong side, sew in place and sew edge of Pelliza band to neck edge. Sew in sleeves. Sew sleeve and side seams. Sew on buttons.
