Materials: 
Either Cotton Chenille or Tynn Chenille can be used. The garment in the photograph is knit in Cotton Chenille. All numbers for Tynn Chenille are given in ( ).

Garnstudio COTTON CHENILLE,
100% cotton, 50 g./70 m
300-350-400-400-450-500-550 g col. no. 20, gray variegated

DROPS 3.5 mm  Circular needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge.

or use: Garnstudio TYNN CHENILLE, 
90% cotton, 10% polyester, 50 g/120 m
300-350-400-400-450-500-550 g col. 928, dark gray

DROPS 2.5 mm  Circular needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge.

DROPS black Zipper: 30-40-40-40-40-40-50 cm 
(30 cm = no. 7106, 40 cm = no. 7107, 50 cm = no. 7108).
Gauge: 
Cotton Chenille: 15 sts x 27 rows in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm . 
(Tynn Chenille: 20 sts x 32 rows in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm .) 

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.

Body: Cast on 88-92-100-104-112-116-120 (116-124-132-140-148-156-160) sts and knit 2 rows garter st. Attach a marker at each side: fronts each = 22-23-25-26-28-29-30 (29-31-33-35-37-39-40) sts, back = 44-46-50-52-56-58-60 (58-62-66-70-74-78-80) sts. Knit stockinette st, keeping the 2 sts at each side (for the zipper-placket) in garter st throughout. After the garter st border, inc 1 st at each side of the markers 4-5-5-6-7-8-9 (6-6-7-8-10-10-12) times every 11-10-10-8-8-7-7 (10-10-9-8-7-7-6) rows = 104-112-120-128-140-148-156 (140-148-160-172-188-196-208) sts. When the work measures 23-24-24-24-26-27-29 cm  bind off 2 sts at each side of each marker, and knit the rest of each part separately.

Front: = 24-26-28-30-33-35-37 (33-35-38-41-45-47-50) sts. Bind off for armhole every other row: 3 sts 0 (1) time, 2 sts 1-1-1-1-2-3-3 (1-1-1-1-2-3-3) times, 1 st 2-2-2-3-3-3-4 (1-1-1-3-3-3-5) times = 20-22-24-25-26-26-27 (27-29-32-33-35-35-36) sts on the needles. When the work measures 31-34-36-38-40-42-44 cm  put sts on a st holder for the neck every other row: 6-7-7-7-7-8-8 (7-9-9-9-9-10-10) sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 (2) times, 1 st 2 (2) times. Bind off when the work measures 36-39-41-43-45-47-49 cm .

Back: = 48-52-56-60-66-70-74 (66-70-76-82-90-94-100) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front = 40-44-48-50-52-52-54 (54-58-64-66-70-70-72) sts. When the work measures 34-37-39-41-43-45-47 cm  put the center 18-20-20-20-20-22-22 (22-26-26-26-26-28-28) sts on a st holder for the neck. Then put sts at each neck edge on a st holder every other row: 1 (2) sts 1 time. Bind off when the work measures 36-39-41-43-45-47-49 cm .

Sleeve: Cast on 27-27-31-33-33-36-36 (36-36-40-44-44-48-48) sts and knit 2 rows garter st, then knit stockinette st. When the sleeve measures 4-4-4-4-5-5-5 cm , inc 1 st at each side 7-10-11-13-13-13-13 (9-13-15-17-17-17-17) times every 7-6-6-5-6-6-7 (7-5-5-5-5-6-6) rows = 41-47-53-59-59-62-62 (54-62-70-78-78-82-82) sts.
When the work measures 25-29-31-34-37-40-42 cm  bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 2 sts 7-7-9-10-11-12-14 (8-8-10-12-13-15-16) times, bind off. The work measures approx. 30-34-37-41-45-49-52 cm .

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seams. 

Hood: Put the sts from the st holder at the neck on circular needles and pick up sts evenly distributed for a total of approx. 50-54-54-56-56-60-60 (68-72-72-78-78-80-80) sts on the needles. Knit stockinette st keeping 2 sts at each edge in garter st. After 2 cm  inc 6 (8) sts evenly distributed on the next row = 56-60-60-62-62-66-66 (76-80-80-86-86-88-88) sts. Attach a marker at the center back. Knit stockinette st and inc 1 st at each side of the marker every 4th (6th) row. After 27-28-29-30-30-31-31 cm  put the last half of the sts on a second needle. Fold the work double with the wrong side out and weave sts together.
Sew the sleeve seams and sew in the sleeves. 
Sew in the zipper.
