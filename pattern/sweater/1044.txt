Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 17 sts x 23 rows on needles size 5 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 

Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Pattern:
The entire pattern is knitted in stocking st.  Anker = anchor 
Front and back piece: continue in red until piece measures 17-20 (22-23-24) cm, 5 rounds navy blue, 3 rounds white, 5 rounds navy blue, 3 rounds white, anchor pattern, 3 rounds white, 5 rounds navy blue, complete piece in white.
Sleeve: continue in white until piece measures 9-12 (13-14-17) cm, 5 rounds navy blue, 5 rounds white, 5 rounds navy blue, 15 rounds red, 5 rounds navy blue, complete piece in white. 
----------------------------------------------------------

Front and back piece: 
Knitted in the round on circular needle.  Cast on 110-118 (126-134-140) sts on circular needle size 3 mm with red and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 5 mm and stocking st (see pattern description above), at the same time inc 22-32 (30-34-40) sts evenly on first round = 132-150 (156-168-180) sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately.  

Front piece:  = 66-75 (78-84-90) sts. When piece measures 36-40 (44-46-48) cm cast off the middle 24-25 (26-28-28) sts and complete each side separately. . Right shoulder:  Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 1 st 3 times, cast off remaining sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. Left shoulder: change to needle size 3 mm and knit 4 cm Rib, but after 2.5 cm make 3 buttonholes. 1 buttonhole = cast off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts on return row.  Cast off. 

Back piece: = 66-75 (78-84-90) sts. When piece measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm cast off the middle 28-29 (30-32-32) sts. Right shoulder: dec 1 st towards neckline on next row and cast off when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.  Left shoulder: change to needle size 3 mm and knit 5 cm Rib, cast off. 

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 28-28 (30-30-32) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with white and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in stocking sts (see pattern description above), at the same time, inc 6-6 (6-6-8) sts evenly on first round = 34-34 (36-36-40) sts. Now inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 15-17 (19-19-21) times as follows:
Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately

Size 11/12: on every *4th, 4th and 3rd * round alternately
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. Cast off when sleeve measures 27-29 (33-35-40) cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 72-80 sts round the neck on needle size 3 mm with navy blue (pick up over Rib on front piece, round the neck up to the start of Rib on back piece). Knit 4 rows stocking st, P 1 row = folding edge, 4 rows stocking st. Cast off, fold neck double towards WS and fasten.  Set in sleeves and sew on buttons.
