Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 23 sts x 30 rows on needles size 3.5 mm in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Stripe pattern: 10 rounds violet, 2 rounds black, 10 rounds purple, 2 rounds black, 10 rounds yellow, 2 rounds black, 10 rounds mint, 2 rounds black, 10 rounds turquoise.

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needle.  Cast on 160-170 (180-190-200) sts on circular needle size 2.5mm with black and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 3.5 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 24-28 (32-36-40) sts evenly on first round = 184-198 (212-226-240) sts. When piece measures 10-12 (14-16-18) cm continue in stripe pattern – see above.  Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

Back piece:  = 92-99 (106-113-120) sts. After stripe pattern complete piece in black. When piece measures 39-41 (46-48-50) cm cast off the middle 40-41 (42-43-44) sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Dec to shape the neckline: 1 st 1 time. Cast off remaining sts on shoulder when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Front piece: = = 92-99 (106-113-120) sts. Continue as described for back piece until piece measures 38-41 (45-47-49) cm. Now cast off the middle 36-37 (38-39-40) sts for neck and complete each side separately. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1 time. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.  

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 36-38 (40-42-44) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with black and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 3.5 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 10-8 (10-14-14) sts evenly on first round = 46-46 (50-56-58) sts.  At the same time, inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 21-23 (26-23-26) times as follows:
Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8 years: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round 
= 88-92 (102-102-110) sts. At the same time, when piece measures 6-7 (10-14-17) cm knit stripe pattern, and then complete sleeve in black. Cast off when piece measures 26-30 (33-37-40) cm.  

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 90-100 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with black and knit 3 cm Rib, change to purple, knit 1 round and cast off in purple. Set in sleeves.
