Sizes: 3/4 - 5/6 (7/8 - 89/10 - 11/12) years

Finished measurements: 78-84 (90-96-102) cm

Materials:
Garnstudio KARISMA CLASSIC
100% pure new wool 
300-350 (350-400-400) g col. 01, natural

DROPS 2.5 mm  and 4 mm  straight and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge. 

5-6 buttons.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 22 sts x 28 rows on larger needles in Pattern 1 = 10 x 10 cm 

Rib: * K 1, P 1 *

"Bobble": K 3 sts into 1 st, knit 3 rows stockinette st over these sts, on the 4th row K 3 tog to make 1 st. 

Double Seed st (on an even number of sts): 
1st row: k 1, p 1
2nd row: k 1, p 1
3rd row: p 1, k 1
4th row: p 1, k 1

Pattern: See chart below (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is seen from the right side. 

Fronts: Cast on 36-38 (40-42-44) sts on smaller needles and knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger needles, increasing 10-12 (14-16-18) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 46-50 (54-58-62) sts. Establish pattern as follows (right side row, beginning at the side edge): 2-3 (5-6-8) sts double seed st, Pattern 2, 8-10 (12-14-16) sts double seed st, Pattern 1, 4-5 (5-6-6) sts double seed st (center front). Knit pattern as established until the work measures 23-25 (27-29-29) cm . Bind off 1 st at the side edge for armhole. When the work measures 37-40 (44-46-48) cm , bind off at the neck edge every other row: 9-10 (11-13-14) sts 1 time, 4 sts 1 time, 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 42-45 (49-51-53) cm .

Back: Cast on 72-76 (80-84-88) sts on smaller needles and knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger needles, increasing 14-16 (20-22-24) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 86-92 (100-106-112) sts. Establish pattern as follows: 6-6 (7-8-10) sts double seed st, Pattern 2, 25-28 (31-33-34) sts double seed st, Pattern 2, 25-28 (31-33-34) sts double seed st, Pattern 2, 6-6 (7-8-10) sts double seed st. When the work measures 23-25 (27-29-29) cm , bind off 1 st at each side for armholes. When the work measures 41-44 (48-50-52) cm , bind off the center 34-34 (36-38-38) sts, then bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off the remaining sts when the work measures 42-45 (49-51-53) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 32-34 (34-36-38) sts on smaller double pointed needles; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger double pointed needles, increasing 18-18 (20-22-24) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 50-52 (54-58-62) sts. Establish the pattern as follows: Pattern 1 centered on the sleeve and double seed st on either side. Then inc 1 st each side of marker 20-21 (24-23-25) times:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: every 3rd row 
Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row 
= 90-94 (102-104-112) sts - knit the increased sts into double seed st as you go along. When the work measures 27-30 (34-37-41) cm , bind off. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 84-100 sts along the left buttonband on smaller needles and knit 3 cm  rib. Repeat along the right buttonband, but after 1 cm  make 5-6 buttonholes (1 buttonhole = bind off 3 sts and cast on 3 new sts on the next row over the bound-off sts). Pick up approx. 84-90 sts around the neck on smaller needles and knit 2 cm  stockinette st, purl 1 row (right side, for the folding edge), 2 cm  stockinette st, bind off and fold the edge over double against the wrong side and sew. Sew the sleeves to the body and sew the sleeve- and side-seam in one. Sew on the buttons.
