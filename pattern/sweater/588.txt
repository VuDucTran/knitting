Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 21 sts x 28 rows with Karisma Superwash on needle size 4 mm in stocking st  = 10 x 10 cm.
Rib: * K1, P1 *, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: See diagram. Stocking st the entire pattern. If it proves complicated to knit in all colours at the same time, black Chenille can be embroidered on with mattress sts afterwards (see diagram). Knit with brown Chenille over the symbol of the black Chenille if embroidering black Chenille on. The white of the eye is embroidered on afterwards.
Knitting tips: Remember to twist the threads at colour changes. Knit Pelliza in double yarn. Important – to avoid stretching the yarn over too many stitches at the colour changes use small balls of yarn on each side of the motif.

Front piece: Cast on 87-91-99-103 sts (incl 1 edge sts each side) on needle size 3 mm with Karisma Superwash and knit Rib with edge sts in garter sts. After 5-5-6-6 cm change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st with edge sts in garter st. Start pattern when piece measures 2-5-5-6 cm – see explanation + knitting tips - (make sure to place the motif in the center – count from the marked middle st). Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 32-35-36-37 cm cast off for armhole each side on every other row: 3 sts 1-1-2-2 times, 2 sts 3-3-3-4 times, 1 st 3-4-4-3 times = 63-65-67-69 sts. When piece measures 42-47-48-50 cm cast off the middle 9-11-11-13 sts for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 4 times, 1 st 2 times = 17-17-18-18 m left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 48-53-55-57 cm.

Back: Cast on and knit as front piece – without the motif. Cast off for armhole as described for front piece. Continue until piece measures 47-52-54-56 cm, now cast off the middle 27-29-29-31 sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row = 17-17-18-18 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 48-53-55-57 cm.

Sleeve: Piece is knitted in the round on double pointed needles.
Cast on 44-48-48-50 sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with Karisma Superwash. Knit 5-5-6-6 cm Rib. Change to double pointed needles size 4 mm and continue in stocking sts. At the same time, inc 2 sts mid under arm: 12-12-14-15 times on every 6-6-6-6 rounds = 68-72-76-80 sts. When piece measures 32-35-39-41 cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm and complete the sleeve back and forth on needle. Cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 2 sts 2-2-3-2 times, 1 st 7-8-9-11 times, 2 st 3-3-3-3 times, 3 st 1-1-1-1 times. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures approx 39-43-48-51 cm.

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seams. Gather approx 90-100 sts on double pointed needles size 4 mm in double red Pelliza. Knit 5-6 cm rib, cast off, turn the neck edge double towards WS and sew.
