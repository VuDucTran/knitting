Sizes: 8/10 (12/14) years
Finished measurements: 96 (106) cm 

Materials: Garnstudio TYNN CHENILLE, 
90% cotton, 10% polyester, 50 g/120 m. 
250 (250) g color no. 901, natural

and use: Garnstudio COTTON VISCOSE
54% Egyptian cotton, 46% viscose
50 g/110 m
250 (250) g color no. 02, natural

and use: Garnstudio PELLIZA, 
100% Polyester, 50 g./115 m
200 (250) g color no. 12, natural

DROPS 5 mm  and 6 mm  circular and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 12 sts x 16 rows on larger needles in stockinette st with 3 strands of yarn = 10 x 10 cm .

Rib: * K 3, P 3 *. Repeat from * - *.

Body: Cast on 114 (126) sts on smaller circular needles with 1 strand Tynn Chenille + 1 strand Cotton Viscose + 1 strand Pelliza (= 3 strands). Knit rib for 9 cm . Change to larger circular needles and stockinette st. When the work measures 28 (32) cm  knit the next row as follows: bind off 2 sts for armhole, 53 (59) sts for the front, bind off 4 sts for armhole, 53 (59) sts for the back, bind off 2 sts for armhole. Knit the rest of each part separately.

Front: = 53 (59) sts. Bind off for armholes each side every other row: 2 sts 2 (3) times, 1 st 2 (2) times = 41 (43) sts. When the work measures 41 (46) cm  bind off the center 9 (11) sts for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 47 (53) cm .

Back: = 53 (59) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front = 41 (43) sts. When the work measures 45 (51) cm  bind off the center 13 (15) sts for the neck. Bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 47 (53) cm .

Sleeve: Cast on 24 (24) sts on smaller double pointed needles with 1 strand Tynn Chenille + 1 strand Cotton Viscose + 1 strand Pelliza (= 3 strands); join and place a marker at the join. Knit rib for 8 cm . Change to larger double pointed needles and stockinette st. After the rib, inc 1 st each side of marker 11 (13) times alternately every 3rd and 4th row = 46 (50) sts. When the work measures 37 (42) cm  bind off 2 sts each side of marker and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 5 (6) times, 3 sts 1 time, bind off. The work measures approx. 47 (53) cm .

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seam. Pick up approx. 48-58 sts around the neck on smaller double pointed needles with 1 strand Tynn Chenille + 1 strand Cotton Viscose + 1 strand Pelliza (= 3 strands). Join and purl 1 row (right side row), knit 6 rows stockinette st, bind off and let the neck edge roll up. Sew in the sleeves.
