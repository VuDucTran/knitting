DROPS 85-24
Sizes: 12/14 years - S/M - L - XL - XXL

Materials: DROPS KARISMA SUPERWASH from Garnstudio
450-500-550-600-650 gr nr 44, light grey
and use:
DROPS ALPACA from Garnstudio
250-300-350-350-400 gr nr 0100, natural

DROPS 4.5 mm straight and double-pointed needles and 5.5 mm  straight needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge. You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 37.40£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. You will find a prices table in this print to help you calculate the price for other sizes.All yarns are DROPS yarns. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 15 sts x 21 rows on larger needles with 1 strand Karisma and 1 strand Alpaca in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: *K 3, P 3*, repeat *-*.

Pattern: See the chart. The pattern is seen from the right side. Arrows indicate the starting points for different sizes.


Decreasing tips (for armholes):
All decs are made on right side rows. Dec 1 st inside 5 edge sts in stockinette st as follows:
After 5 edge sts: Sl 1 as if to knit, K 1, psso.
Before 5 edge sts: K 2 tog.

Front: Cast on 71-77-83-89-95 sts on smaller needles with 1 strand Karisma Superwash + 1 strand Alpaca and knit 2 rows stockinette st, then knit rib as follows: K 1 (edge st knit in garter st), *K 3, P 3*, finish with K 3 and K 1 (edge st knit in garter st). When the piece measures 6-7-7-7-7 cm change to larger needles and knit 4 rows garter st, adjusting (dec/inc) to 70-76-82-88-94 sts on the 1st row. Then knit stockinette st. When the piece measures 10-11-11-11-11 cm inc 1 st at each side every 8-12-12-13-13 cm a total of 3 times = 76-82-88-94-100 sts.
When the piece measures 17-21-21-22-23 cm P rows (first row = right side), then knit Pattern 1 to finished measurements (start at appropriate arrow for your size).
When the piece measures 34-42-43-44-45 cm bind off 4 sts at each side for armhole. Then knit Pattern 1, keeping 5 sts at each side in stockinette st. Dec 1 st at armholes every other row 3-2-5-4-7 times – see Decreasing tips above = 62-70-70-78-78 sts. When the piece measures approx. 48-57-59-61-63 cm – adjust to end after 1 entire square of Pattern 1 – bind off the center 10-12-12-12-12 sts for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 3 times and 1 st 1-1-1-2-2 times = 19-22-22-25-25 sts remain on each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 54-64-66-68-70 cm.

Back: Cast on and knit 2 rows stockinette st as on front, and then knit rib as follows: K 1 (edge st knit in garter st), P 3, *K 3, P 3*, finish with K 1 (edge st knit in garter st). Then continue as on front. Bind off and dec for armholes as on front.
When the piece measures 52-62-64-66-68 cm bind off the center 22-24-24-26-26 sts for the neck. On the next row dec 1 st at each neck edge = 19-22-22-25-25 sts remain on each shoulder Bind off when the piece measures 54-64-66-68-70 cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 38-38-38-44-44 sts on smaller needles with 1 strand Karisma Superwash + 1 strand Alpaca. Knit 4 rows garter st, and then knit rib keeping 1 edge st at each side in garter st.
When the piece measures 6-7-7-7-7 cm change to larger needles and knit 4 rows garter st, adjusting (inc/dec) on the 1st row to 37-38-38-41-41 sts. Continue in stockinette st to finished measurements. When the piece measures 10-9-12-10-10 cm inc 1 st at each side (inc inside 1 edge st for smooth edge) every 3.5-3.5-2.5-3-2.5 cm a total of 11-12-15-14-16 times = 59-62-68-69-73 sts.
When sleeve measures 48-50-51-51-51 cm bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 4 sts 1 time, 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 2 times and 1 st 1 time, then 2 sts at each side until the piece measures 54-56-58-58-60 cm, then bind off 3 sts at each side 1 time. Bind off the remaining sts, the piece measures approx. 55-57-59-59-61 cm.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams.
Neckband: Pick up and knit approx. 66-78 sts (divisible by 6) around the neck on smaller double-pointed needles with 1 strand Karisma Superwash + 1 strand Alpaca; join and P 1 row and K 1 row then knit rib until the neck edge measures 13-15-15-15-15 cm, P 1 row, K 1 row, then bind off.
