Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 20 sts and 26 rows on larger needles in pattern = 10 x 10 cm 

Pattern: See chart. One chart equals one repeat of the pattern. The pattern is seen from the right side.

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter st, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Rib: * K1, P1*. Repeat from * - *. 

Body: Cast on 160-176-184 (192-208-216) sts on smaller circular needles with blue and knit as follows: 2 rows garter st, 2 cm  in rib, 2 rows garter st. Change to larger circular needles and knit pattern. When the work measures 29-31-34 (35-36-38) cm , knit the next row as follows: 38-42-44 (46-50-52) sts for the front, bind off 4 sts for armhole, 76-84-88 (92-100-104) for the back, bind off 4 sts for armhole, 38-42-44 (46-50-52) sts for the front. Knit each part separately. 

Front: 38-42-44 (46-50-52) sts. Bind off for armholes every other row: 2 sts 2-3-2 (3-3-3) times, 1 st 3-2-3 (2-3-3) times. 31-34-37 (38-41-43) sts remain. When the work measures 39-42-46 (48-49-52) cm , bind off for the neck every other row: 8-8-9 (9-10-10) sts 1 time, 2 sts 3 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off all sts when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm . 

Back: 76-84-88 (92-100-104) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front. 62-68-74 (76-82-86) sts remain. When the work measures 43-46-51 (53-55-58) cm , bind off the center 30-30-32 (32-34-34) sts for the neck. Bind off 1 st on each neck edge on the next row. Bind off all sts when the work measures 45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm.

Sleeves: Cast on 36-38-40 (42-44-46) on smaller double pointed needles with blue. Join, being careful not to twist the sts. Place a marker at the join and knit 2 rows garter st, 2 cm in rib, 2 rows garter st. Change to larger double pointed needles and pattern (centering the pattern across the sleeve). Inc 0-2-4 (2-2-0) sts evenly distributed over the first row = 36-40-44 (44-46-46) sts.
At the same time, after beginning pattern, inc 1 st on each side of the marker 14-14-16 (18-19-21) times:
Size 3/4 years: every 3rd row.
Size 5/6 years: alternately every 3rd and 4th row.
Sizes 7/8 - 9/10 - 11/12 - 13/14 years: every 4th row.
= 64-68-76 (80-84-88) sts remain. When the work measures 23-26-30 (34-37-40) cm, bind off 2 sts on each side of the marker and knit the rest back and forth on the needle. Bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 3 sts 6 times. Bind off when the work measures 29-32-36 (40-43-46) cm.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 90-96-106 (110-114-120) sts along the left front on smaller circular needles with blue and knit 2 rows garter st, 2 cm in rib, 2 rows garter st. Bind off. Repeat along right front, but after 1 cm make 6-6-7 (7-8-8) buttonholes evenly spaced along the row. 1 buttonhole = bind off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts over those bind off on the next row. Pick up approx. 86-100 sts around the neck on smaller circular needles with blue and knit 2 rows garter st, 2 cm in rib, 2 rows garter st, bind off. Sew on sleeves.
