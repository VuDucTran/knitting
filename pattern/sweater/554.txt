Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension:  22 m x 30 rows on needle size 3.5 mm and stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. Remember the needles sizes are guidelines only!
Garter sts (on circular needle): 1st round: P, 2nd round: K.
Garter sts (back and forth): knit all rows.
Pattern: See diagram M.1 and M.2. 

Back and front piece: Cast on 132-144-156 (168-180-192) sts on circular needle size 3.5 mm in blue. Knit 4 rounds garter sts and continue with 2 repeats of M.2. Knit 1 round garter sts at the same time increasing 4-0-4 (0-4-0) sts evenly on round = 128-144-152 (168-176-192) sts. Now insert a marking thread each side and finish the piece in M.1 - start at the arrow for appropriate size and knit pattern over sts on front piece, start at the appropriate arrow again and knit pattern over sts on back piece. Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 22-25-29 (30-32-37) cm cast of 3 sts for armhole each side of both marking threads (= 6 sts for each armhole). Complete front and back pieces separately.

Front piece: = 58-66-70 (78-82-90) sts. Cast off for armhole each side on every other row: 2 sts 2-3-2 (2-2-3) times, 1 st 3-2-2 (3-2-3) times = 44-50-58 (64-70-72) sts on needle. When piece measures 29-33-38 (41-44-49) cm cast off the middle 10-10-12 (12-14-14) sts for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 2-2-3 (3-3-4) times, 1 st 2-3-1 (2-2-2) times. When piece measures 33-37-42 (46-49-54) cm cast off remaining sts.

Back: = 58-66-70 (78-82-90) sts. Cast off for armhole as done for the front piece. = 44-50-58 (64-70-72) sts on needle. Continue as follows:
The 3 smallest sizes: Knit until piece measures 29-33-38 cm, divide the piece mid back for split and finish each side separately. Continue in pattern with 1 st towards the split in garter sts until piece measures 32-36-41 cm and cast off 11-12-13 sts for neck mid back. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 33-37-42 cm.
The 3 largest sizes: Knit until piece measures (45-48-53) cm, now cast off the middle (28-30-34) sts for neck. Continue in pattern and cast off remaining sts when piece measures (46-49-54) cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 32-32-40 (40-48-48) sts on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm in blue. Knit garter sts for 3-3-3 (4-4-4) cm. Continue in M.1 – beg pattern mid under arm for all sizes by the arrow for size 6/9 months. At the same time when piece measures 6-6-6 (8-8-8) cm inc 2 sts mid under arm 6-8-6 (13-11-11) times: sizes 6/9 months: Every 6th round. Size 12/18 months: Alternating every 5th and 6th round. Size 2 years: Every 9 th round. Size 3/4 years: Alternating every 4th and 5 th round. Size 5/6 years: Alternately every 6 th and 7th round. Size 7/8 years: Every 7th round  = 44-48-52 (66-70-70) sts. When piece measures 19-21-25 (28-32-35) cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm and complete the piece back and forth on needle. Cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 3 sts 0-0-0 (1-1-1) times, 2 sts 1-1-2 (2-2-2) times, 1 st 6-6-4 (2-1-3) times, 2 sts 1-1-2 (2-3-3) times, 3 sts 1-1-1 (2-2-2) times. Cast off remaining sts, sleeve measures approx 26-28-32 (35-39-43) cm.

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seems together. Pick up approx 55-80 sts round the neck (from mid back on the 3 smallest sizes) on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm in blue and knit garter st for 3 cm, cast off. Sew the sleeves onto the jersey. The 3 smallest sizes: Double crochet with blue on needle size 3 mm round the split at the back – crochet 2 loops for the button holes = 5 slip sts – evenly distributed along the right side of the split.
