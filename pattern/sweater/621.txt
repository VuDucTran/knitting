Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 17 sts x 23 rows on larger needles in pattern = 10 x 10 cm .

Rib: * K 1, P 1 *

Pattern: See charts (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette st. 

Stripes: * 1 row yellow, 5 rows purple *, repeat * - *

Body: Cast on 112-120 (128-136-140) sts on smaller circular needles with blue; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger circular needles and knit 3-4 (8-8-9) cm  stockinette st, increasing 20-24 (28-32-40) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 132-144 (156-168-180) sts (66-72 (78-84-90) sts each front and back). Knit Pattern 1 once (center the pattern across the front) and then knit with green. When the work measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm , divide at the sides and knit the rest of the front and back separately.

Front: = 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. When the work measures 26-27 (31-32-33) cm , knit Pattern 2 once, then 1-3 (3-3-4) cm  with blue, Pattern 3 once, and knit the rest with red. At the same time, when the work measures 35-38 (42-44-46) cm , bind off the center 18-18 (20-22-22) sts for the neck. Then bind off at each neck edge 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off the remaining sts for the shoulder when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Back: = 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. Knit pattern as for front. When the work measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm , bind off the center 28-28 (30-32-32) sts for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 28-28 (32-32-32) sts on smaller double pointed needles with blue; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger double pointed needles and knit Pattern 4 (centering the pattern on the sleeve) and increasing 6-6 (6-8-10) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 34-34 (38-40-42) sts. At the same time, inc 1 st each side of marker 15-17 (18-17-20) times:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: every 3rd row 
Size 9/10: every 4th row 
Size 11/12: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row = 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. After Pattern 4 is complete, knit stripes (see instructions above) until the sleeve measures 21-24 (27-31-34) cm . Adjust for stripe pattern (start with 1 row yellow and finish with 4 rows purple). After the stripe pattern knit Pattern 3 once. Bind off. The work measures approx. 27-30 (33-37-40) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up 76-88 sts on smaller double pointed needles with purple around the neck; join and knit 2 cm  rib, change to red and knit 1 row rib. Bind off with red. 
Sew the sleeves to the body.
