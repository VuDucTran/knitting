Sizes: 
child’s, 8/10 - 12/14 years 
(women’s, Small-Medium-Large)
Finished Measurements: 92-104 (112-120-126) cm. 

Note: Finished measurements indicate sweater size after it has been blocked. The sweater will be narrower before blocking due to the rib pattern.

Materials: DROPS PELLIZA, 
100% Polyester 
450-500 (550-600-650) g in Color No. 04, Turquoise.

DROPS 3.5mm circular and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.
Gauge: 23 sts x 30 rows in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm . 

Rib: * knit 1, purl 1.* Repeat from * - *.

Long model: If a long model is desired knit as follows: increase the measurements at armhole, neck and binding off by 17-18 (15-18-20) cm . Finished length = 62-67 (70-74-77) cm . Increase the yarn amount by approximately 50 g. 

Front: Cast on 108-122 (130-140-148) sts (including 1 edge st on each side for seam) and knit in rib pattern keeping the edge sts in garter stitch. When the work measures 25-27 (31-31-32) cm , bind off for armholes on each side every other row: 3 sts 2-2 (1-2-2) times, 2 sts 2-2 (3-3-4) times, 1 st 3-3 (3-3-4) times. 82-96 (106-110-112) sts.
When the work measures 40-44 (50-51-52) cm , bind off the center 18-20 (20-22-24) sts for the neck. Then bind off at the neck edge every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1-1 (2-2-2) times, 1 st 3 times. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (55-56-57) cm . 

Back: Cast on and knit as on the front, but when the work measures 43-47 (53-54-55) cm , bind off the center 30-32 (36-38-40) sts for the neck. Then bind off 2 sts at each neck edge. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (55-56-57) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 48-52 (52-56-60) sts (includes 1 edge st on each side for seam kept in garter st throughout) and knit in Rib pattern. When the work measures 5 cm , inc 1 st on each side 23-26 (30-30-28) times:
Sizes 8/10 + 12/14 years: every 4th row. 
Sizes S + M + L: every 3rd and every 4th row. 
= 94-104 (112-116-116) sts. When the work measures 38-44 (44-43-43) cm , bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 3 sts 11-11 (12-14-15) times. Bind off. The work measures approximately 45-51 (52-52-53) cm . 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam. Pick up approximately 88-92 (98-102-106) sts (divisible by 2) around the neck on double pointed needles and knit rib for 1 cm , then stockinette stitch for 10 cm , then inc 20 sts evenly distributed on the row. Change to reverse stockinette stitch and, when the collar measures 17 cm , inc 20 sts evenly distributed on the row. When the collar measures 22 cm  inc 15 sts evenly distributed on the row. Bind off loosely when the collar measures 24 cm . Sew the sleeves to the body. Sew sleeve seam and side seam in one.
