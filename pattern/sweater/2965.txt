Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 10 x 10 cm = 14 sts x 24 rows on needle size 5.5 mm and moss st.

Moss st: Row 1: * K2, P2 * Row 2: K over K and P over P. Row 3: * P2  K2 * Row 4: Like row 2. Repeat row 1-4. 

Rib: * K1, P1 *

BACK PIECE: Cast on 60-66 (72-78-84) sts on needle size 3 mm. Work 5 cm rib. Change to needle size 5.5 mm and continue in moss st – see above – AT THE SAME TIME dec evenly to 54-58 (60-64-70) sts on first row. Cast off all sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-51-53) cm.

FRONT PIECE: Cast on and work like back piece until piece measures 30-32 (34-36-37) cm. Now cast off the middle 14-14 (16-18-18) sts and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off remaining sts on shoulder when piece measures 40-43 (47-51- 53) cm.

SLEEVES: Cast on 34-36 (38-38-40) sts on needle size 3 mm. Work 5 cm rib. Change to needle size 5.5 mm and continue in moss st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly to 32-34 (40-44-48) sts on first row. Now inc 1 st each side a total of 8-8 (8-9-8) times on every 6-8 (9-9-10) row = 48-50 (56-62-64) sts. Cast off all sts when sleeve measures 25-29 (35-40-42) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 48-52 (60-66-70) sts round neck on circular needle size 4 mm – do not pick up on the middle 14-14 (16-18-18) sts on front piece. Work rib back and forth on needle. When collar measures 4-4 (4-4-4) cm inc every other P-section to P2. When collar measures 7-7 (8-9-9) cm inc inc remaining P-section to P2, i.e. work K1, P2 all the way round. Cast off when collar measures 11-11 (12-13-13) cm. Place the right side of rib over the left side mid front and sew to front piece. Set in sleeves.  Sew sleeve and side seams.
