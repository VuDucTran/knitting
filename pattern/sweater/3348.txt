Cast on 138-142-152 (162-172-182) sts on circular needle size 2,5 with indigo, work 4 cm rib and inc 42-50-52- (66-68-70) sts evenly on the last row = 180-192-204 (228-240-sts. Switch to circular needle size 4 and work in pattern to end of row as follows: M.1 x 3-3-3 (4-4-4), 18-24 (10-16-22) sts of M.2, 34-34-34 (47-47-47) sts of M.1 - but M.1 is worked from left to right, 10-10-10 (10-10-10) sts of M.2, 34-34-34, (47-47-47) sts of M.1 - but M.1 is worked from left to right, 12-18-24 (10-16-22) sts of M.2 and then M.1 x 3-3-3 (4-4-4) - but M.1 is worked from left to right. Work the pattern upwards. Remember the tension. When work measures 24-24-26 (28-29-30) cm divide into front and back piece as follows: 45-48-51 (57-60-63) sts front piece, 90-96-102 (114-120-12 back piece and 45-48-51 (57-60-63) sts front piece. Back piece: = 90-96-102 (114-120-126) sts. When work measures 35-38-41 (45-47-49) cm cast off the mid 30-36-36 (38-38-40) sts for neck. Then dec 1 st in each side on next row towards the neck. Cast off when work measures 37 43 (47-49-51) cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 36-36-38 (40-42-44) sts on double pointed needle size 2.5 with indigo and work 4 cm rib. Switch to double pointed needle size 4, work M.2 and inc 4-4-6 (8-6-6) sts evenly on 1 st round 40-40-44 (48-48-50) sts. Then inc mid under sleeve: 2 sts x 6-12-12 (14-16-17) on Size 2 years: Every 6th round, Size 3/4: Alternately every 3th and 4th round, Size 7/8 years: Every 4th round and Sizes 5/6+9/10+11/12 years: Alternately every 4th and 5th round = 52-64-68 (76-80-84) sts. When piece measures 20-23-27 (30-34-37) cm cast off 6 sts mid under sleeve and complete piece back and forth on needle. Then cast off for sleeve cap in each size on every 2nd row: 5 sts x 3, cast off. Piece measures approx. 23-26-30 (33-37 cm.

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seam. Pick up approx. 70-90 sts along left front piece on circular needle size 2,5 with indigo and work 2 cm rib back and forth on needle. Repeat along the right front piece, but after 0,5 cm make 5 buttonholes evenly distributed on the row- 1 buttonhole = cast off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts on next row. Pick up 90-106 sts around the neck on double pointed needle size 2,5 with indigo and work 2 cm rib back and forth on needle. Sew in the sleeves and buttons.
