Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 

Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st. See M.1-M.4.  
Body piece: Knit M.1, begin by arrow and knit pattern on front piece, begin by arrow again and knit pattern on back piece, knit M,2, and complete piece in M.1. Sleeve: Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: M.3. Size 9/10 + 11/12: M.1, M.2, M.4. 

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needles and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on circular needle size 2.5mm with ice blue and knit Rib as follows: 1 round ice blue, change to white and knit 5 cm. Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 26-30 (36-40-44) sts evenly on first round = 156-168 (180-192-204) sts. When piece measures 11-14 (18-20-22) cm continue in pattern – see above explanation. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm inc 4 sts each side for armhole (to be cut for armholes afterwards and not included in pattern but knitted in stocking st). When piece measures 38-41 (45-47-49) cm cast off 30-30 (32-34-34) sts mid front for neck and complete each side separately. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row:  2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1 time. At the same time, when piece measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm cast off 36-36 (38-40-40) sts mid back for neck and complete each side separately. Cast off when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 30-32 (34-36-38) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with ice blue. Knit Rib as follows: 1 round ice blue, change to white and knit 4 cm. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 10-8 (10-12-12) sts evenly on first round = 40-40 (44-48-50) sts. When sleeve measures 19-22 (26-14-18) cm continue in pattern – see above explanation and make sure to place a pattern repeat mid upper sleeve. At the same time, inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 18-20 (22-20-23) times as follows:
Size 3/4 +5/6 + 7/8 years: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round 
= 76-80 (88-88-96) sts – incorporate the new sts in pattern as you go along. After the last pattern round, sleeve measures approx 27-30 (34-37-41) cm. Now knit 5 rounds in reverse stocking st = edge for sewing on sleeve to body piece. Cast off and knit the other sleeve. 

Assembly: Insert a marking thread in the middle of the inc sts for armhole each side. Make 2 seams each side on your sewing machine, the first seam ½ st from the MT and the other one ½ st from the first one. Cut the piece between these. Sew shoulder seams.
Set in sleeves from the RS as follows: sew in the last row before the edge in reverse stocking sts on sleeves to the first row after the seam in the sts for cutting on body piece. Turn the jumper inside out and sew the piece in stocking sts over the cutting edge on body piece. 
Neckline:  Pick up approx 80-100 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with blue and knit 3 cm Rib, change to ice blue, knit 1 round and cast off in ice blue.
