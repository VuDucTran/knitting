Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4 mm in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st.  

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on circular needle size 2.5 mm with purple and knit 4-4 (4-5-5) cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in pattern, at the same time inc 26-30 (36-40-44) sts evenly on round = 156-168 (180-192-204) sts. Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, inc 4 sts each side for armhole (to be cut for armholes afterwards and not included in pattern) (if you prefer not to cut for armhole, divide the piece and complete front and back piece separately). When piece measures 37-40 (44-46-48) cm cast off the middle 30-30 (32-34-34) sts on front piece for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time. At the same time, when piece measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm cast off the middle 34-34 (36-38-38) sts for neck on back piece and dec 1 st on neckline on next row. Cast of remaining sts on shoulder when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.

Sleeve: Knitted in the  round on double pointed needles. Cast on 30-32 (34-36-38) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with purple and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in pattern, at the same time inc 12-10 (14-12-16) sts evenly on round = 42-42 (48-48-54) sts.  Now inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 17-19 (20-20-21) times as follows:
Size 3/4, 5/6: on every 3rd round 
Size 7/8 years: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round
= 76-80 (88-88-96) sts. When piece measures 27-30.5 (33-38-41) cm knit 5 rounds in reverse stocking st = edge for sewing on sleeve to body piece. Cast off and knit the other sleeve. 

Assembly:  Insert a marking thread in the middle of the inc sts for armhole each side. Make 2 seams each side on your sewing machine, the first seam ½ st from the MT and the other one ½ st from the first one. Cut the piece between these. Sew shoulder seams. Set in sleeves from the RS as follows: sew in the last row before the edge in reverse stocking sts on sleeves to the first row after the seam in the sts for cutting on body piece. Turn the jumper inside out and sew the piece in stocking sts over the cutting edge on body piece. 
Neckline: Pick up approx 76-88 sts with purple on needle size 2.5 mm round the neck, knit 6 rows stocking st, P 1 round = folding edge, 5 rows stocking st. Fold neck double towards WS and fasten.
