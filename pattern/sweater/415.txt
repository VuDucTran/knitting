Sizes: 0/3-6/9-12/18 months (2-3/4-5/6 years). 
Finished measurements: 56-60-66 (76-80-86) cm 


Materials: Garnstudio SAFRAN 
(100% Egyptian cotton) 50 g./160 m
100-100-100 (100-150-150) g. Col. 30, turquoise
100-100-150 (150-150-150) g. Col. 24, royal blue
50-50-50 (50-50-50) g. Col. 31, pistachio

DROPS 2.5 mm  and 3 mm  circular and double pointed needles, or sizes to obtain correct gauge.

2-2-2 (0-0-0) Smily buttons (pistachio)

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 24 sts and 32 rows on larger needle in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm 

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter Stitch, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl *. Repeat from * - *.

Border: 4 rows garter st, 2 rows stockinette st, 4 rows garter st, 3 rows stockinette st.

Pattern: See chart. The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette st. Arrows indicate the starting points for different sizes.


Body: Knit the sweater in the round and cut steeks for armholes afterwards. Cast on 132-144-156 (180-192- 204) sts on smaller circular needles with turquoise; join and place a marker at the join. Knit border (see instructions above). Change to larger circular needles and knit Pattern 1 (start at the arrow on all patterns). After Pattern 1 continue knitting with royal blue until the work measures 6-6-7 (7-11-11) cm . Knit Pattern 2, then knit Pattern 3. At the same time, when the work measures 25-24-25 (31-32-31) cm , inc 5 sts on each side (at the marker and then halfway through the row - these sts are for the armhole steeks and should not be knit into the pattern). 
3 smallest sizes: When the work measures 29-29-31 cm , bind off the center 4 sts in the back for a split and knit the work back and forth on the needles. When the work measures 31-30-32 cm , bind off the center 8-10-12 sts for the front neck. Bind off at each front neck edge every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time. Work even until the work measures 33-33-35 cm  (now see «All sizes» below).
3 largest sizes: When the work measures 38-42-42 cm , bind off the center 14-16-16 sts for the front neck and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time. Work even in pattern until the work measures 42-46-46 cm , bind off the center back (20-22-22) sts for the neck. 
All sizes: Bind off at each back neck edge every other row: 6-7-8 (2-2-2) sts 1 time, 1 st 2-2-2 (1-1-1) times. When the work measures approx. 34-34-36 (43-47-47) cm  - adjust for complete pattern - finish with 2 rows turquoise, bind off all sts.

Sleeves: Cast on 36-36-38 (40-40-46) sts on smaller double pointed needles with turquoise; join and place a marker at the join. Knit border. Change to larger double pointed needles and knit Pattern 4 to finished dimensions. At the same time, when the work measures 3 cm , inc 1 st each side of marker 6-8-10 (11-18-18) times:
Sizes 0/3 +12/18 mos.+2 +5/6 years: every 4th row
Size 6/9 mos.: alternately every 4th and 5th row
Size 3/4 years: every 3rd row 
= 48-52-58 (62-76-82) sts. When the work measures 12-16-17 (18-23-27) cm , knit 2 cm  reverse stockinette st for a seam allowance over the cut edge on the body, bind off all sts.

Assembly: Sew a marker thread down into the center of the increased sts on each side. Sew two machine- stitched seams around the marker thread, 1st seam = 1/2 stitch from the marker thread, 2nd seam = 1/2 stitch from 1st seam. Cut up for armholes. Sew shoulder seams. Sew sleeves to the body from the right side as follows: Sew alternately a stitch in the last row on the sleeve before the seam allowance and a stitch on the body after the machine-stitched seams. Turn the sweater wrong side out and sew the seam allowance from the sleeve over the cutting edge by hand. Sew shoulder seams. 
Sizes 0/3 + 6/9 + 12/18 mos.: Pick up approx. 10-16 sts along the left side of the split in the back on smaller needle with turquoise and knit garter st for 1.5 cm , bind off. Repeat along the right side of the split, but after 0.5 cm  bind off for two buttonholes evenly distributed along the split (1 buttonhole = bind off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts over the bound off sts on the next row). Sew the bottom of the split together. Pick up approx. 30-40 sts around the neck from the center front to the center back over half of the placket from the wrong side on smaller needles with royal blue. Knit the collar in stockinette st but knit the two edge sts at the center front and center back in garter st. At the same time, inc 1 st every 3rd row on each side of the stitch directly on top of the shoulder. Knit until the collar measures 4-5-5 cm , then finish with 2 rows garter st in pistachio, 2 rows garter st in royal blue and 2 rows garter st in turquoise. Bind off all sts. Repeat the collar on the other side of the neck. Sew on buttons.
Sizes 2 + 3/4 + 5/6 years: Pick up approx. 70-90 sts around the neck from the center front from the wrong side on smaller needles with royal blue. Knit the collar in stockinette st but knit the two sts at the center front edge in garter st. At the same time, inc 1 st every 3rd row on each side of the sts directly on top of the shoulder. Knit until the collar measures (6-6-7) cm , then finish with 2 rows garter st in pistachio, 2 rows garter st in royal blue and 2 rows garter st in turquoise. Bind off all sts.
