Sizes: 3/4 - 5/6 (7/8 - 89/10 - 11/12) years

Finished measurements: 78-84 (92-98-106) cm

Materials:
Garnstudio PARIS, 100% cotton 
100-150 (150-200-200) g col. 01, salmon
150-150 (150-200-200) g col. 12, red

50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 37, wine red
50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 24, gray

DROPS 3.5 mm  and 5 mm  circular and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 17 cm x 23 rows on larger needles in pattern = 10 x 10 cm 

Rib: * K 2, P 2 * 

Pattern: See charts below (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette st. 

Body: Cast on 112-120 (128-136-140) sts on smaller circular needles with salmon; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger circular needles and Pattern 1, increasing 20-24 (28-32-40) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 132-144 (156-168-180) sts. After Pattern 1, knit 2-2 (3-3-3) cm  salmon and then knit Pattern 2 (centering the pattern across the front). When the work measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm , divide the work into two parts for armholes and knit the rest of the front and back separately. 

Back: = 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. After Pattern 2:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: knit the rest with salmon. Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: Pattern 1 and then knit the rest with salmon. 
At the same time, when the work measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm .

Front: 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. Knit Pattern as on the back. When the work measures 36-39 (43-45-47) cm , bind off the center 22-22 (24-26-26) sts for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times. Bind off the remaining sts for the shoulder when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 28-28 (32-32-32) sts on smaller double pointed needles with salmon; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. Change to larger double pointed needles and knit Pattern 1 (centering the pattern on the sleeve), increasing 6-6 (6-8-10) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 34-34 (38-40-42) sts. Inc 1 st each side of marker 15-17 (18-17-20) times:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: every 3rd row 
Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row 
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. After Pattern 1 knit 2-2 (2-3-3) cm  salmon, then knit:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6: the first 20-40 rows of Pattern 2, then the rest with red. 
Sizes 7/8 + 9/10: the first 45-45 rows of Pattern 2, then the rest with salmon. 
Size 11/12: Pattern 2, then the rest with salmon.
When the work measures 27-30 (34-37-40) cm , bind off.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up 76-86 sts on smaller double pointed needles with salmon around the neck and knit 3 cm  rib, but knit the last row with coral and bind off with coral. Sew the sleeves to the body.
