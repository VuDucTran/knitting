K all rows.

BUTTONHOLES:
Make buttonholes on right front band. 1 buttonhole = cast off 4th and 5th st from mid front and cast on 2 new sts on return row. 
Make buttonholes when piece measures:
SIZE 7/8 years: 3, 10, 16, 23, 29 and 36 cm
SIZE 9/10 years: 3, 10, 17, 24, 31 and 38 cm
SIZE 11/12 years: 5, 12, 19, 26, 33 and 40 cm
SIZE 13/14 years: 5, 13, 20, 28, 35 and 43 cm
NOTE: The last buttonhole is made on neckline. 
----------------------------------------------------------
----------------------------------------------------------
BACK PIECE:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 96-100-106-110 sts (includes 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3 mm with Alpaca. Work 6 rows garter st – see above – and continue in stocking st. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures 6 cm dec 1 st on each side and repeat the dec on every 4-4.5-5-5 cm a total of 6 times = 84-88-94-98 sts. When piece measures 32-34-36-38 cm cast off for armhole each side at the beg of every row: 3 sts 1-1-1-1 time, 2 sts 1-2-2-2 times and 1 st 3-2-2-2 times = 68-70-76-80 sts. When piece measures 37-39-41-43 cm dec 4 sts evenly on row = 64-66-72-76 sts. Now complete piece in garter st. When piece measures 45-48-51-54 cm cast off the middle 24-26-28-30 sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off on every row from neckline: 1 st 2 times = 18-18-20-21 sts left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 47-50-53-56 cm. 

LEFT FRONT PIECE:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 56-58-61-63 sts (includes 1 edge st at the side and 8 front band sts towards mid front) on needle size 3 mm with Alpaca. Work 6 rows garter st and continue in stocking st with 8 front band sts towards mid front in garter st. When piece measures 6 cm dec at the side as described for back piece = 50-52-55-57 sts. When piece measures 32-34-36-38 cm cast off for armhole at the side as described for back piece = 42-43-46-48 sts. When piece measures 37-39-41-43 cm dec 3 sts evenly on row (do not dec on front band) = 39-40-43-45 sts. Now complete piece in garter st on all sts, but to mark the front band P the 9th st from mid front on every row. When piece measures 42-45-47-50 cm slip 13-14-15-16 sts towards mid front on a stitch holder for neck and cast off to shape the neckline at the beg of every row from mid front: 2 sts 2 times and 1 st 4 times = 18-18-20-21 sts left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 47-50-53-56 cm. 

RIGHT FRONT PIECE: 
Like left front piece, but mirrored, and remember BUTTONHOLES – see above. 

SLEEVE: Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 50-52-54-56 sts (includes 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3 mm with Alpaca. Work 6 rows garter st and continue in stocking st. When piece measures 5 cm inc 1 st each side, and repeat the inc on every 11-9-10-9 row a total of 8-10-10-11 times = 66-72-74-78 sts. When piece measures 29-31-33-35 cm cast off for sleeve cap each side at the beg of every row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 2-3-4-4 times and 1 st 7-5-4-3 times, then 2 sts each side until piece measures 35-37-39-41 cm, now cast off 3 sts each side 1 time and cast off remaining sts. Piece measures approx 36-38-40-42 cm. 

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams.

NECKLINE: Pick up approx 85 to 105 sts round neckline (incl sts on stitch holders mid front) on needle size 3 mm with Alpaca. K 5 rows, AT THE SAME TIME on row 2 make 1 buttonhole in line with the others on front band. Cast off loosely after 5 rows. 

FURTHER ASSEMBLY: Set in sleeves. Sew side and sleeve seams inside 1 edge st. Sew on buttons.
