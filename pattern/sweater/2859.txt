Sizes: 
child’s, 8/10 - 12/14 years
(Women’s Small-Medium-Large)
Finished Measurements: 94-100 (106-114-120) cm 

Materials: DROPS ULL-FLAMÉ 
(alternative ESKIMO from Garnstudio)
550-600 (700-750-850) g in Color No. 01, Natural.
DROPS 6mm circular needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge.
Gauge: 12 sts x 16 rows in pattern = 10 x 10 cm . 

Rib Pattern: 
Right side: * Knit 1, purl 3*. Repeat from * - *. 
Wrong side: * Purl 3, knit 1*. Repeat from * - *. 

Body: Cast on 112-120 (128-136-144) sts knit in Rib Pattern. When the work measures 28-30 (32-33-34) cm , knit the next row as follows: bind off 1 (2) st(s) for armhole, knit 54-58 (60-64-68) sts for the front, bind off 2 (4) sts for armhole, knit 54-58 (60-64-68) sts for the back, bind off 1 (2) st(s) for armhole. Knit the rest of the front and back separately from this point.

Front: 54-58 (60-64-68) sts. Bind off for armholes every other row: 2 sts 1-1 (1-1-2) times, 1 st 3-3 (2-3-2) times. 44-48 (52-54-56) sts. When the work measures 43-47 (49-51-52) cm , put the center 6-8 (8-10-10) sts on a stitch holder for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 49-53 (56-58-59) cm .

Back: 54-58 (60-64-68) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front. 44-48 (52-54-56) sts. When the work measures 47-51 (54-56-57) cm , bind off the center 14-16 (16-18-18) sts for the neck. Bind off 2 sts on each side of the neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 49-53 (56-58-59) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 24-28 (32-32-32) sts on double pointed needles and join, being careful not to twist the sts. Place a marker at the join and knit Rib Pattern. When the work measures 5 cm , inc 1 st on each side of the marker 13-14 (13-14-14) times:
Size 8/10 years: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row. 
Sizes 12/14 + M + L: every 4th row. 
Size Small: alternately every 4th and 5th row. 
= 50-56 (58-60-60) sts. When the work measures 36-44 (45-44-44) cm , bind off 1 st each side of the marker and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 2-2 (3-3-2) sts 4-4 (4-5-6) times, 4-4 (5-5-5) sts 1 time. Bind off. The work measures approximately 44-52 (53-53-54) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approximately 48-56 sts (divisible by 4 sts), including sts from the stitch holder, around the neck on double pointed needles and knit in Pattern for 7 cm  continuing pattern from the body up into the neck. Bind off. Sew on the sleeves.
