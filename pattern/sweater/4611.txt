DROPS design: Pattern no ME-049-by
---------------------------------------------------------
Size:  1/3 - 6/9 - 12/18 months (2 - 3/4) years
Finished measurements:
Bust: 44-50-56 (62-68) cm
Full length: 18-20-22 (24-26) cm

Materials: DROPS MERINO EXTRA FINE from Garnstudio
100-100-100 (100-150) g colour no 08, light beige mix
A remnant colour no 01, off-white (for crochet borders) 

DROPS POINTED NEEDLES size 4.5 mm – or size needed to get 20 sts x 34 rows in moss st = 10 x 10 cm. 
DROPS CROCHET HOOK size 4 mm (for borders).
DROPS LIGHT WOODEN BUTTON no 503: 2 pcs.

MOSS ST: 
Row 1: * K1, P1 *, repeat from *-*. 
Row 2: K over P and P over K. 
Repeat row 2.
INCREASING TIP:
Make all inc in the outermost st by working 2 sts in the same st.
DECREASING TIP (applies to neckline):
Make all dec at beg of row by K/P 2 tog. 
---------------------------------------------------------
---------------------------------------------------------
BACK PIECE: 
Cast on 42-48-54 (60-66) sts (includes 1 edge st each side) on needle size 4.5 mm with light beige mix. Work MOSS ST – see above. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures 2-3-3 (4-4) cm inc 1 st each side – See INCREASING TIP. Repeat the inc each side when piece measures 5-6-7 (8-8) cm = 46-52-58 (64-70) sts. When piece measures 8-9-10 (11-12) cm cast off 4-4-4 (5-5) sts at the beg of the next 2 rows for armhole and then dec 1 st at the beg of every row a total of 4-4-5 (5-6) times each side – See DECREASING TIP = 30-36-40 (44-48) sts. When piece measures 16-18-20 (22-24) cm cast of the middle 14-16-18 (20-20) sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Dec 1 st on neckline on next row, work 1 row without dec and dec 1 st on neckline on next row = 6-8-9 (10-12) sts left on shoulder. Cast off in moss st when piece measures 18-20-22 (24-26) cm. Work the other shoulder in the same way. 

LEFT FRONT PIECE:
Cast on 15-17-19 (21-23) sts (includes 1 edge st at the side) on needle size 4.5 mm with light beige mix. 
READ ALL OF THIS SECTION BEFORE CONTINUING! 
Work moss st, AT THE SAME TIME on first row inc 1 st towards mid front – See INCREASING TIP! Repeat the inc on every row (i.e. from RS as well as WS) a total of 8-8-7 (5-1) times and then on every other row a total of 3-4-6 (9-14) times. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 2-3-3 (4-4) cm inc 1 st at the side, and repeat the inc when piece measures 5-6-7 (8-8) cm. When all inc are complete there are 28-31-34 (37-40) sts on needle. When piece measures 8-9-10 (11-12) cm cast off for armhole at the side as described for back piece. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 8-9-11 (12-14) cm dec 1 st towards mid front for neck – See DECREASING TIP! Repeat the dec on every other row a total of 14-15-16 (17-17) times = 6-8-9 (10-12) sts left on shoulder. Continue until piece measures 18-20-22 (24-26) cm and cast off in moss st.

RIGHT FRONT PIECE:
Cast on and work like left front piece, but mirrored. 

SLEEVE:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 28-30-34 (38-42) sts (includes 1 edge st each side) on needle size 4.5 mm with light beige mix. Work moss st, AT THE SAME TIME at the beg of the first 2 rows dec 1 st – See DECREASING TIP! Repeat the dec at the beg of every row until piece measures approx 4.5 -5.5 -6.5(7.5 -7.5) cm. Now cast off 2 sts at the beg of the next 2 rows and cast off remaining sts in moss st. 

ASSEMBLY: 
Sew shoulder seams. Sew side seams inside 1 edge st and set in sleeves – set in sleeves approx 2-3 cm from where armhole beg. Sew buttons on left front band. Use crochet border as buttonholes. 

CROCHET BORDER:
Crochet a border round the opening, including bottom edge, with crochet hook size 4 mm and off-white as follows: 1 dc in first st, * 3 ch, 1 tr in first ch, skip approx 1-1.5 cm, 1 dc in next st *, repeat from *-* and finish with 1 dc in first st
Crochet a border along sleeve edges as follows with crochet hook size 4 mm and off-white – beg where sleeve is sewn to back piece: 1 dc in first st, * 3 ch, skip approx 1-1.5cm, 1 dc in next st *, repeat from *-*.
