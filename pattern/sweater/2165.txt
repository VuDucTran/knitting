Sizes: 
child’s, 8/10 - 12/14 years 
(women’s, Small/Medium - Medium/Large - Xlarge).
Finished Measurements: 100-110 (114-120-126) cm .

Materials: DROPS KARISMA SUPERWASH from Garnstudio
400-450 (500-550-600) g in Col. 27, Dark Turquoise.
and: DROPS PELLIZA from Garnstudio
50-50 (100-100-100) g in Color No. 1, Corn Blue.

DROPS 3mm and 3.5mm needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.

Gauge: 22 sts x 30 rows on larger needles in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm . 

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter Stitch, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Pattern: See chart. The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette stitch. 

Front: Cast on 96-108 (112-116-120) sts (including 1 edge st for seam on each side) with dark turquoise Karisma on smaller needles and knit in garter stitch for 2 cm . Change to larger needles and stockinette stitch. At the same time, inc 1 st at each 8-8 (8-9-10) times for every 2-2.5 (2.5-2.5-2.5) cm . 112-124 (128-134-140) sts. 
When the work measures 6-8 (10-11-11) cm , knit the next row as follows: 25-31 (33-36-39) sts stockinette stitch, 62 sts Pattern, 25-31 (33-36-39) sts stockinette stitch. When the work measures 25-27 (29-31-32) cm , bind off for armholes every other row: 4 sts 1 time, 3 sts 1-1 (0-1-1) time, 2 sts 2-2 (2-2-3) times, 1 st 3-4 (4-3-3) times. 84-94 (104-106-108) sts. When the work measures 38-42 (46-48-50) cm , bind off the center 16-20 (24-26-26) sts for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (54-56-58) cm .

Back: Cast on and knit like the front, except do not knit Pattern. When the work measures 43-47 (52-54-56) cm , bind off the center 30-34 (38-40-40) sts for the neck. Bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (54-56-58) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 50-52 (52-56-56) sts on smaller needles with Pelliza, knit in garter stitch for 3 cm . Change to larger needles and Karisma. Knit stockinette stitch. When the work measures 5 cm , inc 1 st each side 19-22 (29-27-29) times:
Sizes 8/10 + 12/14 years: alternately every 4th and every 5th row. 
Sizes S/M + XL: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row. 
Size M/L: every 4th row. 
= 88-96 (110-110-114) sts 
When the work measures 36-42 (43-43-42) cm , bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 
Sizes 8/10 + 12/14 years: 4 sts 2 (2) times, 3 sts 1 (2) times, 2 sts 4 (2) times, 1 st 3 (3) times, 2 sts 4(3) times, 3 sts 1 (3) times. 
Sizes S/M + M/L + XL: 4 sts 3-2-2 times, 3 sts 4-3-3 times, 2 sts 1-6-8 times, 3 sts 5-4-3 times. 
Bind off. The work measures approximately 45-51 (51-52-52) cm . 

Assembly: Sew right shoulder seam. Pick up approximately 90-95 (105-105-110) sts on smaller needles with Pelliza, knit in garter stitch for 3 cm . Bind off loosely. Sew left shoulder seam and neck in one. Sew on the sleeves and sew the sleeve seam and side seam in one.
