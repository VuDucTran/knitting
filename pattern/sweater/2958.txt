Materials: DROPS Karisma Superwash from Garnstudio
300-350 (400-400-450) g main colour
50 g edge colour

Pointed needles size 3 and 5 mm. Small circular needle size 3 mm for neck.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 14 sts x 40 rows in English rib on needle size 5 mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: * K1, P1 *

ENGLISH RIB: Row 1: 1 edge st * K1 , 1 YO , slip 1 st as if to K * , 1 edge st. Row 2: 1 edge st * K tog YO and slipped st, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to P *,1 edge st. Row 3: 1 edge st * K tog YO and slipped st, 1 YO, slip 1 st as if to K * 1 edge st. Repeat row 2 and 3. 

FRONT PIECE: Cast on 72-80 (88-92-100) sts on needle size 3 mm with main colour and work 5 cm rib. Change to needle size 5 mm and work 1 row stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME adjust no of sts evenly to 54-58 (60-64-68) sts. Now continue in English rib. When piece measures 31-34 (37-39.5-40) cm cast off the 2 middle sts for V-neck and complete each side separately. Cast off 1 st on neckline a total of 12-12 (13-13-13) times on every 2-2 (2-3-4) row. Cast off remaining 14-16 (16-18-20) sts on shoulder when piece measures 40-43 (47-51-53) cm.

BACK PIECE: Cast on and work like front piece, but without V-neck. Continue until piece measures 40-43 (47-51-53) cm and cast off all sts. 

SLEEVE: Cast on 44-48 (48-52-52) sts on needle size 3 mm with edge colour and work 1 row rib. Change to main colour and continue in rib. When rib measures 5 cm change to needle size 5 mm and work 1 row stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME dec evenly to 31-33 (35-37-39) sts. Continue in English rib, AT THE SAME TIME inc 1 st each side a total of 9-10 (11-13-14) times on every 8-8 (10-8-10) row = 49-53 (57-63-67) sts. Cast off when sleeve measures 25-29 (34-38-41) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 86-86 (92-96-100) sts round the neck on needle size 3 mm with main colour. Work 3 cm rib, AT THE SAME TIME dec mid front on every row as follows: Work row to the middle 3 sts – make sure the middle st is a K st. Now slip the middle st on a cable needle and K tog the 1st and 3rd st, psso. When rib measures 3 cm work 1 row rib with edge colour and cast off in edge colour.  Sew sleeve and side seams in outer loops of sts.
