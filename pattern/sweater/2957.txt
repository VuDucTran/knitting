= 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: * K1, P1 *

STRIPE PATTERN: The entire stripe pattern is worked in stocking st. 1 stripe = 3-3 (3-4-4) rows. Work stripes in the following order: 1 violet zigzag stripe on black base colour, 1 green stripe, 1 blue zigzag stripe on yellow base colour, 1 green zigzag stripe on blue base colour – shift this zigzag stripe 1/2 time (4 sts) to the right so that the points sit over each other, then 5-5 (7-8-8) rows green , 1 violet zigzag stripe on green base colour – work this  zigzag stripe over the previous zigzag (i.e. this zigzag will also shift 4 sts to the right of the first zigzag) -, 1 yellow stripe. 
1 square = 1 st x 1 row

BODY PIECE: Cast on 120-132 (144-156-168) sts on circular needle size 3 mm with black and work 5 cm rib. Change to circular needle size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly to 124-136 (140-152-160) sts on first round. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 13-19 (22-24-26) cm continue in stripe pattern – see above and the diagram, beg by arrow at the side, work pattern on front piece, beg by arrow again and work pattern on back piece. When piece measures 20-25 (27-29-30) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

FRONT PIECE: 62-68 (70-76-80) sts. Continue in pattern. After the last yellow stripe work 3-3 (3-4-4) rows in black. Cast off. Piece measures 37-43 (47-51-53) cm. 

BACK PIECE: Like front piece. 

SLEEVE: Cast on 34-36 (38-38-40) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with black and work 5 cm rib. Change to double pointed needles size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly to 34-38 (42-46-50) sts on first round. Now inc 2 sts mid under sleeve a total of 10-10 (11-12-12) times on every 3-3 (4-4-5) round = 54-58 (64-70-74) sts. AT THE SAME TIME when sleeve measures 18-21 (26-30-33) cm work 1 violet zigzag stripe with black base colour and then 1 green stripe. Cast off. Sleeve measures 24-27 (32-36-39) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 68-72 (74-76-78) sts round neck on double pointed needles size 3 mm with black, work 2 cm rib, cast off. Set in sleeves.
