Knitting tip: Knit poncho in two pieces and sew tog sides. Knit each part from neck down. When inc on each side of the middle st the point is automatically created.

Front piece: Read knitting tip! Cast on 25-27-29 (31-33-35) sts (incl 1 edge st each side) on needle size 8 mm with Puddel, insert marking thread in mid st. Knit stocking st – at the same time inc 1 st on each side of mid st on every other row until final measurement. When piece measures 16-18-20 (22-24-26) cm cast off 1 st each side: 3-4-4 (5-5-4) times every 8-6.5-7 (6-6-7.5) cm. After last cast off continue inc until piece measures 41-45-48 (53-55-57) cm.
