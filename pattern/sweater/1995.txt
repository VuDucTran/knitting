Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: Ardesia: 10 x 10 cm = 12 sts x 16 rows in pattern on needle size 6 mm.

RIB: * K1, P1 *

PATTERN: The entire pattern is worked in stocking st. Work all red sts in double thread

BODY PIECE: Cast on 94-98 (104-116-120) sts on circular needle size 4 mm with grey and work 5 cm rib. Change to circular needle size 6 mm and continue in M.1, AT THE SAME TIME inc 2-4 (4-4-6) sts evenly on first row = 96-102 (108-120-126) sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm divide the piece in two for armholes and complete front and back pieces separately.

BACK PIECE: = 48-51 (54-60-63) sts. Continue in pattern. When piece measures 34-37 (41-43-45) cm work M.2. AT THE SAME TIME when 2 rows remain of M.2 cast off the middle 20-21 (20-22-23) sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off 1 st on neckline on next row. After M.2 K 1 row with off-white and cast off. Piece measures approx 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

FRONT PIECE: = 48-51 (54-60-63) sts. Work like back piece, but cast off the middle 14-15 (14-16-17) sts for neck when piece measures 34-37 (41-43-45) cm (when working M.2), and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off to shape the neckline at the beg of every row from mid front: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time. After M.2 K 1 row with off-white and cast off. Piece measures approx 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

SLEEVE: Cast on 24-24 (30-30-32) sts on double pointed needles size 4 mm with grey and work 4 cm rib. Change to double pointed needles size 6 mm and continue in M.1. AT THE SAME TIME inc 2 sts mid under sleeve 11-12 (11-11-13) on every 3rd row for size 3/4+5/6 years and on every 4th row for size 7/8+9/10+11/12 years = 46-48 (52-52-58) sts. Incorporate inc sts in pattern as you go along. When 6 cm remain (adjust to pattern) work M.2. After M.2 piece measures approx 26-30 (34-36-40) cm, K 1 round in off-white and cast off. 

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 74-80 sts round the neck on circular needle size 4 mm with grey and work rib as follows: 3 cm grey, 1 cm white, cast off with K over K and P over P. Set in sleeves in outer loops of sts to avoid a chunky seam.
