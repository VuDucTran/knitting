Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4 mm in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st. See diagram. Change colour for each new row with triangles as follows: purple, blue, light purple. If changing to a darker colour, knit 6 rows white instead of 4 in between rows with triangles.   

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needles. You can chose to knit the entire piece in the round and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on circular needle size 2.5 mm with white and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in M.II, at the same time inc 30-38 (48-48-48) sts evenly on round = 160-176 (192-200-208) sts. Remember the knitting tension. After M.II knit M.I – see explanation above. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back piece separately) (if you prefer to cut for armhole, inc 4 sts each side (to be cut for armholes afterwards and not included in pattern).  

Front piece: = 80-88 (86-100-104) sts. When piece measures 37-40 (44-46-48) cm cast off the middle 28-28 (30-32-32) sts for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Cast of remaining sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Back piece: = 80-88 (96-100-104) sts. When piece measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm cast off the middle 34-34 (36-38-38) sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row. Cast of remaining sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 30-32 (34-36-38) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with white and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in M.II, at the same time inc 11-9 (11-13-13) sts evenly on round = 41-41 (45-49-51) sts.  Now inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 18-20 (22-20-23) times as follows:
Size 3/4, 5/6, 7/8 years: on every 3rd round 
Size 9/10 years: on every 4th round
Size 11/12 years: on every *4th, 4th and 3rd * round alternately
= 77-81 (89-89-97 sts. After M.II, continue in M.I. When piece measures 16-19 (22-26-30) cm knit stripes as follows: 8 rounds purple, 1 round white, 8 rounds blue, 1 round white, 8 rounds light purple. Cast off, sleeve measures 26-29 (32-36-40) cm. If you chose to cut for armholes, knit 5 rounds in reverse stocking st = edge for sewing on sleeve to body piece. Cast off and knit the other sleeve. 

Assembly: see cut for armhole. Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 76-90 sts with white on needle size 2.5 mm round the neck, knit 6 rows stocking st, P 1 round = folding edge, 5 rows stocking st. Fold neck double towards WS and fasten. Set in sleeves.
Cut for armhole: Insert a marking thread in the middle of the inc sts for armhole each side. Make 2 seams each side on your sewing machine, the first seam ½ st from the MT and the other one ½ st from the first one. Cut the piece between these. Sew shoulder seams. Set in sleeves from the RS as follows: sew in the last row before the edge in reverse stocking sts on sleeves to the first row after the seam in the sts for cutting on body piece. Turn the jumper inside out and sew the piece in stocking sts over the cutting edge on body piece.
