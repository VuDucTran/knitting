SLEEVELESS JUMPER:
Size: 3/4 – 5/6 – 7/8 (9/19.11/12-13/14) years.

Materials: DROPS Puddel from Garnstudio
150-150-150 (200-200-200)g colour no. 01, off-white

DROPS pointed needles size 7 mm

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: 10 sts x 14 rows on needles size 7 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows.
Binding off tips: Bind off inside 2 garter sts from the right side.
Bind off after 2 garter sts: Slip a st as if to knit, K1, psso
Bind off before 2 garter sts: K2 tog.

Front piece: 
Cast on 27-30-33 (36-39-42) sts ( inclusive of 1 edge st each side) with Puddel on needles size 7 mm. Knit 4 rows of garter sts and continue in stocking sts. Remember the knitting tension! Inc 1 st each side when the piece measures 10 and 18 cm = 31-34-37 (40-43-46) sts. When the piece measures 25-26-27 (28-29-30) cm purl 4 sts each side from the right side (continue in stocking sts over the remaining sts). On the next row bind off 2 sts each side for armhole and dec 1 st each side on every other row inside the 2 garter sts – see binding off tips above - 0-1-2 (3-4-5) times  = 27-28-29 (30-31-32) sts. When the piece measures 31-33-35 (36-38-40) cm purl the middle 7-8-7 (8-7-8) sts from the right side. On the next row bind off the middle 3-4-3 (4-3-4) sts for neck. Continue with 2 sts in garter sts at the neck side – at the same time bind off 1 st each side on every other row – see binding off tips: 3-3-4 (4-4-4) times = 9-9-9 (9-10-10) sts left for each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 39-41-43 (45-47-49) cm.
Back piece: Cast on and knit as the front piece. Bind off for the armholes as done for the front piece and continue to knit until the piece measures 37-39-41 (43-45-47) cm. Purl 1 row (seen from the right side) over the centre 13-14-15 (16-15-16) sts. On next row bind off the middle 9-10-11 (12-11-12) sts for neck = 9-9-9 (9-10-10) sts left for each shoulder. Continue with 2 sts in garter sts at the neck side and bind off when the piece measures 39-41-43 (45-47-49) cm.

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seams and the side seams inside the edge sts. 


HAT:
Size: 3/5 – 6/9 – 10/14 years.

Materials: DROPS Alpaca from Garnstudio
50-50-50 g colour no. 0100, off-white

DROPS crochet hook size 3mm

Crochet Tension: 14 tr with a ch in between each tr x 12 rows on hook size 3mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Crochet info: Replace the first tr on each round with 3 ch, and finish each round with a sl st in the 3rd ch from beginning of round. 
Read crochet info!

Crochet 6 ch on hook size 3 mm and make a loop with a sl st.
1st round: Crochet 10 tr around the loop
2nd round: Crochet 2 tr in each tr from previous round = 20 tr
3rd round: Crochet 1 tr in each tr from previous round with a ch in between each = 40 sts
4th round: Crochet 1 tr in each ch from previous round with a ch in between each, but in every other ch crochet an extra ‘tr + 1 ch’ = 60 sts
5th round: Crochet 1 tr in each ch from previous round with a ch in between each, but in every 3rd ch crochet an extra ‘tr + 1 ch’ = 80 sts
6th round: Crochet as 5th round, but crochet an extra ‘tr + 1ch’ in every 4th. ch = 100 sts
7th round:  Crochet as 6th round, but crochet an extra ‘tr + 1ch’ in every 5th. ch = 120 sts

Finish the increases for size 3/5 years here.

Size 6/9 years;
8th round: Crochet as 7th round, but crochet an extra ‘tr + 1ch’ in every 12th. ch = 130 sts
Size 10/14 years:
8th round: Crochet as 7th round, but crochet an extra ‘tr + 1ch’ in every 6th. ch = 140 sts

All sizes:
Continue to crochet 1 tr and 1 ch in each ch from previous round without any increases until the hat measures 15-17-19 cm.
Crochet 1 round of dc around the hat. Finish as follows:* 3 ch, 1 dc in the 3rd ch from the hook ( = 1 picot), skip 1 dc from previous round and crochet 1 sl st in next dc*, repeat from *-* all around the hat. Cut the thread and sew the thread.
