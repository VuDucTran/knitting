Rib: * K1, P1 *

PATTERN: The entire pattern is worked in stocking st. Remember the knitting tension!
1 rute = 1 square, 1 st x 1 row
1 rapport = 1 pattern repeat, 53 rows
Luse mønster = dotted pattern 
Mønster øverst på erme = pattern for top of sleeve
Start str = beg size  

BODY PIECE: Cast on 144-160 (176-184) sts on circular needle size 3 mm with off-white and work 4 cm rib.  Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in dotted pattern (see diagram), AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly on first round to 160-172 (180-196) sts. When piece measures 17-21 (25-29) cm work 1 vertical pattern repeat (see diagram = 53 rows) – insert a marker each side, beg by start arrow and work pattern on front piece, beg by arrow again and work pattern on back piece. When the lama beg (row 9 in pattern repeat) work 3-3 (4-4) turquoise sts each side before beg by arrow for size 5/6 and 9/10 years. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 22-25 (27-29) cm divide piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

BACK PIECE: Continue pattern repeat. After the last row in pattern piece measures approx 39-43 (47-51) cm, cast off.

FRONT PIECE: Like back piece. 

SLEEVE: Cast on 42-46 (50-54) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with off- white and work 4 cm rib. Change to double pointed needles size 4 mm and continue in stocking, AT THE SAME TIME inc 1 st on first round = 43-47 (51-55) sts. Now work 2 rounds yellow and continue in dotted pattern (see diagram). AT THE SAME TIME inc 2 sts mid under sleeve 14-15 (17-19) times on every 3-4 (4-4) round = 71-77 (85-93) sts. When sleeve measures 20-23 (29-33) cm work 4 rounds pattern for top of sleeve (see diagram) with red. Cast off when sleeve measures 23-27 (32-36) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams – neck = approx 18-18 (19-19) cm. Pick up approx. 90-90 (94-94) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with off-white round the neck, work 2 cm rib and cast of loosely. Set in sleeves. Lightly steam iron jumper.
