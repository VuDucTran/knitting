Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 9 sts x 13 rows on larger needles with 2 strands Alpaca + 1 strand Vienna in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.

Front: Cast on 32-34-36-39-42 sts on smaller needles with 3 strands of yarn (1 strand Vienna + 1 strand Alpaca in each color). Knit 4 rows garter st. Change to larger needles and stockinette st. When the piece measures 10 cm  inc 1 st at each side = 34-36-38-41-44 sts. When the piece measures 24-26-28-29-30 cm bind off for armhole at each side every other row: 2 sts 1 time, then 1 st 1-1-2-3-2 times = 28-30-30-31-36 sts. When the piece measures 35-37-40-41-43 cm  bind off the center 6-6-6-7-8 sts for the neck. Then bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1 time = 8-9-9-9-11 sts remain on each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 39-42-45-47-49 cm .

Back: Cast on and knit the same as the front. Bind off for armhole as on front and when the piece measures 37-40-43-45-47 cm, bind off the center 10-10-10-11-12 sts for the neck. On the next row dec 1 st at each neck edge = 8-9-9-9-11 sts remain on each shoulder. Bind off when the piece measures 39-42-45-47-49 cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 18-19-20-21-22 sts on smaller needles with 3 strands (1 strand Vienna + 1 strand Alpaca in each color). Knit 4 rows garter st. Change to larger needles and stockinette st. When the piece measures 7 cm  inc 1 st at each side every 5-6-5.5-6-6.5 cm  5-5-6-6-6 times = 28-29-32-33-34 sts.
When sleeve measures 29-33-37-40-42 cm bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 3 sts 1 time, then 2 sts at each side until the piece measures 34-38-43-47-48 cm . Bind off the remaining sts.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams.

Collar: Pick up approx. 32 to 44 sts (divisible by 4) around the neck (begin at center front) on smaller circular needles with 3 strands (1 strand Vienna + 1 strand Alpaca in each color). Knit as follows (seen from the right side): P 1 row, K 1 row. Knit the next row as follows: K 1 (edge st knit in garter st throughout), * K 2, P 2 *, repeat from * - *, finish with K 2 and K 1 (edge st knit in garter st throughout).
