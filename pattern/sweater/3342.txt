Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 18 sts x 23 rows on needles size 4.5 mm with Paris in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm

Pattern: See diagram M.1. Diagrams are seen from RS. 

Stripes: Stripes after M.1. 
Knit as follows:
Yoke: See start of diagram M.1.
Back and front pieces: Continue with M.1. After M.1 repeat M.1b until finished measurement.
Sleeve: Knit M.1 like back and front pieces until * in diagram. Knit M.1c 1 time in height. Continue with M.1 from * in diagram. After M.1 repeat M.1b until finished measurement.

Knitting tip (applies to neck): To avoid holes when turning piece slip 1st st as if to knit when knitting back and tighten yarn slightly.

Increasing tip (applies to raglan):
Inc as follows from RS: Start 2 sts from marking threads: Yo, K4 (marking thread is placed in middle between these sts), yo. 
On next row K into back of yo (i.e. knit into back of sts in stead of into front of sts) to avoid creating hole when increasing.

YOKE
Knit piece in round on circular needle. Knit from top down so length of jersey is easily adjust according to requirement.
Cast on 76-80-84-88-88 sts on double pointed needles size 4.5 mm with petrol. Knit Rib K2 / P2 until piece measures 3 cm. Insert 1 marking thread (MT) at beginning of round (= right side). Continue with a rise on back of neck (to ensure neck will fit neatly). Continue in Rib K2/ P2 and knit back and forth as follows: Knit 38-40-42-44-44 sts, turn piece – read Knitting tip – and knit back to MT. Turn piece. Knit 43-45-47-49-49 sts. Turn piece. Knit 48-50-52-54-54 sts back. Turn piece. Knit 56-58-60-62-62 sts. Turn piece. Knit 64-66-68-70-70 sts back. Turn piece. Knit 1 round over all sts and continue to MT. Continue all the way around. Knit 1 round stocking sts with petrol at the same time inc 12-10-8-12-12 sts evenly = 88-90-92-100-100 sts. Continuing knit 8 sts from MT (move round beginning). Insert 4 new MT into piece as follows:
1st MT in beginning of round (= 1st transition between right arm and back piece ), 2nd MT  after 28-29-30-34-34 sts (= back piece), 3rd MT  after 44-45-46-50-50 sts (= left sleeve), 4th MT after 72-74-76-84-84 sts (= front piece). Sts between 4th and 1st marking thread = right sleeve. Continuing knit stocking sts and stripes after M.1- see explanation above – until completed measurement. At the same time inc for raglan: Inc 1 sts on each side of 4 marking threads (= 8 inc) – read  Increasing tips. Inc on each round 2-4-6-3-7 times and on every other round 15-15-15-18-17 times.  
After all inc for raglan there are 224-242-260-268-292 sts on needle. On next round slip 50-54-58-58-64 sts on each side (between 2nd and 3rd MT  and between 4th and 1st MT) on 1 thread or stitch holder for sleeves. At the same time cast on 8 new sts on each side under sts on thread or stitch holder = 140-150-160-168-180 sts on needle. 

Back and front pieces: Continue in stocking sts and stripes after M.1 – see explanation above. On 1st round insert 1 MT on each side of piece under arm (i.e. 4 new sts on each side of MT). At the same time when piece measures 4 cm from MT on side dec 1 sts on each side of MT on every 5 -6 -5.5 -6 -6 cm 4-4-5-5-5 times in total = 124-134-140-148-160 sts. Continue until piece measures 41-44-49-51-53 cm from shoulder. Finish off M.1. If extra length is required continue knitting stripes from M.1 – see explanation above. 
Continue in Rib K2 / P2 with light grey. NOTE: Size 7/8 years: Before starting Rib knit 1 round stocking st and dec 2 sts evenly = 132 sts.) Continue like this until Rib measures approx 4 cm. Cast off loosely K over K and P over P. Piece measures approx 45-48-53-55-57 cm from shoulder.

Sleeve: Slip sts from threads or stitch holder (= 50-54-58-58-64 sts) left on double pointed needles size 4.5 mm. Continue in stocking sts and stripes from M.1 – see explanation above. At the same time on 1st row cast on 8 new sts under sleeve = 58-62-66-66-72 sts. Insert 1 MT mid under sleeve (i.e. 4 new sts on each side of MT). When piece measures  4-4-3-3-3 cm from MT mid under sleeve dec 1 sts on each side of MT on every 3.5 -3 -3 -4 -3.5 cm 7-9-11-9-12 times in total = 44-44-44-48-48 sts. Continue knitting until piece measures 29-33-36-39-43 cm from MT mid under sleeve, change to light grey. Continue with Rib K2/ P2 for 6 cm, cast off loosely K over K and P over P.

Assembly: Sew openings under arms.
