Sizes: 3/4 - 5/6 (7/8 - 89/10 - 11/12) years

Finished measurements: 78-84 (90-96-102) cm


Materials: DROPS KARISMA from Garnstudio 
100% pure new wool
250-300 (300-350-350) g col. 01, natural white
50-50 (100-100-100) g col. 18, red
50-50 (50-100-100) g col. 41, blue
50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 10, yellow
50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 09, green

DROPS 2.5 mm  and 4 mm  straight and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 20 sts x 26 rows on larger needles in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm 

Rib: * K 1, P 1 *

"Bobble": K 3 sts into 1 st, knit 3 rows stockinette st over these sts, on the 4th row K 3 tog to make 1 st. 

Pattern: See charts below (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette stitch (except for bobbles). 


Body: Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on smaller circular needles with natural; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 5 cm  rib. Change to larger circular needles and Pattern 1 once, increasing 26-30 (36-40-44) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 156-168 (180-192-204) sts. Then knit Pattern 2 until the work measures 19-22 (26-28-30) cm  (adjust for complete pattern). Knit Pattern 1 once and at the same time, when the work measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm , divide the work into front and back and knit the rest separately.

Front: = 78-84 (90-96-102) sts. After Pattern 1 knit Pattern 3 with natural. When 7 rows of Pattern 3 remain, bind off the center 30-30 (32-34-34) sts for the neck, then bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time. Bind off when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Back: = 78-84 (90-96-102). Knit Pattern as on the front, until the work measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm  then bind off the center 34-34 (36-38-38) sts for the neck. Bind off 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 32-34 (34-36-38) sts on smaller double pointed needles with natural; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4-4 (5-5-5) cm  rib. Change to larger double pointed needles and knit Pattern 1 one time, centering the pattern on the sleeve, and increasing 8-6 (10-12-12) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 40-40 (44-48-50) sts. After Pattern 1 knit Pattern 2 until the work measures approx. 24-27 (31-34-38) cm  (adjust for complete pattern). 
At the same time, after the rib, inc 1 st each side of marker 18-20 (22-20-23) times:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: every 3rd row
Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row 
= 76-80 (88-88-96) sts. After Pattern 2 is complete knit the first 8 rows of Pattern 1 and then knit the rest with yellow until the work measures 27-30 (34-37-41) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 80-84 (86-88-92) sts around the neck on smaller double pointed needles and knit 3 cm  to 5 cm  rib, bind off. Sew the sleeves to the body.
