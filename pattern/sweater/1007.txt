Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 23 sts x 30 rows in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm .

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter st, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Front: Cast on 76 (84) sts with Pelliza. Knit 4 rows garter st, change to stockinette st, keeping the edge st at each side in garter st. After the garter st border, inc 1 st at each side 4 (5) times every 11 (12) rows = 84 (94) sts. When the work measures 19 (25) cm  knit 4 rows garter st over the 5 (5) sts at each side, then bind off 3 (3) sts for armhole at each side. Dec 1 st at each side for armhole inside the 2 edge sts (which are knit in garter st throughout) every other row 3 (5) times = 72 (78) sts. When the work measures 29 (36) cm  knit 4 rows garter st over the center 20 (22) sts, then bind off the center 16 (18) sts for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge inside the 2 edge sts (which are knit in garter st throughout) every row 6 times, then every other row 3 times. Bind off when the work measures 36 (44) cm .

Back: Cast on and knit as on the front through armhole shaping. When the work measures 34 (42) cm , knit 4 rows garter st over the center 34 (36) sts, then bind off the center 30 (32) sts for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge inside the 2 edge sts (which are knit in garter st throughout) every row 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 36 (44) cm . 

Sleeve: Cast on 89 (97) sts on double pointed needles with a double strand of Pelliza; join and place a marker at the join. Change to a single strand and knit 4 rows garter st, then stockinette st. When the work measures 5 cm , 10 cm  and 15 cm , dec 15 (17) sts evenly distributed on the row = 44 (46) sts. After the last decrease row change to Cotton Viscose and inc 1 st each side of marker 14 (17) times:
Size 8/10 years: alternately every 4th and 5th row
Size 12/14: every 6th row 
= 72 (80) sts. When the work measures 39 (51) cm  bind off 2 sts each side of marker, and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 2 sts 10 (8) times, 3 sts 1 (3) times, bind off. The work measures approx. 46 (58) cm .

Assembly: Sew the shoulder- and side seams, sew in the sleeves.
