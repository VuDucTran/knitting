Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st. 

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needles and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on circular needle size 2.5mm with black and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in M.1, at the same time inc 30-30 (40-40-48)  sts evenly on first round = 160-168 (184-192-208) sts. When piece measures 19-22 (26-25-27) (adjust to pattern) continue in M.2 and then complete piece in M.1 – beg by arrow in diagram and knit pattern on front piece, beg by arrow again and knit pattern on back piece. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm inc 5 sts each side for armhole (to be cut for armholes afterwards and not included in pattern but knitted in stocking st) (if you prefer not to cut for armhole, divide the piece in two and complete back and front piece separately).  When piece measures 37-40 (44-46-48) cm cast off 28-28 (30-32-32) sts mid front for neck and complete each side separately. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row:  2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times. At the same time, when piece measures 38-41 (45-47-49) cm cast off 34-34 (36-38-38) sts mid back for neck and complete each side separately. Dec 1 st towards neckline on next row.  Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 30-32 (34-36-38) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with black and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in M.1 – make sure to place a pattern repeat mid upper arm. At the same time inc 10-8 (10-12-12) sts evenly on first round = 40-40 (44-48-50) sts. Now inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 18-20 (22-20-23) times as follows:
Size 3/4 +5/6 + 7/8 years: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round 
= 76-80 (88-88-96) sts. At the same time, when sleeve measures 23-27 (30-34-37) cm knit M.3 and then 1 round black. Piece measures approx 26-30 (33-37-40) cm. Now knit 2 cm in reverse stocking st = edge for sewing on sleeve to body piece. Cast off and knit the other sleeve. 


Assembly: Insert a marking thread in the middle of the inc sts for armhole each side. Make 2 seams each side on your sewing machine, the first seam ½ st from the MT and the other one ½ st from the first one. Cut the piece between these. Sew shoulder seams.
Set in sleeves from the RS as follows: sew in the last row before the edge in reverse stocking sts on sleeves to the first row after the seam in the sts for cutting on body piece. Turn the jumper inside out and sew the piece in stocking sts over the cutting edge on body piece. 
Neckline:  Pick up approx 80-90 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with black and knit 3 cm Rib, cast off.
