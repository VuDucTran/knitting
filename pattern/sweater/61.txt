Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: Remember needle size is only a guide. 10 stitches x 12 rows with needle size 9 mm and stocking stitch = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: * K2, P1 *, repeat from *-*.
---------------------------------------------------------

Top: The top is worked in 1 piece, back and forth on circular needle. Loosely cast on 62-68-74-74-80 stitches (incl. 1 edge stitch in each side) with circular needle size 9 mm and 1 strand Vienna + 2 strands Glitter. Purl 1 row from the wrong side and 2 rows rib with 1 edge stitch in each side. Then continue with stocking stitch. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 21-23-25-26-27 cm, work 2 rows rib with 1 edge stitch in each side. Cast off in rib.

Assembly: Sew together mid back inside the 1 edge stitch.

Shoulder straps: Cast on 4 stitches with circular needle size 9 mm and 1 strand Vienna + 2 strands Glitter. Knit 1 row, * push the stitches to right end of needle without turning piece, tighten strand at back and knit 1 row back from the same side as the previous row *, repeat from *-*. In this way, you get a round strap without purling. NOTE: So the strap is neat, you need to tighten the strand well at the back on each row. Cast off when strap measures approx. 28-30-32-34-36 cm (incl. 1 cm seam on each side). Work 1 more strap and fasten to back of rib with approx. 15-15-16-17-17 cm between front and back.

Crochet flower: Work 4 chain stitches with hook size 3 mm and Safran and form them into a ring with 1 slip stitch.

Round 1: 6 double crochets around ring, finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet.
Round 2: 1 double crochet in first double crochet, * 3 chain stitches, 1 double crochet in next stitch *, repeat from *-* a total of 5 times and finish with 3 chain stitches and 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round = 6 chain-spaces.
Round 3: 1 chain stitch, then work as follows in each chain-space: 1 double crochet, 1 chain stitch, 3 treble crochets, 1 chain stitch and 1 double crochet, finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round.
Round 4: Turn piece, * work 1 double crochet in double crochet from round 2 (work at back of petals around stitch from previous round, tighten strand), 5 chain stitches *, repeat from *-* a total of 6 times and finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round.
Round 5: 1 chain stitch, then work as follows in each chain-space: 1 double crochet, 1 chain stitch, 5 treble crochets, 1 chain stitch and 1 double crochet, finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round.
Round 6: Turn piece, * work 1 double crochet in double crochet from round 2 (work at back of the 2 petals around stitch from previous round, tighten strand), 7 chain stitches *, repeat from *-* a total of 6 times and finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round.
Round 7: 1 chain stitch, then work as follows in each chain-space: 1 double crochet, 1 chain stitch, 1 treble crochet, 5 double treble crochets, 1 treble crochet, 1 chain stitch and 1 double crochet, finish with 1 slip stitch in first double crochet at beginning of round.
