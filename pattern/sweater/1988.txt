DROPS circular needle and double pointed needle size 3.5 and 5 mm 

Knitting tension: 17 sts x 24 rows on needles size 5 mm in moss st = 10 x 10 cm.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comRib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Double moss st: 
row 1: K1, P1, 
row 2 + 4: K over K and P over P, 
row 3: P1, K1. 

Knitting tips: knit the first and last round of each moss st section in single moss st to make the transition to pattern even.  

Pattern: 
Body piece: 2-2 (3-3-3) cm moss st, M.2, 4-7 (9-10-11) cm moss st, M.1, complete piece in moss st. 
Sleeve: 3-4 (6-1-2) cm moss st, M.2, 5-8 (10-5-7) cm moss st, size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8 years: M.2, size 9/10 + 11/12 years: M.1 until finished measurements = 26-30 (34-37-40) cm.



JUMPER: 

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needle.  Cast on 110-118 (126-134-140) sts on circular needle size 3.5mm with off-white and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 5 mm and continue in pattern – see explanation above - at the same time inc 26-26 (26-34-36) sts evenly on first round = 136-144 (152-168-176) sts. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

Back piece:  = 68-72 (76-84-88) sts. Continue in pattern. When piece measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm cast off the middle 28-28 (30-32-32) sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Dec to shape the neckline: 1 st 1 time. Cast off remaining 19-21 (22-25-27) sts on shoulder when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Front piece: = 68-72 (76-84-88) sts. Continue as described for back piece until piece measures 37-40 (44-46-48) cm. Now cast off the middle 24-24 (26-28-28) sts for neck and complete each side separately. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1 time. Cast off remaining 19-21 (22-25-27) sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.  

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 28-28 (30-30-32) sts on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm with off-white. Knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in pattern – see explanation above and make sure to place a pattern repeat mid upper arm - at the same time inc 6-6 (8-10-10) sts evenly on first round = 34-34 (38-40-42) sts. At the same time, inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 15-17 (18-17-20) times as follows:
Size 3/4: on every 3rd round
Size 5/6 + 7/8 years: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round 
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. Cast off when piece measures 26-30 (34-37-40) cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 78-82 sts round the neck on needle size 3.5 mm with off-white and knit 6 cm Rib, cast off. Fold Rib double towards WS and fasten. Set in sleeves.


SCARF:

Materials: DROPS “Karisma Superwash”, from Garnstudio
200 g colour no 001, off-white

DROPS pointed needles size 4 mm

Knitting tension: 25 sts on needle size 4 mm in pattern = width 10 cm 

Pattern: 
Row 1: K1, P1, K1, *K3, P1*, repeat from *-* and finish with K1, P1, K1
Row 2: P1, K1, P1, *K2, P1, K1*, repeat from *-* and finish with P1, K1, P1
Repeat row 1 and 2.

Cast on 46 sts on needle size 4 mm and knit 2 rows Rib, K1, P1. Continue in pattern until piece measures 150 cm and finish with 2 rows Rib, K1.P1. Cast off.
