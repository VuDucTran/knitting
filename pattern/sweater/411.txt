Cast off the middle 20-22-24-24-26 sts for neck and dec 1 st towards neckline on next row = 10-11-13-16-16 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 36-41-45-48-53 cm.

When piece measures 24-27-31-34-38 cm cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 3 sts 1-1-1-1-1 time, 2 sts 1-2-2-3-3 times, 1 st 3-2-1-1-2 times and 2 sts each side until piece measures 30-33-37-40-45 cm. MITTENS:

Size: 1/2 – 3/5 – 6/8 years

Materials: Garnstudio’s Vienna
50 g for all sizes, colour no 27, red or no 11, corn blue
and use:
Garnstudio’s Baby-ull
50 for all sizes, colour no 21, burgundy or no 30, blue mix

DROPS pointed needles size 4.5 mm.

Knitting Tension: 16 sts x 28 rows on needle size 4.5 mm in garter sts with 1 thread Vienna and 1 thread Baby-ull = 10 x 10 cm
Garter sts: Knit all rows.

The mitten is knitted back and forth on needles lengthwise. Cast on 24-26-29 sts on needles size 4.5 mm with 1 thread Vienna and 1 thread Baby-ull (double thread) and knit garter sts. When piece measures 3-3-4 cm dec 4 sts evenly on row = 20-22-25 sts. When piece measures 8-9-9 cm inc 1 st each side for thumb on every other row a total of 2-2-2 times and on every 4th row a total of 2-3-4 times = 28=32=37 sts. When piece measures 11-13-14 cm put the outermost 4-5-6 sts each side on a thread. Insert a marking thread and measures piece from this point onwards. Continue in garter sts. When piece measures 5-6-6 cm from MT dec 5-5-6 sts evenly on row = 15-17-19 sts. 
Knit 1 cm and dec 5-5-3 sts evenly = 10-12-16 sts. Knit 1 cm and continue as follows:
The 2 smaller sizes: Cast off remaining sts.
The bigger size: Dec 3 sts evenly = 13 sts. Knit 1 cm and cast off.
The whole mitten measures approx 18-21-23 cm. 
Thumb: Fold the mitten double and put the 4-5-6 sts from threads each side on the same needle size 4.5 mm = 8-10-12 sts. Knit garter sts, at the same time on the first row cast on 1 new st each side = 10-12-14 sts. When thumb measures 2-2-3 cm knit all sts tog 2 by 2. Cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. Sew tight.  

Assembly: Sew tog mitten with Vienna in the outer loop of the outermost sts. Beg at the top of the thumb and work towards the bottom and back up to the top. Then sew the seam from the thumb to the top of mitten.

NECK WARMER:
size: 1/2 – 3/5 – 6/8 years

Materials: Garnstudio’s Vienna
50 g for all sizes, colour no 27, red or no 11, corn blue
and use:
Garnstudio’s Baby-ull
50 for all sizes, colour no 21, burgundy or no 30, blue mix

DROPS pointed needles size 4.5

Knitting Tension: 16 sts x 20 rows on needle size 4.5 mm in stocking sts with 1 thread Vienna and 1 thread Baby-ull = 10 x 10 cm
Garter sts: Knit all rows.

Cast on 26-28-30 sts on needle size 4.5 mm with 1 thread Vienna and 1 thread Baby-ull and knit 4 rows garter sts. Continue in stocking sts with 3 sts each side in garter sts. At the same time on the first row in stocking sts inc 1 st inside 3 garter sts each side and repeat this inc on every other row a total of 3 times = 32-34-36 sts – knit the inc sts in stocking sts. When piece measures 7-8-10 cm cast off the middle 12-14-16 sts for neck and complete each shoulder piece separately. 

Right shoulder piece: = 10 sts Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 1 times = 7 sts. When piece measures 11-12-14 cm insert a marking thread (= mid shoulder) and measure piece from this point onwards. On next row inc 1 st towards neckline = 8 sts. Put sts on a thread. 

Left shoulder piece: = 10 sts. Knit as right piece. 

Back piece: Put both shoulder pieces on the same needle and cast on 16-18-20 sts between pieces = 32-34-36 sts. Continue in stocking sts with 3 sts each side in garter sts. When piece measures 6-7-9 cm (from MT) dec 1 st each side inside 3 garter sts on every other row (K2 tog) = 26-28-30 sts. Knit 4 rows garter sts over all sts and cast off.

Neck: Pick up approx 40 to 52 sts (divisible by 4) on double pointed needles size 4.5 mm with 1 thread Vienna and 1 thread Baby-ull and P 1 round from RS, K 1 round from WS. Continue in Rib and cast off loosely when Rib measures 6-7-8 cm.

SOCKS:
Shoe size: 23/25 – 26/28 – 30/34
Foot length: 15-19-22 cm 

Materials: Garnstudio’s Viking
100 g for all sizes, colour no 06, red mix or no 09, blue/green mix

DROPS double pointed needles size 2.5 and 3.5 mm

Knitting Tension: 22 sts x 30 rows on needle size 3.5 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm
Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 44-48-52 sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm and knit 10-12-14 cm Rib. Change to needle size 3.5 mm and knit 1 round in stocking sts, at the same time dec 6 sts evenly = 38-42-46 sts. Remember the knitting tension! Continue as follows:
Heel: Knit 4-4.5-5 cm stocking sts over 18-18-20 sts mid back.
Heel decreases: 
1st row = RS: knit 10-10-12 sts, K 2 tog into back of st, turn the piece. 
2nd row: slip 1 st as if to knit, P 4-4-6 sts, P 2 tog, P1, turn the piece. 
3rd row: slip 1st as if to knit, K 5-5-7 sts, K 2 tog into back of st, K1, turn the piece. 
4th row: slip 1 st as if to knit, P 6-6-8 sts, P 2 tog, P1, turn the piece. 
5th row: slip 1 st as if to knit, K 7-7-9 sts, K 2 og into back of st, K 1, turn the piece. 
6th row: slip 1 st as if to knit, P 8-8-10 sts, P2 tog, P1, turn the piece. 
7th row: K2 tog, K 8-8-10, K 2 tog into back of st = 10-10-12 sts left. 
Now pick up 7-7-8 sts on each side of heel and put all sts on the same needles = 44-48-54 sts. Dec 1 st on each side of the upper 22-24-27 sts on every round a total of 4-6-9 times = 36-36-36 sts. 
When foot measures 12-16-19 cm (from beg of the heel dec) dec for toes as follows: *K2 tog, K4*, repeat from *-* = 30 sts on round. Knit 3 rounds and dec as follows: *K2 tog, K3*, repeat from *-* = 24 sts. Knit 1 round and dec as follows: *K2 tog, K2*, repeat from *-* = 18 sts. Knit 1 round and dec as follows: *K2 tog, K1*, repeat from *-* = 12 sts. Knit 1 round and knit all sts tog 2 by 2 = 6 sts.
