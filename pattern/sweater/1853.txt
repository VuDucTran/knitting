550-600-650-700-750 g colour no 17, blue green

DROPS Needle size 7 and 9 mm
DROPS Crochet needle size 5 mm

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 11 stitches x 16 rows with Ull-Flamé and needle size 8 mm and stocking stitch = 10 x 10 cm. Remember needle size is only a guide!

Ridge/Garter stitch (back and forth on needle): 1 ridge = Knit 2 rows.

Knitting tip: If you are using tweed yarn, use 2 balls and work alternate rows/rounds from first the one then the other ball; this gives a more even colour.
-------------------------------------------------------

Back piece: Cast on 46-49-53-56-59 stitches (incl. 1 edge stitch in each side) with needle size 7 mm and Ull-Flamé. Work as follows: * 2 ridges with Ull-Flamé, 1 ridge with Alaska *, repeat from *-* a total of 2 times = 6 ridges altogether. Change to needle size 8 mm and continue with stocking stitch and Ull-Flamé to finished length. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 33-37-37-38-39 cm decrease for armholes in each side every 2nd row: 3 stitches 1 time and 1 stitch 2-2-3-4-5 times = 36-39-41-42-43 stitches. When piece measures 46-51-53-55-57 cm, cast off the middle 12-13-15-16-17 stitches for neck. Then decrease 1 stitch on next row towards neck = 11-12-12-12-12 stitches left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 48-53-55-57-59 cm.

Left front piece: Cast on 25-27-29-30-32 stitches (incl. 1 edge stitch in the side and 3 band stitches towards mid front) with needle size 7 mm and Ull-Flamé. Work as follows: * 2 ridges with Ull-Flamé, 1 ridge with Alaska *, repeat from *-* a total of 2 times = 6 ridges altogether. Change to needle size 8 mm and continue with stocking stitch and Ull-Flamé with 3 band stitches towards mid front in garter stitch. When piece measures 33-37-37-38-39 cm decrease for armhole in side as for back piece = 20-22-23-23-24 stitches on needle. When piece measures 44-48-50-51-53 cm cast off 6-7-7-7-8 stitches towards mid front for neck. Continue to decrease towards neck every 2nd row: 2 stitches 1 time and 1 stitch 1-1-2-2-2 times = 11-12-12-12-12 stitches left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 48-53-55-57-59 cm.
Right front piece: Cast on and work as for left but decrease for armhole and neck in opposite sides. In addition, work buttonholes on band. 1 buttonhole = cast off 3rd stitch from edge and cast on a new stitch over the cast-off stitch on next row. Work buttonholes when piece measures: 
Size 3/4 years: 27, 35 and 43 cm. 
Size 5/6 years: 31, 39 and 47 cm. 
Size 7/8 years: 32, 40 and 48 cm. 
Size 9/10 years: 32, 41 and 50 cm. 
Size 11/12 years: 34, 43 and 52 cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 22-23-24-25-26 stitches (incl. 1 edge stitch in each side) with needle size 7 mm and Ull-Flamé. Work as follows: * 2 ridges with Ull-Flamé, 1 ridge with Alaska *, repeat from *-* a total of 3 times = 9 ridges altogether. Change to needle size 8 mm and continue with stocking stitch and Ull-Flamé to finished length.
At the same time, when piece measures 8 cm, increase 1 stitch in each side every 3-3.5-3-3-3.5 cm a total of 6-6-8-9-9 times = 34-35-40-43-44 stitches. When sleeve measures 27-29-33-37-39 cm decrease for sleeve cap in each side every 2nd row: 3 stitches 1 time then 2 stitches in each side until piece measures 33-36-40-45-48 cm. Cast off the remaining stitches. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. 

Hood: Knit up approx. 40 to 50 stitches around neck (over the bands as well) with needle size 7 mm and Ull-Flamé. Work as follows: * 2 ridges with Ull-Flamé, 1 ridge with Alaska *, repeat from *-* until hood measures approx. 26-28-30-32-34 cm. Cast off, sew top of hood together.

Band: Crochet with hook size 5 mm and Alaska: 1 row of double crochets around the outermost ridge along both bands. 

Pockets: Cast on 12-12-13-14-14 stitches with needle size 7 mm and Ull-Flamé. Work 1 ridge, continue with stocking stitch, but 1 stitch garter stitch in each side. When pocket measures 9-9-10-11-11 cm change to Alaska. Work 2 ridges and cast off. Work 1 more pocket. Crochet 1 row of double crochets with hook size 5 mm and Alaska on both sides and along bottom edge of pockets. 

Assembly: Sew the pockets onto the front pieces, approx. 9-9-10-11-12 cm from mid front and 11-12-13-14-14 cm from bottom edge. Sew in sleeves. Sew sleeve and side seams in one go inside the 1 edge stitch. Sew on buttons. 

Plaits: Make 3 plaits, 1 short and 2 long. 
1 short plait: Cut 3 strands Ull-Flamé and 3 strands Alaska of approx. 100 cm each. Fold all strands in the middle so that you have 12 strands to plait with. Make 3 bunches as follows: 2 bunches of 3 strands Ull-Flamé and 1 bunch of 6 strands Alaska. Plait the 3 bunches together, tie a knot in the end so that you have a tassel of approx. 4 cm at the bottom. At top of plait, where the strands have been folded, is a small loop.
1 long plait: Cut the same number of strands as for the short plait, but of 140 cm each. Divide them into bunches in the same way and plait them together. Make 2 long plaits. 
Fasten the short plait in the tip of the hood as follows: Pull the loop on the plait through a couple of stitches on the hood, then thread the tassel through the loop.
