Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle):
K all rows.

INCREASE TIP:
Inc 1 st by making a YO inside 1 edge st. On next row K YO twisted (i.e. work in the back loop of st instead of front) to avoid holes.
----------------------------------------------------------

BACK PIECE:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 48-50-54-58-60 sts (incl 1 edge st in each side) on needle size 7 mm with purple. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! Work in garter st until finished measurements. When piece measures 27-30-33-36-39 cm, cast off for armholes as follows: Cast off 2 sts at the beg of the next 2 rows = 44-46-50-54-56 sts. 
Continue in garter st until piece measures 38-42-46-50-54 cm. On next row, cast off the middle 18-18-20-20-22 sts for neck and finish each shoulder separately = 13-14-15-17-17 sts remain. Loosely cast off all sts when piece measures 40-44-48-52-56 cm. 

FRONT PIECE:
Work front piece as back piece until piece measures 37-41-45-49-53 cm. On next row, cast off the middle 14-14-16-16-18 sts for neck and finish each shoulder separately. Then cast off 1 st at beg of the next 2 rows from neck = 13-14-15-17-17 sts remain on the shoulder. Loosely cast off all sts when piece measures 40-44-48-52-56 cm.

SLEEVE:
Worked back and forth on needle. Cast on 28-29-30-33-34 sts (incl 1 edge st in each side) on needle size 7 mm with purple. Work in garter st until finished measurements. When piece measures 3 cm, inc 1 st in each side - READ INCREASE TIP! Repeat inc approx. every 9-7-6-7-7 cm 3-4-5-5-6 more times = 36-39-42-45-48 sts on needle. Work in garter st until piece measures 32-36-39-43-47 cm and loosely cast off.  

ASSEMBLY:
Sew shoulder seams inside cast-off edges. Sew sleeves in front and back piece, sew the upper part of sleeve to where 2 sts were cast off for armhole, and sew sleeves seams inside 1 edge st. Then sew side seam from armhole and down.

NECK EDGE:
Worked in the round on circular needle. Pick up from RS approx. 30 - -40 sts along the neck on circular needle size 7 mm. P 1 round and loosely cast off with K. Fasten off.
