Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle): K all rows. 
----------------------------------------------------------

BACK PIECE: 
Worked back and forth on needle.
Cast on 78-82-86-90 sts (includes 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3.5 mm with Muskat. Work 8 rows garter st – see above. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures 32-34-36-38 cm cast on new sts for sleeves each side at the end of every row: 1 sts 5-3-1-1 times, 2 sts 1-3-4-5 times and 3 sts 0-0-1-1 time = 92-100-110-118 sts. When piece measures 45-48-51-54 cm cast off the middle 26-28-30-32 sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off on every row from neckline: 1 st 2 times = 31-34-38-41 sts left on shoulder/sleeve. Cast off loosely when piece measures 47-50-53-56 cm.

RIGHT FRONT PIECE: 
Cast on 39-41-43-45 sts (includes 1 edge st at the side) on needle size 3.5 mm with Muskat. Work 8 rows garter st. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st. When piece measures 32-34-36-38 cm cast on new sts at the side as described for back piece = 46-50-55-59 sts. When piece measures 41-44-46-49 cm cast off 8-9-10-11 sts towards mid front for neck and cast off to shape the neckline at the beg of every row from mid front: 2 sts 2 times and 1 st 3 times = 31-34-38-41 sts left on shoulder/sleeve. Cast off loosely when piece measures 47-50-53-56 cm.  

LEFT FRONT PIECE: 
Like right front piece, but mirrored. 

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. 

SLEEVE EDGES: Pick up 50-56-56-62 sts along sleeve edge on needle size 3.5 mm with Muskat. K 1 row from WS, K 1 row from RS and continue in rib, K3/P3, with 1 edge st each side. Cast off in rib when rib measures 3 cm. Repeat along the other sleeve edge.  

FRONT BANDS: Pick up approx 93-99-105-111 sts (divisible by 6 + 3) along left front piece on needle size 3.5 mm with Muskat. K 1 row from WS, K 1 row from RS and continue in rib = P3/K3, with 3 garter sts each side – from RS = K3 sts next to 3 garter sts each side.  Cast off in rib when front band measures 3-3-4-4 cm. 
Repeat along right front piece, but after 1.5-1.5-2-2 cm make 8 buttonholes evenly distributed, place the top buttonhole approx 0.5 cm from neckline and the bottom one approx 4 cm from bottom edge. 1 buttonhole = cast off 1 st and cast on 1 new st on return row. 

NECKLINE: Pick up approx 90 to 110 sts round neckline (also pick up on front bands) on needle size 3.5 mm with Muskat. K 5 rows, cast off. 

ASSEMBLY: Sew side and under arm seams inside 1 edge st and sew on buttons.
