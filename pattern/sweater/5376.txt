Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle): K all rows.   

DECREASE TIP: (applies to armholes and neckline): 
All dec are done from RS, dec on the inside of 3 sts in garter st at the armhole and at the neck. 
Dec AFTER 3 sts as follows: Slip 1 st as if to K, K 1, psso. Dec BEFORE 3 sts as follows: K 2 tog.
--------------------------------------------------------

BACK PIECE:  
Worked back and forth on needle. 
Cast on 50-54-58-60-62 sts on needle size 5 mm with Nepal. Work in GARTER ST over all sts - see explanation above! REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! 
When piece measures 5-5-6-6-7 cm, inc 1 st in each side, repeat inc when piece measures 10-11-12-13-14 cm = 54-58-62-64-66 sts. When piece measures 16-18-20-21-22 cm, cast off the outermost 3 sts in each side for armholes (cast off at beg of the next 2 rows). On next row from RS, dec 1 st in each side - SEE DECREASE TIP! Repeat dec every other row a total of 3-4-4-4-4 times = 42-44-48-50-52 sts. When piece measures 27-30-33-35-37 cm, cast off the middle 14-14-14-16-16 sts for neck and finish each shoulder (= 14-15-17-17-18 sts) separately. Cast off when piece measures 28-31-34-36-38 cm (or slip on a stitch holder to work the shoulders tog with grafting/kitchener st). 

LEFT FRONT PIECE: 
Worked back and forth on needle. 
Cast on 25-27-29-30-31 sts on needle size 5 mm with Nepal. Work in garter st over all sts. When piece measures 5-5-6-6-7 cm, inc 1 st in the side, repeat inc when piece measures 10-11-12-13-14 cm = 27-29-31-32-33 sts.
READ ALL OF THE FOLLOWING SECTION BEFORE CONTINUING! 
When piece measures 16-18-20-21-22 cm, cast off 3 sts at beg of next row from the side. Then dec 1 st for armhole at beg of next row from RS – READ DECREASE TIP! Repeat dec every other row a total of 3-4-4-4-4 times. 
AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures approx. 18-20-22-23-24 cm, dec (on next row from RS) 1 st towards the neck - SEE DECREASE TIP! Repeat dec every other row a total of 3-3-3-4-4 times and then every 4th row a total of 4 times in all sizes. After dec for neck and armhole,
14-15-17-17-18 sts remain on needle for shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 28-31-34-36-38 cm (or slip sts on a stitch holder). 

RIGHT FRONT PIECE: 
Work as left front piece but reversed.

ASSEMBLY: 
Sew or use grafting/kitchener stitches to work the shoulders tog. Sew side seams tog edge to edge in front loop of outermost sts. 

TIES:
Crochet 1 tie with Nepal on hook size 3.5 mm on each side of neck. 
Start with 1 dc in outermost st where dec for neck beg. Crochet approx. 18-18-20-20-22 cm with ch. Now work a cone at the end of the ch-row as follows: Form a ring with 1 sl st in 4th ch from hook, this is the top of the cone.
ROUND 1: Work 4 dc in ch-ring.
ROUND 2: 2 dc in every dc = 8 dc.
ROUND 3: * 1 dc in first dc, 2 dc in next dc *, repeat from *-* the rest of the round = 12 dc.
ROUND 4-5: 1 dc in every dc = 12 dc.
ROUND 6: * 1 dc in first dc, then crochet 2 dc tog *, repeat from *-* the entire round = 8 dc.
ROUND 7: 1 dc in every dc = 8 dc.
ROUND 8: Work all dc tog 2 by 2 = 4 dc.
Cut the thread, baste it up and down through sts, pull the thread to tighten and fasten tightly.
