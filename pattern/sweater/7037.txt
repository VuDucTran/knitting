DROPS AIR from Garnstudio
150-200-200-250-250-300 g colour no 10, fog

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE SIZE 5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 33 rows in garter st = 10 x 10 cm, and 17 sts x 22 rows in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm.
DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (40 cm) SIZE 4 mm – for edge in garter st. You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 12.60£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K. 
----------------------------------------------------------

FRONT PIECE:
Worked back and forth on circular needle. 
Cast on (51) 54-58-61-64-68 sts on circular needle size 5 mm with Air and work in GARTER ST - see explanation above. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION When piece measures (22) 24-27-30-33-36 cm, dec 1 st at beg of the next 2 rows (marks armholes) = (49) 52-56-59-62-66 sts on needle. When piece measures (29) 32-35-39-42-46 cm, slip the middle (7) 8-10-11-14-14 sts on 1 stitch holder for neck and finish each shoulder separately. Then cast off at beg of every row from neck as follows: Cast off 2 sts 2 times and 1 st 2 times = (15) 16-17-18-18-20 sts remain on shoulder. Continue to work until piece measures (33) 36-40-44-48-52 cm and loosely cast off. Repeat on the other shoulder. 

BACK PIECE:
Cast on and work as on front piece until piece measures (31) 34-38-42-46-50 cm. Now cast off the middle (17) 18-20-21-24-24 sts for neck and finish each shoulder separately. Continue to dec 1 st on next row from neck = (15) 16-17-18-18-20 sts remain on row. Continue to work until piece measures (33) 36-40-44-48-52 cm and loosely cast off. Repeat on the other shoulder.

SLEEVE:
Worked back and forth on circular needle.
Cast on (29) 31-33-33-34-34 sts (incl 1 edge st in each side) on circular needle size 4 mm with Air. Work 2 ridges. Switch to circular needle size 5 mm and work in stocking st. When piece measures (5) 6-6-6-7-7 cm, inc 1 st in each side. Repeat inc every (5) 6-4½-4½-3½-3½ cm (4) 4-6-7-9-10 times in total = (37) 39-45-47-52-54 sts. When piece measures (22) 26-31-35-39-43 cm, work 2 rows while cast off 4 sts at beg of each of these rows, then loosely cast off all sts.

ASSEMBLY:
Sew the shoulder seams. Sew side seams in each side on front and back piece from armhole and down until approx. 6 cm at the bottom of body (= vent) – sew in outer loops of edge sts to avoid a chunky seam.
Sew sleeves tog inside 1 edge st and sew in sleeves.

NECK EDGE:
Pick up from RS approx. 44 to 62 sts around the neck (incl sts on stitch holder at the front) on circular needle size 4 mm with Air.
