Knitting tension: 21 sts x 28 rows on needles size 4mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 

Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.

Back and front piece: 
The jumper is knitted round on circular needles all the way up to the neckline and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 144-160-176 (184-200-216) on circular needle size 3 mm and knit 4-4-5 (5-6-6) cm Rib. Change to needle size 4mm and continue in M.1 and then M.2. Remember your knitting tension! When piece measures 25-27-29 (31-32-33) knit next row as follows: cast off 5-8-9 (9-11-13) sts for armhole, knit 63-65-71 (75-79-83) sts = front piece, cast off 9-15-17 (17-21-25) sts for armhole, knit 63-65-71 (75-79-83) sts = back piece, cast off 4-7-8 (8-10-12) sts for armhole . On next round cast on 5 new sts over the cast off armhole sts (these sts are to be cut for armhole and are not included in pattern) = 136-140-152 (160-168-176) sts. When piece measures 26-29-32 (35-37-39) cm knit M.3 – start by arrow for appropriate size on both back and front. When piece measures 34-37-40 (42-44-46) cm cast off the middle 11-11-13 (15-17-19) sts mid front for neck = 125-129-139 (145-151-157) sts left on round. Now finish the piece back and forth on needle, continue in M.3. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 4 times = 107-110-121 (127-133-139) sts. When piece measures 38-41-44 (47-49-51) cm cast of the middle 25-25-27 (29-31-33) sts on back piece and dec 2 sts 1 time towards neckline = 39-41-45 (47-49-51) sts left on each shoulder (incl the 5 sts to be cut for armhole). Cast of remaining sts when M.3 is finished, piece measures approx 40-43-46 (49-51-53) cm.

Sleeves: Cast on 44-48-48 (52-52-56) sts on double pointed needles size 3mm with grey and knit 4-4-5 (5-6-6) cm Rib. Change to needles size 4mm, knit M.1 and then M.2. At the same time when sleeve measures 8 cm inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 10-10-12 (12-14-14) times as follows:
Size 3/4 years: on every 7th round
Size 5/6 – 9/10 – 13/14 years: on every 8th round
Size 7/8 – 11/12 years: on every 7th and 8th round alternately
= 64-68-72 (76-80-84) sts – incorporate new sts in M.2 as you go along. 
Read the rest of sleeve pattern before knitting it!
When sleeve measures 34-37-40 (43-46-49) cm knit the rest of the sleeve back and forth on needle. At the same time when sleeve measures 33-37-41 (44-48-52) cm knit M.4 – start at the right side of diagram for all sizes. After M.4 continue in grey until sleeve measures approx 36-40-44 (47-51-55) cm. Knit 2 cm in reverse stocking sts (for edge to be sewn on to body piece).

Assembly: Insert a marking thread in the middle of the 5 armhole sts. Sew 2 machine seams on both sides of marking thread, 1st seam = ½ st from the thread, 2nd seam = ½ st from 1st seam. Cut for armhole. Sew shoulder seams. 
Neck: Pick up approx 80 to 100 sts (divisible by 2) round the neck on needle size 3mm with grey. Purl 1 round, knit 1 round and continue in Rib until neck measures 5-5-5 (6-6-6) cm. Cast off in Rib. Fold neck double and attach to wrong side. Sew sleeves to body pieces, fold edge on sleeve cap over the cut edge on front and back pieces and sew.

HAT:
Knitting tension: 10 sts x 14 rows on needles size 9mm with Eskimo in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K2, P2*, repeat from *-*

The hat is worked back and forth on needle.
Cast on 44-48-52  sts on needle size 9mm with Eskimo and knit 4 rows stocking sts (= rolling edge). Make all measurements from this point. Knit 3 cm Rib and continue in stocking sts until finished measurements. At the same time after the Rib dec 4-3-2 sts evenly on row = 40-45-50 sts. 
When piece measures 10.5-11-11.5 cm from RS place 5 markers as follows: 1 st, 1 marker, *8-9-10 sts, 1 marker*, repeat from *-* a total of 4 times, after the last marker there are 7-8-9 sts left. 
Now dec 1 st to the left of all markers on every row from the right side by knitting tog 2 sts a total of 6-7-8 times = 10 sts left. 
Cut the thread, pull it through remaining sts and sew. Sew hat mid back in the outer loops of sts. 

SCARF: 
Knitting tension: 9 sts x 18 rows on needles size 10mm with Eskimo in garter sts = 10 x 10 cm. 
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows

Loosely cast on 12-14-16 sts on needle size 10mm with Eskimo and knit garter sts until scarf measures 10-12-14 cm. Now put half the sts on a thread. Continue over the remaining 6-7-8 sts until piece measures 19-22-25 cm and put these sts on a thread. Put sts from the first thread back on needle and knit until piece measures 19-22-25 cm. Put all sts back on same needle and knit until scarf measures approx 70-80-90 cm. Cast off loosely. Have you made this or any other of our designs?
