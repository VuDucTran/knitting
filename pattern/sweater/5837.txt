Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth on needle):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K.

GARTER ST (in the round on circular needle/double pointed needles):
* K 1 round and P 1 round *, repeat from *-*.

STRIPES 1 (applies to bottom of body and sleeves):
1 ridge dark purple, 2 ridges moss green, 1 ridge dark purple, 1 ridge orange, 1 ridge moss green, 1 ridge orange.

STRIPES 2 (applies to collar):
* 1 ridge orange, 1 ridge moss green, 1 ridge orange, 1 ridge dark purple, 2 ridge moss green, 1 ridge dark purple, 1 ridge moss green *, repeat from *-*.
-------------------------------------------------------

BODY: 
Worked in the round on circular needle. 
Cast on 100-108-116-124-132 sts on circular needle size 4 mm with dark purple. Insert a marker at beg of round and a marker after 50-54-58-62-66 sts (these marks the sides). P 1 round. Continue in GARTER ST and with STRIPES 1 – see explanation above. After ridges, piece measures approx. 4 cm. Switch to circular needle size 5 mm, continue in stocking st and moss green. REMEMBER THE KNITTING TENSION! When piece measures 6-7-8-9-10 cm, P 1 round, continue in stocking st. When piece measures 10-10-11-11-12 cm, inc 1 st on each side of markers in the sides. Repeat inc every 5-6-7-8-9 cm 2 more times = 112-120-128-136-144 sts. When piece measures 22-24-26-28-30 cm and 24-26-29-31-34 cm, P 1 round. When piece measures 27-30-33-36-39 cm, work next round as follows: Cast off 3 sts for armhole, K 18-20-22-23-25, cast off 14-14-14-16-16 sts for neck, K 18-20-22-23-25, cast off 6 sts for armhole, K 50-54-58-62-66 and cast off the last 3 sts for armhole, cut the thread. Finish front and back piece separately.

BACK PIECE: 
= 50-54-58-62-66 sts. Work in stocking st - AT THE SAME TIME cast off for armholes at beg of every row in each side: 1 st 2-2-3-4-4 times = 46-50-52-54-58 sts. When piece measures 38-42-46-50-54 cm, cast off the middle 18-20-22-24-24 sts for neck and finish each shoulder separately. On next row from the neck, dec 1 st = 13-14-14-14-16 sts remain on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 40-44-48-52-56 cm. 

RIGHT FRONT PIECE: 
= 18-20-22-23-25 sts. Then cast off for armhole in the side as on back piece. At the same time 2 cm after dividing for armhole and neck, dec 1 st towards the neck. Repeat dec every 2 cm 2-3-4-4-4 more times = 13-14-14-14-16 sts remain on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 40-44-48-52-56 cm. 

LEFT FRONT PIECE: 
Work as right front piece but reversed.   

SLEEVE:
Worked in the round on double pointed needles. 
Cast on 30-32-32-34-34 sts on double pointed needles size 4 mm with dark purple.  P 1 round – continue in garter st and with stripes as on bottom of body. Switch to double pointed needles size 5 mm and continue in stocking st - insert a marker at beg of round. 
READ ALL OF THE FOLLOWING SECTION BEFORE CONTINUING!
INCREASE: When piece measures 8 cm, inc 1 st on each side of marker. Repeat inc every 4-4-3½-3½-3½ cm 4-5-7-8-9 more times = 40-44-48-52-54 sts.  
PATTERN: When piece measures 8-9-10-11-12 cm, P 1 round. Also P 1 round when piece measures 20-22-24-26-28 cm and 22-24-27-29-32 cm. 
When piece measures 28-32-35-40-45 cm, cast off 6 sts mid under sleeve (i.e. 3 sts on each side of marker) and work back and forth on needle until finished measurements. Then cast off at beg of every row in each side: 2 sts 2 times and 1 st 1-1-2-2-2 times, then cast off 2 sts in each side until piece measures 33-37-41-46-51 cm, finally cast off 3 sts 1 time in each side. Cast off the remaining sts, piece measures approx. 34-38-42-47-52 cm.  

ASSEMBLY: 
Sew shoulder seams and sew in sleeves. 

COLLAR:  
Worked back and forth on circular needle size 4 mm from mid front. Start by knitting up sts on the left side of the 14-14-14-16-16 sts cast off mid front - knit up with moss green inside 1 st as follows: Approx. 22 to 30 sts up to shoulder, insert a marker, approx. 20 to 28 sts over back piece, insert a marker and 22 to 30 sts down along the other side of neck (do not knit up over the 14-14-14-16-16 sts cast off mid front) = approx. 64 to 88 sts. K 1 row from WS. Continue in GARTER ST and with STRIPES 2 – see explanation above. AT THE SAME TIME every 6th row, inc 1 st on each side of marker on each shoulder (inc by working 2 sts in 1 st). When collar measures 8-8-8-10-10 cm, cast off, cast off from WS on last row of 1 ridge.
Place left part of neck edge over right and fasten towards the 14-14-14-16-16 sts cast off at the bottom of neck.
