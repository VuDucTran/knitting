Yarn group A + A or C

Size: 2 - 3/4 - 5/6 - 7/8 - 9/10 years
Size in cm: 92 - 98/104 - 110/116 - 122/128 - 134/140  
Materials: 
DROPS ALPACA from Garnstudio
100-100-100-150-200 g colour no 100, off white
DROPS KID-SILK from Garnstudio
50-50-50-50-100 g colour no 01, off white

DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE (60 cm) SIZE 4.5 mm - or size needed to get 17 sts x 22 rows in moss st = 10 x 10 cm with 1 strand of each yarn held together.
DROPS CIRCULAR NEEDLE SIZE 4 mm – for rib
DROPS BUTTON: ARCHED (white), NO 521: 3-3-3-3-4 pieces You can get the yarn to make this pattern from 13.40£*.(*) Pattern prices are calculated from the smallest size / cheapest colour. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGARTER ST (back and forth):
K all rows. 1 ridge = 2 rows K. 

MOSS ST: 
Row 1: * K 1, P 1 *, repeat from *-*. 
Row 2: K over P and P over K. Repeat 2nd row.

BUTTONHOLES:
Dec for buttonholes on right band. Dec by working third and fourth st tog from mid front, then make a YO.
Dec for buttonholes when piece measures: 
SIZE 2 years: 5, 11 and 17 cm
SIZE 3/4 years: 5, 11 and 18 cm
SIZE 5/6 years: 5, 12 and 20 cm
SIZE 7/8 years: 5, 13 and 21 cm
SIZE 9/10 years: 5, 11, 17 and 23 cm.
----------------------------------------------------------

JACKET:
Work back and forth on circular needle from bottom up.
Cast on 128-132-140-152-160 sts on circular needle size 4 mm with 1 strand Alpaca and 1 strand Kid-Silk (= 2 strands). Work as follows – from RS: 5 sts in garter st (= band), K 2, P 2/K 2 until 5 sts remain, work 5 sts in garter st (= band). Continue with rib like this until piece measures 4 cm, P a row from WS (continue bands in garter st) while dec 22-22-22-26-26 sts evenly = 106-110-118-126-134 sts. Switch to circular needle size 4.5 mm and continue in MOSS ST - see explanation above with 5 sts in garter st in each side until finished measurements. When piece measures 5 cm, beg to dec for BUTTON HOLES – see explanation above. 
When piece measures 10-11-12-13-14 cm, work as follows: Work 26-27-29-31-33 sts (= right front piece), cast off the next 6 sts for armhole, work 42-44-48-52-56 sts (= back piece), cast off the next 6 sts for armhole, work 26-27-29-31-33 sts (= left front piece). Finish each part separately.

BACK PIECE:
= 42-44-48-52-56 sts. Continue cast off for armholes in each side at beg of every row as follows: 2 sts 1 time = 38-40-44-48-52 sts. When piece measures 20-22-24-26-28 cm, cast off the middle 18-20-22-24-24 sts for neck and finish each side separately. Then cast off 1 st on next row towards the neck = 9-9-10-11-13 sts remain on the shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 22-24-26-28-30 cm.

RIGHT FRONT PIECE: 
= 26-27-29-31-33 sts. Cast off for armhole in the side as on back piece = 24-25-27-29-31 sts. When piece measures 18-19-21-22-24 cm, slip 11-12-13-14-14 sts towards mid front on a stitch holder for neck. Then cast off on every row towards neck: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times = 9-9-10-11-13 sts remain on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 22-24-26-28-30 cm. 

LEFT FRONT PIECE:
Work as right front piece but without buttonholes.

SLEEVE: 
Worked back and forth on circular needle. Cast on 42-42-46-46-50 sts on circular needle size 4 mm with a strand of each yarn (= 2 strands). Work rib K 2/P 2 and 1 edge st in garter st in each side until piece measures 4 cm. Switch to circular needle size 4.5 mm and K 1 row from RS while dec 8-6-8-6-8 sts evenly = 34-36-38-40-42 sts. Now work in moss st. When piece measures 6 cm, inc 1 st inside 1 edge st in each side of piece. Inc every 5½-7-6-5-4½ cm 2-2-3-4-5 more times = 40-42-46-50-54 sts. Work the inc sts in moss st. When piece measures 20-23-26-29-32 cm, cast-off 3 sts in each side of piece. Then cast-off for sleeve cap in each side as follows: Dec 2 sts in each side until piece measures 24-27-30-33-36 cm. Cast-off 3 sts in each side, then cast off the remaining sts. Piece measures approx. 25-28-31-34-37 cm.

ASSEMBLY: 
Sew the shoulder seams. Sew in sleeves. Sew the buttons on to the left band.

NECK EDGE: 
Pick up sts with circular needle size 4 mm around the neck from RS with a strand of each yarn, approx. 58 to 72 sts (incl sts on stitch holders). Work 2 ridges. Loosely cast off.
