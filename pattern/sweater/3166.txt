Sizes: 3/4 - 5/6 (7/8 - 89/10 - 11/12) years

Finished measurements: 78-84 (92-98-106) cm


Materials:
Garnstudio ALASKA, 100% wool 
250-300 (350-350-400) g col. 25, light yellow
100-100 (100-150-150) g col. 02, natural
50-100 (100-100-100) g col. 06, black
50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 08, gold
50-50 (50-50-50) g col. 09, orange

DROPS 3.5 mm  and 5 mm  circular and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge. Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 17 sts x 21 rows on larger needles in pattern = 10 x 10 cm 

Rib: * K 1 st into back of st, P 1 st *

Pattern: See charts below (1 chart = 1 repeat). The pattern is seen from the right side and is knit entirely in stockinette stitch. The chart for Pattern 1 has arrows indicating the starting row for different sizes; the chart for Pattern 2 is identical for all sizes.

Body: Cast on 110-118 (126-134-140) sts on smaller circular needles with natural; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 5 cm  rib, but after 1 row change to light yellow. Change to larger circular needles and Pattern 1 (start at the appropriate arrow on the chart), increasing 22-26 (30-34-40) evenly distributed on the 1st row = 132-144 (156-168-180) sts. When the work measures 12-15 (18-20-22) cm , knit Pattern 2 (center the pattern across the front) and then knit the rest with light yellow. At the same time, when the work measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm , divide the work for armholes and knit the rest of the front and back separately. 


Back: = 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. Continue Pattern. When the work measures 39-42 (46-48-50) cm, bind off the center 28-28 (30-32-32) sts for the neck, then bind off 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off the remaining sts for the shoulder when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.


Front: = 66-72 (78-84-90) sts. Continue Pattern. When the work measures 36-39 (43-45-47) cm , bind off the center 22-22 (24-26-26) sts for the neck. Then bind off at each neck edge every other row 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times. Bind off the remaining sts for the shoulder when the work measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 28-28 (30-30-32) sts on smaller double pointed needles with natural; join and place a marker at the join. Knit rib for 4 cm , but after 1 row change to light yellow. Change to larger double pointed needles and knit Pattern 1 (start at the appropriate arrow), increasing 6-6 (8-10-10) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 34-34 (38-40-42) sts. Inc 1 st each side of marker 15-17 (18-17-20) times:
Sizes 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8: every 3rd row 
Sizes 9/10 + 11/12: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row 
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. When the work measures 27-30 (34-37-40) cm , bind off.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 76-86 sts on smaller double pointed needles with light yellow around the neck; join and knit 3 cm  rib with the last row in natural and bind off with natural.
