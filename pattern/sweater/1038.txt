Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needles size 4 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*. 
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st.  Back and front piece: 1-3 (1-1-3) rounds in mint, M.1, M.2 (knit 3 vertical repeats, finish on round 2 or 11), M.3, M.4, M.5. Finished measurements for size 3/4 and 5/6. Size 7/8 + 9/10 + 11/12: continue with M6, M.7, 1 round grey, 2 rounds mint. Sleeves: 2-2 (2-2-2) rounds mint, M.8, M.2  (knit 2-3 (4-5-6) vertical repeats, finish on round 2 or 11), M.3, M.6, M.7, 1 round grey.

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needles and cut for armholes afterwards. Cast on 130-138 (144-152-160) sts on circular needle size 2.5mm with mint and knit 4-4 (4-5-5) cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 26-30 (36-40-44) sts evenly on first round = 156-168 (180-192-204) sts. Continue in pattern – see explanation above. Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 21-23 (26-27-27) cm inc 4 sts each side for armhole (to be cut for armholes afterwards and not included in pattern). When 5-5 (6-6-6) rounds left of pattern acc to size, cast of the middle 32-32 (34-36-36) sts on front piece for neck and complete each side separately. Dec 2 sts on next row to shape the neckline. At the same time, when 2-2 (2-2-2) rounds left of pattern cast off the middle 36-36 (38-40-40) sts on back piece. After the last row of pattern , piece measures approx 40-43 (48-50-51), cast off remaining sts.  

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 30-32 (34-36-38) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with mint and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time inc 11-9 (11-13-13) sts evenly on first round = 41-41 (45-49-51) sts. Continue in pattern – see explanation above and make sure to place a pattern repeat mid upper sleeve. At the same time, inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 18-20 (22-22-23) times as follows:
Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8 years: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10 years:  on every  3rd and 4th round alternately
Size 11/12 years: on every * 4th, 4th and 3rd * round alternately
= 77-81 (89-93-97) sts. After the pattern, sleeve measures approx 26-29.5 (34-38-41) cm. Knit 5 rounds in reverse stocking st = edge for sewing on sleeve to body piece. Cast off and knit the other sleeve. 


Assembly: Insert a marking thread in the middle of the inc sts for armhole each side. Make 2 seams each side on your sewing machine, the first seam ½ st from the MT and the other one ½ st from the first one. Cut the piece between these. Sew shoulder seams.
Neckline:  Pick up 76-90 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with grey and knit 2.5 cm Rib, change to rose, knit 1 round and cast off in rose. 

Set in sleeves from the RS as follows: sew in the last row before the edge in reverse stocking sts on sleeves to the first row after the seam in the sts for cutting on body piece.
