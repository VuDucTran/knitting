SWEATER:

Sizes: 5/6 (8/10-12/14) years
Finished measurements:
Chest: 74 (86-98) cm
Waist: 70 (78-86) cm 

Materials: DROPS PUDDEL from Garnstudio
300 (350-400) g color no. 08, coal gray

DROPS 7 mm needles and double pointed needles (for the neck), or size needed to obtain correct gauge. Gauge: 10 sts x 14 row in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.

Front: Cast on 37 (41-45) sts. Knit 4 rows garter st, then continue in stockinette st. Inc at each side: Sizes 5/6: 1 st when the work measures 13 cm = 39 sts.
Sizes 8/10: 1 st when the work measures 9 cm and 18 cm = 45 sts.
Sizes 12/14: 1 st when the work measures 7 cm, 14 cm  and 21 cm = 51 sts.

When the work measures 26 (28-30) cm bind off for armhole at each side every other row: 2 sts 1 (2-2) times, 1 st 1 (1-3) times = 33 (35-37) sts. When the work measures 38 (42-45) cm , put the center 7 (9-9) sts on a st holder for the neck. Bind off for the neck every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Bind off when the work measures 43 (47-51) cm.

Back: Cast on and knit as for the front. When the work measures 43 (47-51) cm put the center 15 (17-17) sts on a st holder for the neck. Bind off the remaining sts.

Sleeve: Cast on 24 (26-26) sts loosely (if desired, cast on with double yarn). Knit 4 rows garter st and then continue in stockinette st. When the work measures 4 (5-5) cm inc 1 st at each side (make increases 1 st in from the edge for a smooth seam) 5 (6-8) times every 4 (4.5-4) cm = 34 (38-42) sts.
When the work measures approx. 31 (38-43) cm bind off each side for sleeve cap every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 2 (3-4) times, then 3 sts at each side until the work measures 37 (45-52) cm. Bind off.

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 36 (40-40) sts (including sts from the st holders) on double pointed needles around the neck. Join and knit 1 cm stockinette st, then divide the work at the center front. Continue in stockinette st back and forth - but keep the 2 sts at each center front edge in garter st throughout. When the collar measures 6-8 cm knit 2 rows garter st over all sts, bind off loosely (with double yarn if desired). Sew in the sleeves. Sew the sleeve- and the side seam in one.




HAT IN ULL-BOUCLÉ AND ALASKA:

Sizes: years: 6/8 (12/14)
Circumference = 48 (54) cm

Materials: DROPS ALASKA from Garnstudio
100 (100) g color no. 05, coal gray
and use: Garnstudio ULL-BOUCLÉ from Garnstudio
100 (100) g color no. 02, coal gray

DROPS 8 mm double pointed needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge.

Gauge: 10 sts x 14 rows with 1 strand Alaska + 1 strand Ull-boucle in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.

Hat: Cast on 48 (54) sts with 2 strands Alaska + 1 strand Ull-Bouclé on double pointed needles. Join and knit stockinette st with 1 strand Alaska + 1 strand Ull-Bouclé. After 18 (24) cm place 6 markers evenly distributed = 8 (9) sts between the markers. K 2 tog at the left side of each marker across every other row 7 (8) times. After this 6 sts remain on the needles. Continue to knit these for 3 cm. Then pull a strand of Alaska through the sts, pull together well and tie off the strand.
