Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 17 sts x 23 rows on needles size 5 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st. See M.1-M.3. Body piece: M.1, 5-6 (7-8-10) cm red, M.2. Sleeve: 1 round red, M.3 (make sure to place a pattern repeat mid upper sleeve), salmon until sleeve measures 22-26 (30-33-36) cm, M.2.  

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needle.  Cast on 110-118 (126-134-140) sts on circular needle size 3 mm with salmon and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 5 mm and continue in stocking st,  at the same time inc 26-26- (26-34-36) sts evenly on first round = 136-144 (152-168-176) sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 10-12 (15-16-16) cm continue in pattern - see explanation above. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm, divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

Front piece:  = 68-72 (76-84-88) sts. Continue in pattern. When piece measures 36-39 (43-45-47) cm cast off the middle 22-22 (24-26-26) sts for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 2 times. Cast off remaining 19-21 (22-25-27) sts on shoulder after the pattern, piece measures approx 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Back piece: =68-72 (76-84-88) sts. Continue as described for front piece until 2 rows left of M2. Now cast off the middle 30-30 (32-34-34) sts and complete each side separately. Cast off remaining 19-21 (22-25-27) sts on shoulder sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm.  

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 28-28 (30-30-32) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm with red. Knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in pattern – see explanation above. At the same time, inc 6-6 (8-10-10) sts evenly on first round = 34-34 (38-40-42) sts. Now inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 15-17 (18-17-20) times as follows:
Size 3/4 + 5/6 years: on every 3rd round 
Size 7/8 years: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
Size 9/10 + 11/12 years: on every 4th round 
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. Cast off after M/2, sleeve measures approx 26-30 (33-37-40) cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 80-80 (80-86-86) sts round the neck on needle size 3 mm with red and knit 3 cm Rib, cast off.  Set in sleeves.
