Pattern 2: The bobbles in Pattern 2 are knit in different colors as follows: * yellow, green, natural, violet *. Repeat * - *.

Bobble: Increase to 5 sts from 1 st, knit 3 rows back and forth over these sts, on the 4th row knit the 5 sts together.

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter st, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Body: Cast on 114-122-130 (138-146) sts on smaller circular needles with red; join and place a marker at the join. 
Knit 3 cm  rib. Change to larger circular needles, knit 1 row stockinette st and increase 18-22-26 (30-34) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row = 132-144-156 (168-180) sts. 
Knit Pattern 1 and then knit the rest with Pattern 2 (see instructions above). 
Increase 33-36-39 (42-45) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row of Pattern 2 = 165-180-195 (210-225) sts. 
When the work measures 29-31-33 (35-35) cm , divide it as follows: 83-90-98 (105-113) sts for the front and 82-90-97 (105-112) sts for the back. Knit the rest of each part separately. 

Back: = 82-90-97 (105-112) sts. Bind off for armholes every other row: 2 sts 2 times, dec 1 st 1 time = 72-80-87 (95-102) sts. 
When the work measures 47-50-54 (56-58) cm , bind off the center 28-28-29 (31-32) sts for the neck. Then dec 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off all sts when the work measures 48-51-55 (57-59) cm .

Front: = 83-90-98 (105-113) sts. Bind off for armholes as for back = 73-81-88 (96-103) sts. When the work measures 43-46-50 (52-54) cm  bind off the center 7-6-8 (9-11) sts for the neck. Sleeves: Cast on 30-32-32 (34-34) sts on smaller double pointed needles with red; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 4 cm  rib. = 70-74-80 (80-88) sts. After Pattern 3 knit the rest with Pattern 2, increasing 6 sts evenly distributed on the 1st row (see that you get a bobble centered on the sleeve).
