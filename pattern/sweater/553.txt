Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 17 sts x 26 rows on needle size 4.5mm with Uld-Bouclè in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm. Remember the needle sizes are guidelines only!
Garter sts (back and forth): knit all rows.
Rib: * K2, P2 *, repeat from *-*.
Decreasing tips (apply to pocket): Cast off as follows from RS: After 1 edge st: Slip 1 st as if to knit, K1, psso. Before 1 edge st: K2 tog.
Cast off as follows from WS: Before 1 edge st: P2 tog into back of st. After 1 edge st: P2 tog. 

Right front piece: Cast on 31-33-36 (38-41-43) sts (including 1 edge st mid front and 1 edge st at the side) on needle size 4 mm with Uld-Bouclè or Alpaca-Bouclé. Knit 4 rows garter sts. Change to needle size 4.5 mm and continue in stocking sts with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 9-10-11 (12-12-12) cm inc 1 st at the side and repeat this inc when piece measures 18-20-22 (24-24-25) cm = 33-35-38 (40-43-45) sts. When piece measures 28-30-32 (35-36-37) cm cast off for armhole on every other row: 3 sts 1-1-1 (1-2-2) times, 2 sts 2-2-2 (3-2-3) times, 1 sts 3-2-3 (2-3-2) times = 23-26-28 (29-30-31) sts. When piece measures 37-41-44 (48-50-52) cm cast off 5-6-6 (7-7-8) sts mid front for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 3 times = 11-13-15 (15-16-16) sts left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 41-45-48 (53-55-57) cm.

Left front piece: Knit like right front piece, but mirrored.

Back piece: Cast on 60-64-70 (74-80-84) sts (including 1 edge st each side) on needle size 4 mm with Uld-Bouclè or Alpaca-Bouclé. Knit 4 rows garter sts. Change to needle size 4.5 mm, continue in stocking sts with edge sts in garter sts throughout. Inc1 st each side when piece measures 9-10-11 (12-12-12) cm and repeat this inc when piece measures 18-20-22 (24-24-25) cm = 64-68-74 (78-84-88) sts. When piece measures 28-30-32 (35-36-37) cm cast off for armhole as for the front piece = 44-50-54 (56-58-60) sts. Continue until piece measures 39-43-46 (51-53-55) cm. Cast off the middle 20-22-22 (24-24-26) sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row = 11-13-15 (15-16-16) sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when the piece measures 41-45-48 (53-55-57) cm.

Sleeve: Cast on 44-44-48 (48-52-52) sts on double pointed needle size 3 mm with Karisma. Knit 4-4-4 (5-5-5) cm Rib. Change to double pointed needle size 4.5 mm and Uld-Bouclè or Alpaca-Bouclé. Knit stocking sts at the same time dec 10 sts evenly on the first round = 34-34-38 (38-42-42) sts. At the same time inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 3-7-7 (10-10-11) times on every 7th -3.5rd – 4th (3 rd -3.5 rd.-3.5 rd) cm = 40-48-52 (58-62-64) sts. When piece measures 26-30-33 (37-40-43) cm cast off 4-6-6 (6-6-6) sts mid under arm and finish the sleeve back and forth on needle. Dec for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 2 sts 1-2-2 (3-3-2) times, 1 st 5-3-2 (2-3-6) times and then 2 sts each side until sleeve measures 33-37-40 (45-49-53) cm. Cast off.

Pocket: Cast on 26-27-29 (31-32-34) sts on needles size 4 mm with Uld-Bouclè or Alpaca-Bouclé. Knit 2 rows garter sts. Change to needle size 4.5 mm and continue in stocking sts with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. When piece measures 6-7-7 (8-8-9) cm cast off 1 st inside the edge st on the one side (= pocket opening – see Decreasing tips) a total of 10-9-9 (8-8-10) times on every row and thereafter 1 st 8-10-11 (13-14-13) times on every other row = 8-8-9 (10-10-11) sts left on needle. Cast off, the piece measures approx 16-18-19 (21-22-23) cm. Knit another pocket with decreases on the opposite side.

Assembly: Sew the pockets on the jersey above the edge in garter sts and mid front. Sew the shoulder seems. Pick up approx 92-108 sts (divisible by 4) round the neck on needle size 3 mm with Karisma. Knit Rib with 1 garter st each side towards mid front until neck measures 5-5-6 (6-7-7) cm, cast off. Pick up sts up along the front band on needle size 3 mm with Karisma - start from the bottom edge and continue up over the neck edge (pick up sts through both layers where the pocket is attached) = approx 100 -140 sts. Knit Rib with 1 garter st each side for 1 cm, cast off. Repeat along the left front band. Sew the side seams within 1 edge st, sew on the sleeves and the zip.
