DROPS crochet hook size 3 (for borders)
DROPS Inka button, no 531, 2 pcs

Knitting tension: 21 sts x 28 rows in stocking sts on needle size 4 mm = 10 x 10 cm
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: See diagram M.1. The entire pattern is knitted in stocking sts. English Translations for the wording in the diagrams are given at the bottom of this pattern, and they are given in the vertical order in which they appear in the diagram

Back and front piece: Cast on 112-128-144-160 sts with blue on circular needle size 2.5 mm and knit 3-3-4-4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in stocking sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 13-14-17-21 cm knit M.1. After M.1 finish the piece in corn blue. When piece measures 19-20-23-27 cm cast off 6 sts each side for armhole = 50-58-66-74 sts left on front and back piece respectively. Now finish each piece separately. 

Front piece: = 50-58-66-74 sts. Dec for armhole each side on every other row: 2 sts 1-1-2-2 times and 1 st 3-4-3-4 times = 40-46-52-58 sts. When piece measures 26-28-31-36 cm cast off the middle 10-12-14-16 sts for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time and 1 st 4 times = 9-11-13-15 sts left on each shoulder. Continue as follows:
Right shoulder: cast off when piece measures 30-32-36-41 cm
Left shoulder: When piece measures 30-32-36-41 cm change to needle size 2.5 mm and knit Rib over the remaining sts on shoulder. When Rib measures 0.5 cm make 1 buttonhole mid shoulder – cast off 1 st and cast on 1 new st in the same place on the return row. Cast off the remaining sts in Rib on next row. 

Back piece: 50-58-66-74 sts. Dec for armhole as described for front piece = 40-46-52-58 sts. When piece measures 28-30-34-39 cm cast off the middle 20-22-24-26 sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row = 9-11-13-15 sts left on each shoulder.
Continue as follows:
Right shoulder: cast off when piece measures 30-32-36-41 cm
Left shoulder: When piece measures 30-32-36-41 cm change to needle size 2.5 mm and knit 2 cm Rib and cast off. 

Sleeve: Cast on 36-38-40-42 sts on double pointed needle size 2.5 mm with blue and knit 3-3-4-4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 4 mm and knit stocking sts. At the same time after the Rib inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 3-5-5-7 times on every 16-9-9-7 row = 42-48-50-56 sts. When sleeve measures 11-13-15-18 cm knit M.1 – make sure to get a berry mid upper arm. After M.1finish the sleeve in corn blue. At the same time when sleeve measures 17-19-20-23 cm cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row as follows and finish the sleeve back and forth on needle: 3 sts1 time, 2 sts 1-2-1-2 times, 1 st 4-3-6-4 times, 2 sts 1-2-1-2 times and 3 sts 1 time. Cast off when sleeve measures 23-25-27-30 cm. 

Assembly: Sew right shoulder seam. Pick up approx 60-70 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with corn blue and knit 2-2-3-3 cm Rib back and forth on needle – at the same time after 0.5 cm make a buttonhole as described for left shoulder on front piece. Cast off. Sew sleeve seams and sew on buttons.

HAT:
Size: 0/6 months – 1 – 2/4 – 5/6 – 7/8 years
To fit head circumference: approx 42-45-48-50-52 cm

Materials: DROPS Karisma Superwash from Garnstudio
50 g for all sizes, colour no 07, corn blue or colour no 18, red
50 for all sizes, colour no 47, dark olive

DROPS double pointed needles size 3.5 mm + size 2 mm (for the top)

Knitting Tension: 22 sts x 30 rows on needle size 3.5 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm
Pattern: See diagrams M.2 and M.3. 1 diagram = 1 pattern repeat. The diagram is seen from the RS

Cast on 84-90-96-102-108 sts with blue or red on double pointed needle size 3.5 mm. P 1 round and continue in stocking st. When piece measures 6 cm insert a marking thread (= rolling edge) and measure piece from this point onwards. Knit M.2. When piece measures 7-8-9-10-11 cm dec 3-0-6-3-0 sts evenly = 81-90-90-99-108 sts. Now insert 9-10-10-11-12 marking threads in piece with 9 sts between each MT. First st after the first MT = first st in M.3. After M.3 finish the hat in olive green, at the same time dec 1 st to the right of all MT on every other round until there are 9-10-10-11-12 sts left on needle. Change to needle size 2 mm and knit 3 cm over all sts. Cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. Sew tight.

MITTENS:

Size: 2/4 – 5/6 – 7/8 years

Materials: DROPS Karisma Superwash from Garnstudio
50-100-100 g for all sizes, colour no 18, red

DROPS double pointed needles size 2.5 and 3 mm 

Knitting Tension: 23 sts x 32 rows on needle size 3 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm

Rib: *K1/P1*, repeat from *-*.

Cast on 44-48-54 sts on needles size 2.5 mm and knit 10-12-14 cm Rib. Change to needle size 3 mm and continue in stocking st, at the same time dec 8 sts evenly = 36-40-46 sts. Measure piece from this point onwards. When piece measures 1 cm inc for thumb as follows: insert a marking thread, inc 1 st by knitting 2 sts in next st, K1, inc 1 st by knitting 2 sts in next st, insert a marking thread, finish the round. Now inc 1 st on the inner side of each MT on every other round a total of 3-4-5 times = 11-13-15 thumb sts. When piece measures 3-4-5 cm put the 11-13-15 thumb sts on a thread. Cast on 3 new sts behind the thumb sts = 36-40-46 sts. Continue in stocking sts. When piece measures 5-7-9 cm knit next round as follows: 14-15-18 sts (= upper hand), put 8-10-10 sts on a thread for little finger, knit 14-15-18 sts (= palm of hand). Now knit the fingers on needle size 2.5 mm

Little finger: put the 8-10-10 sts from thread back on needle and cast on 3 new sts towards ring finger = 11-13-13 sts. Knit 3-3.5-4 cm in stocking st, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. 
Ring finger: Take 4-5-5 sts from upper hand sts and 4-4-6 sts from palm sts, pick up 3 new sts towards little finger and cast on 2 new sts towards middle finger = 13-14-16 sts. Knit 5-6-7 cm stocking sts, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. 
Middle finger: Take 5-5-6 sts from upper hand sts and 4-5-6 sts from palm sts, pick up 3 new sts towards ring finger and cast on 2 new sts towards index finger = 14-15-17 sts. Knit 6-7-8 cm stocking sts, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. 
Index finger: Take remaining sts from upper hand and palm sts and pick up 3 new sts towards middle finger = 14-14-16 sts. Knit 5-6-7 cm stocking sts, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts. 
Thumb: Put the 11-13-15 thumb sts from thread on needle and pick up 3 sts behind these = 14-16-18 sts. Knit 3-4-5 cm stocking sts, knit all sts tog 2 by 2, cut the thread and pull through remaining sts.
