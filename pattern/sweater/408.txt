Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 10 sts x 16 rows in stocking sts on needle size 8 mm with Highlander = 10 x 10 cm
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Garter sts, back and forth: Knit all rows. 

JUMPER:
Front piece: Cast on 66-76-80-84 sts (incl 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3.5 mm with red/pink Karisma and knit 2 cm Rib with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. Continue as follows: 2 rows garter sts in orange Alaska, 2 rows garter sts in dark pink Paris and 2 rows garter sts in red/pink Karisma. Change to needle size 8 mm and Highlander. Knit 1 row stocking sts from RS, at the same time knitting all sts tog 2 by 2 = 33-38-40-42 sts. Continue in reverse stocking sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 18-19-21-23 continue in stocking sts. At the same time when piece measures 23-25-28-30 cm cast off for armhole each side on every other row: 3 sts 1 time and 1 st 2-3-3-2 times = 23-26-28-32 sts. When piece measures 27-29-32-35 cm finish the piece in reverse stocking sts. When piece measures 31-34-37-40 cm cast off the middle 5-6-8-8 sts for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 1 st 3-3-3-3 times = 6-7-7-9 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 36-39-43-46 cm. 

Back piece: Cast on and knit as front piece. Cast off for armhole as described for front piece and continue until piece measures 34-37-41-44 cm. Now cast off the middle 9-10-12-12 sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row = 6-7-7-9 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 36-39-43-46 cm. 

Sleeve: Cast on 38-40-42-44 sts (incl 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3.5 mm with red/pink Karisma and knit 2 cm Rib with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. Continue as follows: 2 rows garter sts in orange Alaska, 2 rows garter sts in dark pink Paris and 2 rows garter sts in red/pink Karisma. Change to needle size 8 mm and Highlander. Knit 1 row stocking sts from RS, at the same time knitting all sts tog 2 by 2 = 19-20-21-22 sts. Continue in reverse stocking sts, at the same time when piece measures 5 cm inc 1 st each side a total of 4-4-5-5 times on every 4.5-5.5-5-6 cm = 27-28-31-32 sts. When sleeve measures 22-24-28-32 cm cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 2 sts 2-2-2-3 times, 1 st 1-3-2-0 times and 2 sts until piece measures 28-31-35-38 cm. Cast of remaining sts. 

Assembly: Sew right shoulder seam. Pick up approx 28 to 38 sts round the neck on needle size 8 mm with Highlander. Change to needle size 3.5 mm and red/pink Karisma and knit 1 row in stocking sts, at the same inc 1 st in each st (i.e. knit 2 sts in all sts) = approx 56 to 76 sts. Knit 2 rows in garter sts, change to pink Paris and knit 2 rows garter sts, change to orange Alaska and knit 2 rows garter sts. Change back to red/pink Karisma and knit 1 row stocking st (from RS). Cast off from WS in knitted sts. Sew left shoulder, but not up along the neck. Set in sleeves and sew side and sleeve seams inside 1 edge st. Crochet a chain of 4 ch with Karisma on the neck on front piece and sew on button on back piece. 

Crochet flowers: Crochet 2 flowers in Alaska, 2 flowers in Paris and 2 flowers in Karisma with crochet hook size 4.5 mm as follows:
Crochet 4 ch and form a ring with a sl st.
1st row: 5 dc in ring and finish with 1 sl st and 1 ch.
2nd row: *3 ch, 1 dc + 1 tr in 3rd ch from needle, 1 dc in next dc*, repeat from *-* a total of 5 times. Cut the thread.
Sew on flowers to front piece.  

HAT:
Size: 1/3 – 4/6 years
To fit head circumference: approx 46 - 48 cm

The hat is knitted in 2 pieces and sewn tog afterwards. Cast on 50-52 sts (incl 1 edge st each side) on needle size 3.5 mm with Karisma. Knit 2 cm Rib with 1 edge st each side in garter sts. Continue as follows: 2 rows garter sts in orange Alaska, 2 rows garter sts in dark pink Paris and 2 rows garter sts in red/pink Karisma. Change to needle size 8 mm and Highlander. Knit 1 row stocking sts from RS, at the same time knitting all sts tog 2 by 2 = 25-26 sts. Continue in reverse stocking sts. When piece measures 12-14 cm inc 1 st each side a total of 5 times on every other row = 35-36 sts. Cast off when piece measures 18-20 cm. Knit a similar piece.

Assembly: Sew hat tog mid back with mattress sts. Sew side seams. Crochet 1 flower in Alaska, 1 flower in Paris and 1 flower in Karisma (see explanation under jumper) and sew flowers to front piece of hat. Make 2 plaits, 1 in orange Alaska and 1 in pink Paris with 9 threads (3 threads in each section) and attach to each top corner of hat.
