Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 24 sts x 32 rows on larger needles in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm .

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter st, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Body: Cast on 168-180-192-216 (228-240-252) sts with mixed on smaller circular needles; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 3-3-3-4 (4-4-4) rows garter st. Change to larger circular needles and stockinette st. When the work measures 28-29-31-34 (35-36-39) cm  knit the next row as follows: bind off 2 sts for armhole, 80-86-92-104 (110-116-122) sts for the front, bind off 4 sts for armhole, 80-86-92-104 (110-116-122) sts for the back, bind off 2 sts for armhole. Knit the rest of the front and back separately.

Front: = 80-86-92-104 (110-116-122) sts. Bind off the 2 center sts for zipper and knit the rest of each side separately = 39-42-45-51 (54-57-60) sts each side. Knit the 2 sts at the center front edge in garter st throughout. 
Bind off for armholes at each side every other row: 3 sts 0-0-1-2 (2-3-3) times, 2 sts 2-2-1-2 (3-2-3) times, 1 st 2-3-3-2 (2-3-3) times = 33-35-37-39 (40-41-42) sts. When the work measures 36-40-43-47 (49-51-54) cm  bind off at each neck edge every other row: 5-6-8-9 (9-9-10) sts 1 time, 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2-2-2-2 (3-3-3) times. Bind off when the work measures 41-45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm .

Back: = 80-86-92-104 (110-116-122) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front: 68-72-76-80 (82-84-86) sts. When the work measures 39-43-46-51 (53-55-58) cm  bind off the center 22-24-28-30 (32-32-34) sts for the neck. Bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 41-45-48-53 (55-57-60) cm .

Sleeve: Cast on 40-44-46-46 (48-50-50) sts on smaller double pointed needles with mixed; join and place a marker at the join. Knit 3-3-3-4 (4-4-4) rows garter st. Change to larger double pointed needles and stockinette st. After the garter st border, inc 1 st each side of marker 12-17-18-23 (24-26-28) times:
Sizes 2 + 5/6 years: every 5th row
Sizes 3/4 + 7/8 + 9/10 + 11/12 + 13/14 years: every 4th row 
= 64-78-82-92 (96-102-106) sts. When the work measures 24-28-32-34 (38-40-42) cm  bind off 2 sts each side of marker and knit the rest of the work back and forth on the needles. Bind off for sleeve cap at each side every other row: 4 sts 1 time, 1 st 4-4-4-7(8-10-12) times, 2 sts 2 times, 4 sts 1 time, bind off. The work measures approx. 29-33-37-41 (46-49-52) cm .

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seam. Pick up approx. 80-110 sts around the neck on smaller needles with beige and knit 4 rows garter st, bind off. Sew in the sleeves. Sew in the zipper.
