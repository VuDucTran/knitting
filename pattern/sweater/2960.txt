Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 16 sts x 19 rows in stocking st on needle size 5.5 mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: * K1, P1 *

Pattern : see diagram below.
1 square = 1 st x 1 row.
1 mønster = 1 repeat, 41 rows.
1 rapport = 1 repeat across the row, 24 sts

BODY PIECE: Cast on 120-132 (144-156-168) sts on circular needle size 3 mm with main colour and work 6 cm rib. Change to circular needle size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly to 124-136 (140-152-160) sts on first round. Remember the knitting tension.  When piece measures 18-22 (26-30-32) cm work 1 pattern repeat (beg by arrow and work pattern on front piece, beg by arrow again and work pattern on back piece). AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 22-25 (27-29-30) cm divide piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately.

FRONT PIECE: = 62-68 (70-76-80) sts. Continue in pattern and cast off all sts after the last pattern row. Piece measures approx 39-43 (47-51-53) cm. 

BACK PIECE: Like front piece.

SLEEVE: Cast on 34-36 (38-38-40) sts on needle size 3 mm with main colour and work 6 cm rib back and forth on needle. Change to needle size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly to 34-38 (42-46-50) sts on first row. Now inc 1 st each side a total of 10-10 (11-12-12) times on every 3-3 (4-4-5) row = 54-58 (64-70-74) sts. When sleeve measures 18-21 (26-30-33) cm work Pattern II. Cast off after last pattern row, sleeve measures approx 24-27 (32-36-39) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams = 10-11.5 (12-13.5-14.5) cm. 
Pick up approx 66-70 (72-74-76) sts round neck on small circular needle size 3 mm with main colour and work 2 cm rib, cast off. Set in sleeves. Steam iron jumper lightly on WS.
