Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: 20 sts x 26 rows on needle size 4 mm in stocking st = 10 x 10 cm 
Rib: *K1, P1*, repeat from *-*.  
Pattern: The diagram shows the pattern from the RS side. 

JUMPER:
Knit in the round instead of back and forth on needle. Cast on 8 less sts and inc 8 more sts after Rib.

JACKET:

Back and front piece: Knitted back and forth on circular needle. Cast on 138-142-152 (162-172-182) sts on circular needle size 2.5 mm with blue/blue-grey/wine red and work 4 cm Rib, but after 1 row change to blue-grey/off-white/red. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in pattern (beg by arrow), at the same time inc 6-14-16 (18-20-22) sts evenly on first row = 144-156-168 (180-192-204) sts. Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 23-20-22 (24-25-26) divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

Front piece: = 36-39-42 (45-48-51) sts. When piece measures 34-35-38 (42-44-46) dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 11-14-14 (15-16-16) sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 38-39-42 (46-48-50) cm. 

Back piece: = 72-78-84 (90-96-102) sts. When piece measures 36-37-40 (44-46-48) cm cast off the middle 28-34-34 (36-38-38) sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row.  Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 38-39-42 (46-48-50) cm. 

Sleeves:  Cast on 36-36-38 (40-42-44) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm blue/blue-grey/wine red and work 4 cm Rib, but after 1 round change to blue-grey/ off-white/red. Change to needle size 4 mm and continue in pattern beg by arrow), at the same time inc 4-4-2 (4-6-6) sts evenly on first round = 40-40-40 (44-48-50) sts. At the same time after Rib inc 2 sts mid under arm 10-18-20 (22-22-23) times as follows:
Size 2 years: on every 4th and 5th round alternately
Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8 years: on 3rd round 
Size 9/10 years + 11/12 years: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
= 60-76-80 (88-92-96) sts. Cast off when sleeve measures approx 23-26-30 (34-37-40) cm – adjust to pattern.  

Assembly: Join shoulders. Pick up approx 80-100 along left front piece on needle size 2.5 mm with blue-grey/off-white/red and work 3 cm Rib back and forth on needle, cast off. Repeat along right front piece, but after 1 cm make 6-7 buttonholes evenly distributed. 1 buttonhole = cast off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts on return row. Pick up approx 78-90 sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with blue-grey/off-white/red and work 2.5 cm Rib, change to blue/blue-grey/wine red, work 1 row and cast off. Set in sleeves.
