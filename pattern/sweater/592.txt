Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 25 sts x 32 rows on needle size 3 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm.
Moss stich: 1st row: K1, P1. 2nd row: Knit K over P and P over K. Repeat 1st and 2nd row.
Garter st (back and forth): 1st row: K, 2nd row: K.
M.1: See diagram. Knit the entire pattern in stocking st (seen from RS).
M.2: See diagram. Knit the entire pattern as follows (seen from RS): * 2 sts stocking st, 3 sts moss sts*, repeat from *-*.
Buttonhole: 1 buttonhole = cast off the third st from mid front, on next row make a yo over cast off st. Cast off for buttonholse when piece measures 1-1-1 (1-1-1) cm, 5-6-6 (7-8-8) cm, 9-10-12 (13-14-15) cm, 14-15-17 (19-21-23) cm, 18-19-23 (25-27-30) cm and 22-24-28 (31-34-37) cm.
Crochet edge: 1 dc, * crochet 4 ch, 1 dc + 1 tr in first ch, skip 3 st and crochet 1 dc *, repeat from *-*.

Back and front piece: Knitted back and forth on circular needle from mid front. Cast on 135-141-153 (171-183-189) sts (incl 4 front band sts each side towards mid front) on circular needle size 3 mm in red. Knit 4 rows garter st with 4 front band sts mid front in moss st (front band is knitted in moss st and red throughout - remember to twist the threads at colour change when knitting pattern on sweater, and remember buttonhole on right front band, see explanation above). Continue in M.1 (see explanation above). Remember the knitting tension. After M.1 increase 3-4-7 (9-7-11) sts evenly on row =138-145-160 (180-190-200) sts. Continue in M.2 until piece is completed (see explanation above). When piece measures 17-18-21 (24-25-26) cm knit next row as follows (seen from RS): 33-35-39 (44-46-49) sts = front piece, cast off 6 sts for armhole, 60-63-70 (80-86-90) sts = back, cast off 6 sts for armhole, 33-35-39-(44-46-49) sts = front piece. Complete each part separately.

Left front piece: = 33-35-39 (44-46-49) sts. Cast off for armhole on every other row: 2 sts 3-3-3 (4-3-3) times, 1 st 3-3-3 (2-2-2) times = 24-26-30 (34-38-41) sts. When piece measures 23-25-29 (32-35-38) cm slip the 4 front band sts on a thread and on the same row cast off the next 3-4-5 (6-7-7) sts for neckline. Dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 3-3-3 (3-3-4) times, 1 st 3-3-4 (4-5-4) times = 8-9-11 (14-16-18) sts left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 27-29-33 (37-40-43) cm.

Right front piece: Knit like left – remember buttonholes.

Back: = 60-63-70 (80-86-90) sts. Cast off for armhole as described for front piece = 42-45-52 (60-70-74) sts. When piece measures 25-27-31 (35-38-41) cm cast off the middle 22-23-26 (28-34-34) sts for neck and cast off 2 sts on next row towards neckline = 8-9-11 (14-16-18) sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 27-29-33 (37-40-43) cm (adjust to pattern on front piece).

Sleeve: Cast on 38-40-43 (43-48-48) sts on double pointed needle size 3 mm in red and P 1 round, then  3 cm moss st. Continue in  M.2 – at the same time inc 2-2-4 (4-4-4) sts evenly distributed on first  round = 40-42-47 (47-52-52) sts. Continue inc 2 sts mid under arm: 3-4-4 (7-9-14) times on every 4.5-4-4.5 (3-2.5.-2) cm = 46-50-55 (61-70-80) sts – fit increased sts into pattern as you go along. When piece measures 17-20-22 (25-29-33) cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm and complete sleeve back and forth on needle. Cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row: 3 st 0-0-0 (0-1-2) times, 2 st 1-1-2 (2-3-4) times, 1 st 7-7-5 (4-2-0) times, 2 sts 1-1-2 (3-3-0) times, 3 sts 1-1-1 (1-1-3) times = 12-16-17 (21-24-28) sts left. Cast off remaining sts, piece now measures approx 24-27-29 (32-36-39) cm.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Gather approx 60-85 sts up on needle size 3 mm on neckline (incl sts on threads) with red. Knit  4 rows garter st, cast off.
