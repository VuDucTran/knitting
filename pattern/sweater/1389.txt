Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting Tension: 17 sts x 22 rows on needle size 5 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm 
Moss st: first row: K1, P1*, repeat from *-*. Second row: P over K and K over P. Repeat second row. 
Pattern: The diagram shows the pattern from the RS side. 

Back and front piece: Knitted in the round on circular needle. Cast on 110-114-122 (138-146-154) sts on circular needle size 3.5 mm with red and work 3 cm moss st.  Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in M.1, at the same time inc 10-18-22 (18-22-26) sts evenly on first round = 120-132-144 (156-168-180) sts. After M.1 continue in M.2.  Remember the knitting tension. When piece measures 29-31-34 (37-39-40) cm divide the piece in two and complete front and back pieces separately. 

Front piece: = 60-66-72 (78-84-90) sts. When piece measures 39-45-49 (54-57-59) cm cast off the middle 20-22-22 (24-26-26) sts for neck, continue in M.3 on either side of neck, at the same time dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 2 times. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 44-50-54 (59-62-64) cm. 

Back piece: = 60-66-72 (78-84-90) sts. When piece measures 39-45-49 (54-57-59) cm continue in M.3. When 5 rows left of M.3 cast off the middle 26-28-28 (30-32-32) sts for neck and dec 1 st on neckline on next row. Cast off remaining sts when piece measures 44-50-54 (59-62-64) cm. 

Sleeves:  Cast on 30-30-32 (32-34-36) sts on double pointed needles size 3.5 mm with red and work 3 cm moss st. Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in M.4, at the same time inc 4-4-2 (6-6-8) sts evenly on first round = 34-34-34 (39-40-42) sts. At the same time after the moss st inc 2 sts mid under arm 9-15-17 (18-19-20) times as follows:
Size 2 years: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
Size 3/4 + 5/6 + 7/8 years: on every other and 3rd round alternately
Size 9/10 years + 11/12 years: on every 3rd round 
= 52-64-68 (74-78-82) sts. After M.4 complete piece in M.2. When sleeve measures 20-22-25 (28-31-34) cm cast off 4 sts mid under arm and complete sleeve back and forth on needle. Dec to shape the sleeve cap each side on every other row: 4 sts 2 times. Cast off, sleeve measures approx 23-25-28 (31-34-37) cm. 

Assembly: Join shoulders. Pick up approx 70-86 sts round the neck on needle size 3.5 mm with red and work 3 cm moss st, change to rust, work 1 round and cast off. Now crochet a border round neck with crochet hook size 3.5 mm and rust as follows: 1 dc,* 3ch, 1 tr in first ch, skip 2 sts, 1 dc*, repeat form *-*. Set in sleeves.
