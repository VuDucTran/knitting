Sizes: 5/6 (8/10-12/14) years
Finished measurements:
Chest: 80 (92-104) cm 
Waist: 74 (86-96) cm 

Materials: DROPS LEOPARD,
100% acrylic, 100 g./110 m./120 yards
300 (400-500) g color no. 26 green/mixed

Alternate yarns:
* DROPS ULL-FLAMÉ, 100% wool, 50 g./50 m
* DROPS PUDDEL, 64% mohair, 20% wool, 16% viscose, 50 g./50 m.

DROPS 6 mm  needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge. Gauge: 11 sts x 19 row in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.
Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.

Front: Cast on 43 (49-55) sts. Knit 2 rows garter st, then continue in stockinette st. Inc 1 st at each side 2 times every 7 (8-9) cm = 47 (53-59) sts. When the work measures 21 (24-27) cm  bind off for armhole at each side every other row: 2 sts 2 (2-3) times, 1 st 2 (4-3) times = 35 (37-41) sts.
When the work measures 29 (33-35) cm knit 2 rows of garter st over the center 3 sts. Then bind off the center st for the V-neck. Knit the rest of each side separately.

Dec at each neck edge as follows:
Right side: Before 2 edge sts, K 2 tog.
Left side: After 2 edge sts, slip 1, K 1, psso.
Dec 1 st inside 2 edge sts which are knit in garter st throughout, every other row 5 (4-4) times, then every 4th row 2 (3-4) times. Bind off when the work measures 39 (45-49) cm.

Back: Cast on and knit as for the front. When the work measures 38 (44-48) cm knit 2 rows of garter st over the center 15 (15-17) sts and then bind off all sts.

Sleeve: Cast on 24 (25-26) sts. Knit 2 rows garter st, then continue in stockinette st. When the work measures 4 (5-6) cm inc 1 st at each side 9 (12-12) times every 2.5 (2.5-3) cm = 42 (49-50) sts. When the work measures approx. 30 (38-43) cm bind off at each side for sleeve cap every other row: 2 sts 1 (2-2) times, 1 st 4 (4-5) times, then 2 sts at each side until the work measures 37 (46-52) cm. Bind off.
