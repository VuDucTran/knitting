Sizes: 5/6 (8/10-12/14) years
Finished measurements:
Chest: 80 (92-104) cm 
Waist: 74 (86-96) cm 

Materials: DROPS BIG BOUCLÉ,
60% wool, 40% acrylic, 50 g./50 m
350 (400-500) g color no. 8, red

DROPS 5 mm needles and double pointed needles, or size needed to obtain correct gauge.

Alternate yarns:
* DROPS PUDDEL, 64% mohair, 20% wool, 16% viscose, 50 g./50 m. Gauge: 11 sts x 18 rows in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm.

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter Stitch, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl *. Repeat from * - *.

Front: Cast on 42 (49-55) sts. Knit 4 rows garter st, then continue in stockinette st. Inc 1 st at each side 2 times every 7 (8-9) cm = 46 (53-59) sts. When the work measures 22 (25-28) cm bind off for armholes at each side every other row: 2 sts 1 (2-3) times, 1 st 3 (4-3) times = 36 (37-41) sts. When the work measures 34 (40-43) cm put the center 10 (11-11) sts on a st holder for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge every other row, 3 (3-4) times. Bind off when the work measures 39 (45-49) cm.

Back: Cast on and knit as for the front. When the work measures 37 (43-47) cm 
 put the 14 (15-17) center sts on a st holder for the neck. Dec 1 st at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 39 (45-49) cm .

Sleeve: Cast on 25 (28-30) sts, knit 2 rows garter st, then continue in stockinette st. When the work measures 5 (6-6) cm  inc 1 st at each side (make increases 1 st in from the edge for a smoother seam) 7 (9-9) times every 3 (3-4) cm = 39 (46-48) sts. When the work measures approx. 30 (38-43) cmbind off at each side for the sleeve cap every other row: 2 sts 1 (2-2) times, 1 st 4 (4-5) times, then 2 sts at each side until the work measures 37 (46-52) cm. Bind off.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx. 44 (46-48) sts around the neck (including sts on the st holders) on double pointed needles; join and knit 4 (4-5) cm garter st, bind off.
