Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comTension: 17 sts x 22 rows in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Remember needle sizes are guidelines only!

Pattern: See diagram (1 diagram = 1 pattern repeat)  Diagrams are seen from RS.  Knit entire pattern in stocking sts.

Ridge (back and forth on needle): 1 ridge = 2 rows 1st row: K, 2nd round: K
Ridge (on circular needles): 1 ridge = 2 rounds 1st round: K, 2nd round: P.

Buttonhole: Cast off for buttonhole on right (left) front piece as follows: Cast off 3rd and 4th sts and cast on 2 new sts over cast off sts on next row when piece measures cm: 1-1-1 (2-0,5), 10-10-10,5 (11-10), 19-19-20 (20-19,5), 28-28-29,5 (29-29), 37-37-39 (38-38,5), 46-46-48 (47-48) and - - -(56-57) cm.

Body: 
Figures in () = Gent size. 
Knit jacket back and forth on circular needles.  Cast on 200-212-224 (212-224) sts on circular needles size 5 mm with blue (navy) and knit 1 ridge. Remember buttonholes on right (left) side – see explanation above. Continuing knit Pattern with 1 sts ridge on each side mid front which is knit in base colour of pattern and start at the right most of diagram after ridge. Remember knitting tension.  When piece measures 30-32-33 (36-38) cm knit next row as follows: 50-53-56 (53-56) sts front piece, cast off 4 sts for armhole, 92-98-104 (98-104) sts back piece, cast off 4 sts for armhole, 50-53-56 (53-56) sts front piece. Complete each piece separately.

Front piece: = 50-53-56 (53-56) sts. Continuing cast off for armhole on every other row: 2 sts -2-2-2 (1-1) times, 1 st 1-2-3 (1-2) times = 45-47-47 (50-52) sts. When piece measures 47-47-49 (57-58) cm cast off to shape neckline on every other row: 6-6-6 (10-10) sts 1 time, 3 sts 2 times, 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2-3-3 (1-2) times. Cast off when piece measures 55-57-59 (63-66) cm

Back piece: = 92-98-104 (98-104) sts. Continuing cast off for armhole like front piece = 82-86-86 (92-96) sts. When piece measures 53-55-57 (61-64) cm cast off middle 24-26-26 (30-32) sts to shape neckline. Then cast off 2 sts on each side on next row to shape neckline. Cast off when piece measures 55-57-59 (63-66) cm

Sleeve: Cast on 38-38-38 (40-40) sts on double pointed needles size 5 mm with blue (navy) and knit 1 ridge. Continuing, knit Pattern (ensure to place pattern repeat mid sleeve). At the same time when piece measures 5-5-5 (5-5) cm inc mid under arm: 2 sts 24-24-25 (26-28) times on every 3rd round = 86-86-88 (92-96) sts. When piece measures 43-42-43 (47-48) cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm and complete knitting back and forth on needle. Continuing cast off to shape sleeve cap on every other row: 4 sts 6 times, cast off. Piece now measures approx 50-49-50 (54-55) cm.

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 100-104 sts round neck line on circular needles size 5 mm with blue (navy) and knit 1 ridge back and forth on needle, cast off. Crochet edge: Crochet with double yarn from RS with blue (navy) and crochet hook size 5 mm: 1 row dc around ridge edge along bottom of body, up along front bad and around neck distributed as follows: 110-120 dc around body, 50 (60) dc along each front band, 50 (50) dc around neckline. Then crochet a row from RS as follows: * Pick yarn under through dc, loosen sts slightly, (approx 2 cm high), now twist crochet hook 360 degrees anti clock wise, make a new loop on crochet hook and pull this through both sts on hook *. Repeat from *-*. Repeat both rows along bottom sleeve edge, approx 22-24 dc. Alternatively crocket as follows: Crochet one row dc as explained above. Crochet a row of dc the opposite way, i.e. from left to right without twisting piece. Crochet dc from RS in each dc from previous row. Sew in sleeves and buttons.
