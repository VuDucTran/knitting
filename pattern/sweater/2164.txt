Sizes: 
child’s, 8/10 - 12/14 years
(women’s Small/Medium - Medium/Large - Xlarge)
Finished Measurements: 92-104 (112-120-126) cm.

Materials: DROPS PELLIZA, 
100% Polyester
450-500 (550-600650) g in color No. 10, Black.
and: DROPS VAMP, 
100% Acrylic,
50-50 (100-100-100) g in Color No. 01, Rust.

DROPS 4mm and 5mm needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 23 sts x 30 rows on smaller needles with Pelliza in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm . 
12 sts x 16 rows on larger needles with Vamp in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm. 

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter Stitch, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Front: Cast on 92-104 (114-120-124) sts on smaller needles with Pelliza and knit 4 rows garter stitch then stockinette stitch. At the same time, inc 1 st each side 8-9 (8-10-12) times: 
Sizes 8/10 + 12/14 years: every 3 cm . 
Sizes S/M + M/L: every 3 cm . 
Size XL: alternately every 2 cm  and every 3 cm . 
= 108-122 (130-140-148) sts. 
When the work measures 25-27 (31-31-32) cm , bind off for armholes on each side every other row: 3 sts 2-2 (1-2-2) time(s), 2 sts 2-2 (3-3-4) times, 1 st 3-3 (3-3-4) times. 82-96 (106-110-112) sts. 
When the work measures 40-44 (50-51-52) cm , bind off the center 18-20 (20-22-24) sts for the neck. Bind off at each neck edge every other row: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1-1 (2-2-2) times, 1 st 3 times. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (55-56-57) cm . 

Back: Cast on and knit like the front, but when the work measures 43-47 (53-54-55) cm , bind off the center 30-32 (36-38-40) sts for the neck. Then bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (55-56-57) cm . 

Sleeves: Cast on 28-30 (34-36-38) sts on larger needles with Vamp and knit 10-11 (12-13-14) cm  in reverse stockinette stitch. Measure the work from here.
Change to smaller needles and Pelliza and inc to 48-52 (52-56-60) sts evenly distributed on the 1st row. Knit in stockinette stitch. When the work measures 5 cm , inc 1 st at each side 23-26 (30-30-28) times:
Sizes 8/10 + 12/14 years: every 4th row. 
Sizes S + M + L: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row. 
= 94-104 (112-116-116) sts. When the work measures 38-44 (44-43-43) cm , bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 3 sts 11-11 (12-14-15) times. Bind off. The work measures approximately 45-51 (52-52-53) cm .

Assembly: Sew right shoulder seam. Pick up approximately 50-60 sts around the neck on smaller needles with Vamp and knit 3 cm  in garter stitch. Bind off. Sew the left shoulder seam. Sew the sleeves to the body. Sew sleeve and side seam in one.
