Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 17 sts x 23 rows on needles size 5 mm in pattern = 10 x 10 cm. 
Rib: *K1 into back of st, P1*, repeat from *-*.
Pattern: the entire pattern is knitted in stocking st.  

Front and back piece: Knitted in the round on circular needle.  Cast on 110-118 (126-134-140) sts on circular needle size 2.5 mm with white and knit 5 cm Rib. Change to circular needle size 5 mm and continue in M.1, at the same time inc 30-32 (34-36-40) sts evenly on first round = 140-150 (160-170-180) sts. Remember the knitting tension! After M.1, knit M.2. When piece measures 21-23 (25-27-27) cm dec 1 st each side on size 3/4, 7 /8 and 11/12, divide piece in two for all sizes and complete front and back pieces separately.  

Front piece:  = 69-75 (79-85-89) sts. When piece measures 31-34 (36-38-38) cm cast off the middle st and complete each side separately. Dec 1 st 15-15 (16-17-17) times to shape the neckline as follows: 
Size 3/4 and 5/6: on every row
Size 7/8 and 9/10: *3 times on every row, 1 row without dec*
Size 11/12: *3 times on every row, 2 rows without dec*
Cast off the remaining 19-22 (23-25-27) sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Back piece: = 69-75 (79-85-89) sts. When piece measures 38-41 (45-47-49) cm cast off the middle 29-29 (31-33-33) sts and dec 1 st towards neckline on next row. Cast off the remaining 19-22 (23-25-27) sts when piece measures 40-43 (47-49-51) cm. 

Sleeve: Knitted round on double pointed needles. Cast on 28-28 (30-30-32) sts on double pointed needles size 2.5 mm with white and knit 4 cm Rib. Change to needle size 5 mm and continue in M.1, at the same time, inc 2-2 (10-10-8) sts evenly on first round = 30-30 (40-40-40) sts. On round 10 in M.1 inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 17-19 (17-17-21) times as follows:
Size 3/4: on every other round
Size 5/6: on *every other, other, 3rd round*
Size 7/8 + 11/12: on every 3rd round
Size 9/10: on every 3rd and 4th round alternately
= 64-68 (74-74-82) sts. After M.1 complete piece in M.2. Cast off when sleeve measures 26-29 (33-36-40) cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seam.  Pick up approx 74-78 (78-84-90) sts round the neck on needle size 2.5 mm with white and knit 6-8 (8-10-10) cm Rib (for a boy knit only 4 cm Rib) – make sure to have a K st mid front and dec on every 3rd round as follows: knit to st before middle st, put middle st on cable needle in front of piece, K tog st before middle st and st after middle st, pass over middle st.
