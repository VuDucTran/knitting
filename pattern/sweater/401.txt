Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 21 sts x 28 rows on needle size 4 mm in stocking sts = 10 x 10 cm.
Garter sts (back and forth on needle): Knit all rows. 
Pattern: See diagrams M.1-M.3. The diagram shows the pattern from the right side.   
Buttonhole: Make buttonholes on right front edge from the RS: cast off 3rd st from the edge and cast on 1 new st on the return row. Make buttonholes as follows:
Size 1 year: 7, 12, 17, 22 and 26.5 cm
Size 2 years: 7, 13, 18, 23 and 28.5 cm
Size 3/4 years: 7, 13, 19, 25 and 30.5 cm
Size 5/6 years: 7, 14, 20, 26 and 32.5 cm
Casting off tips: When casting off for sleeve cap, and knitting pattern at the same time, make sure not to make more yo’s than cast off sts. 

Back and front piece: The jacket is knitted back and forth on circular needle from mid front. Cast on 139-149-169-179 sts (incl 4 edge sts each side towards mid front) on needle size 4 mm. Knit 1 vertical repeat of M.3 with all edge sts in garter sts. Continue in stocking sts with edge sts in garter sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 7 cm start making buttonholes on right front edge – see above. When piece measures 18-20-21-22 cm knit next row as follows: knit 34-36-41-44 sts (= front piece), cast off 6 sts for armhole, knit 59-65-75-79 sts (= back piece), cast off 6 sts for armhole, knit 34-36-41-44 sts (= front piece). Now complete each piece separately.  

Left front: = 34-36-41-44 sts. Cast off for armholes on every other row: 2 sts 1-1-2-2 times and 1 st 4-5-4-4 times = 28-29-33-36 sts. When piece measures 27-29-31-33 cm cast off 7-7-8-9 sts mid front for neck and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 2 sts 2-2-2-2 times and 1 st 4-4-4-5 times = 13-14-17-18 sts left on shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 31-33-36-38 cm. 

Right front: = 34-36-41-44 sts. Continue as for left front – remember buttonholes!

Back: = 59-65-75-79 sts. Cast off for armholes each side as described for front piece = 47-51-59-63 sts. When piece measures 29-31-34-36 cm cast off the middle 17-19-21-23 sts for neck  and dec to shape the neckline on every other row: 1 st 2-2-2-2 times = 13-14-17-18 sts left on each shoulder. Cast off when piece measures 31-33-36-38 cm.

Sleeves: Cast on 34-36-38-40 sts on double pointed needles size 4 mm. Knit M.1 and inc 6-4-2-0 sts evenly on the last row = 40-40-40-40 sts. Continue in M.2. At the same time when piece measures 5 cm inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of 5-5-10-10 times on every 8-10-6-7 round = 50-50-60-60 sts – knit the inc sts in stocking sts until they fit into pattern. When piece measures 19-24-27-31 cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm and finish the sleeve back and forth on needle. Cast off for sleeve cap each side on every other row – see Cast off tips: 2 sts 1-1-3-3 times, 1 st 6-6-2-2 times, 2 sts 1-1-3-3 times and 3 sts 1-1-1-1 time. Cast of remaining sts, piece measures approx 26-31-34-38 cm. 

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 60 to 70 sts round the neck on needle size 4 mm and knit 4 rows garter sts. Cast off. Set in sleeves and sew on buttons.
