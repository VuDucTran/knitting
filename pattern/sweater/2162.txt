Sizes: 
child’s, 8/10 - 12/14 years 
(women’s, Small/Medium - Medium/Large - XLarge)
Finished Measurements: 100-110 (114-120-126) cm.

Materials: DROPS VAMP 
100% Acrylic
250-300 (500-550-600) g in Color No. 01, Rust.
and: DROPS PELLIZA, 
100% Polyester
150-150 (200-200-200) g in Color No. 10, Black.

5 (6) DROPS shell buttons (button no. 526).

Alternate yarns: (for Vamp)
* DROPS ULL-FLAMÉ, 100% Pure New Wool 

DROPS 3.5mm and 5mm circular needles and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain gauge.

Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comGauge: 12 sts x 16 rows on smaller needles in stockinette stitch = 10 x 10 cm . 

Garter Stitch, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.
Garter Stitch, when knitting in the round: *Rnd 1, knit. Rnd 2, purl.* Repeat from * - *.

Rib: * knit 1, purl 1. Repeat from *.

Body: Cast on 104-116 (120-124-128) sts on smaller circular needles with 2 strands Pelliza. Knit 2 cm  in garter stitch. Change to larger circular needles and single strand of Vamp and knit stockinette stitch. Place markers on the 1st row as follows: knit 26-29 (30-31-32) sts for the front, place marker, knit 52-58 (60-62-64) sts for the back, place marker, knit 26-29 (30-31-32) sts front. Inc 1 st on each side of the markers 4-4 (4-5-6) times every 4 cm . 120-132 (136-144-152) sts 
When the work measures 25-27 (29-31-32) cm , knit the next row as follows: knit 28-31 (32-34-36) sts for the front, bind off 4 sts for armhole, knit 56-62 (64-68-72) sts for the back, bind off 4 sts for armhole, knit 28-31 (32-34-36) sts for the front. Knit the rest of each part separately from this point.

Front: 28-31 (32-34-36) sts. Bind off for armholes every other row: 2 sts 2-2 (1-1-2) times, 1 st 1-1 (2-3-2) times. 23-26 (28-29-30) sts. 
When the work measures 38-42 (46-48-50) cm , bind off for the neck every other row: 5-6 (6-7-7) sts 1 time, 2 sts 1-1 (2-2-2) times, 1 st 1-1 (1-1-1) time. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (54-56-58) cm .

Back: 56-62 (64-68-72) sts. Bind off for armholes as on the front. 46-52 (56-58-60) sts. When the work measures 43-47 (52-54-56) cm , bind off the center 12-14 (18-20-20) sts for the neck. Bind off 2 sts at each neck edge on the next row. Bind off when the work measures 45-49 (54-56-58) cm .

Sleeves: Cast on 24-26 (28-30-30) sts on smaller double pointed needles with 2 strands of Pelliza. Join, being careful not to twist the sts. Place a marker at the join knit 2 cm  in garter stitch. Change to single strand of Vamp and knit stockinette stitch. When the work measures 6 cm , inc 1 st on each side of the marker 12-13 (16-15-17) times:
Sizes S/M + XL: every 3rd row. 
Sizes 8/10 years + M/L: alternately every 3rd and every 4th row. 
Size 12/14 years: every 4th row. 
= 48-52 (60-60-64) sts. When the work measures 36-42 (42-42-41) cm , bind off 2 sts on each side of the marker and knit the rest back and forth on the needles. Bind off for sleeve caps on each side every other row: 2 sts 4-4 (3-4-5) times, 4 sts 2-2 (2-2-2) times. Bind off. The work measures approximately 45-51 (50-51-51) cm .

Assembly: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approximately 60 (70) sts on smaller needles along the right front with Vamp and knit in rib back and forth on the needles. Bind off after 3 cm . Repeat along the left front, but after 1 cm  make 5-5 (6-6-6) buttonholes evenly distributed across the row. (1 buttonhole = bind off 1 stitch, cast on 1 new stitch over the bind off stitch on the next row.)
Pick up approximately 80 (90) sts around the neck and over the placket on smaller circular needles with 2 strands of Pelliza, knit 4 rows garter stitch. Bind off. Sew on the sleeves and buttons.
