Sizes: 8/10 (12/14) years
Finished measurements:
Bust: 70 (80) cm 
Waist: 62 (68) cm 

Materials: Garnstudio POPCORN, 
50% polyester, 41% viscose, 7% acrylic, 
2% nylon. 50 g./75 m 
200 (250) g color no. 01, black/gray/white

DROPS 4 mm  straight needles; 4.5 mm  straight and double pointed needles, or sizes needed to obtain correct gauge. 

1 DROPS Shell button (no. 525)
Gauge: 15 sts x 22 rows on larger needles in stockinette st = 10 x 10 cm .

Garter st, when knitting flat: Knit all sts, all rows.

Back: Cast on 48 (54) sts on smaller needles and knit 2 rows garter st. Change to larger needles and stockinette st. Inc 1 st at each side 3 (4) times every 6 (5) cm  = 54 (62) sts. When the work measures 21 (24) cm  knit the edge st at each side in garter st and inc 1 st at each side (inside the edge st) every other row 8 (10) times for cap sleeve = 70 (82) sts. When the work measures 34 (39) cm  divide the work in two for the split at the center back and knit the rest of each side separately, keeping the st at each edge of the split in garter st throughout. Bind off for shoulder every other row: 2 (3) sts 4 times, 3 (4) sts 1 time -- on the same row as the third shoulder bind off, begin binding off for the neck every other row: 9 (10) sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time. Bind off remaining sts, the work measures approx. 38 (43) cm .

Front: Cast on and knit as for the back but bind off for the neck when the work measures 31 (35) cm  as follows: bind off the center 10 (12) sts, then bind off at each neck edge every other row: 2 sts 2 times, 1 st 2 times. Bind off for shoulder every other row: 2 (3) sts 4 times, 3 (4) sts 1 time, bind off the remaining sts, the work measures approx. 38 (43) cm .

Assembly: Sew the shoulder seam. Sew the side seam = 21 (24) cm. Pick up approx. 80-90 sts around the neck on larger double pointed needles and knit 2 rows garter st, bind off. Make a little loop over 1 cm  (use a smooth yarn) on the right side of the split in back and sew on a button on the left side of the split.
