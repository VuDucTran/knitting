Color charts and updated retailer lists at www.garnstudio.comKnitting tension: 16 sts x 19 rows in stocking st on needle size 5.5 mm = 10 x 10 cm.

Rib: * K1, P1 *

Pattern : see diagram below.
1 square = 1 st x 1 row
Hele mønsteret glattstrikkes = the entire pattern is worked in stocking st
1 rapp. = 1 repeat, 24 sts

BACK PIECE: Cast on 60-66 (72-78-84) sts on needle size 3 mm with black, work 6 cm rib. Change to needle size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME inc or dec evenly on first row to 61-67 (69-75-79) sts. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 18-20 (20-23-25) cm work 1 vertical pattern repeat and then complete piece in grey. AT THE SAME TIME when piece measures 37-40 (42-45-48) cm slip the middle 29-29 (31-31-33) sts on a stitch holder for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off remaining 16-19 (19-22-23) sts on shoulder when piece measures 39-43 (47-51-53) cm.

FRONT PIECE: Cast on and work like back piece until piece measures 34-37 (39-42-45) cm. Now slip the middle 23-23 (25-25-27) sts on a stitch holder for neck and complete each shoulder separately. Cast off to shape the neckline at the beg of every row from neckline: 1 st 3 times. Cast off remaining 16-19 (19-22-23) sts on shoulder when piece measures 39-43 (47-51-53) cm.

SLEEVE: Cast on 34-36 (38-38-40) sts on needle size 3 mm with grey, work 6 cm rib. Change to needle size 5.5 mm and continue in stocking st, AT THE SAME TIME adjust no of sts to 34-38 (42-46-50) sts evenly on first row. Now inc 1 st each side 10-10 (11-12-12) times on every 3-3 (4-4-5) row = 54-58 (64-70-74) sts on needle. Cast off when sleeve measures 24-27 (32-36-39) cm.

ASSEMBLY: Sew shoulder seams. Pick up approx 68-72 (74-76-78) sts round the neck on small circular needle size 3 mm with grey. Work 3 cm rib, cast off. Set in sleeves to. Lightly steam iron jumper on WS.
