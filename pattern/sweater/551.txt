Size: Months: 1/3 - 6/9 - 12/18 
Size: Years: 2 (3/4 - 5/6 - 7/8 - 9/10).
Size in cm: 50/56 - 62/68 - 74/78 - 86/92 (98/104 - 110/116 - 122/128 - 134/140).

Materials: DROPS Den-M-Nit from GARNSTUDIO
350-400-400-450 (500-550-600-650) g colour no 51, mid blue.

Or use: DROPS COTTON LIGHT from GARNSTUDIO
350-400-400-400 (450-500-550-600) g colour no 26, jeans blue.

DROPS circular needle and double pointed needles size 3 mm and 3.5 mm.
DROPS wooden button–light no. 503: 2 pcs. (for the 3 smallest sizes only).Knitting tension: 22 sts x 30 rows on needle size 3.5 mm and M.4 = 10 x 10 cm. 

Note: The needle sizes are guidelines only!

Rib: * K 2, P 2 *. Repeat from *-*.

Garter st (back and forth): Knit all rows

Garter st (on circular needle): 1st round: Purl, 2nd round: Knit.

Pattern: See diagram (1 diagram = 1 repeat). The pattern in the diagram is seen from the RS.  
Back and Front piece: The cable part of the 2 largest sizes is wider than on the other sizes. Cast on 120-132-144-160 (176-192-200-216) sts on circular needle size 3 mm in blue and knit Rib for 2-2-2-2 (3-3-3-3) cm. Change to circular needle size 3.5 mm and knit 2 rounds in garter sts increasing 36-36-36-36 (36-36-60-60) sts evenly on the first round = 156-168-180-196 (212-228-260-276) sts. Knit next round as follows: *18-24-30-38 (46-54-34-42) sts of M.4, 18-18-18-18 (18-18-36-36) sts of M.1, 24-24-24-24 (24-24-24-24) sts of M.2, 18-18-18-18 (18-18-36-36) sts of M.3 *, repeat from *-*. Continue in pattern like this. Remember the knitting tension! When piece measures 23-24-27-28 (29-31-35-36) cm knit next row as follows: 6-9-12-16 (20-24-14-18) sts, cast off 6 sts for armhole, 72-78-84-92 (100-108-124-132) sts, cast off 6 sts for armhole, 66-69-72-76 (80-84-110-114) sts (place the pattern in the same position on the front and back piece). Put the piece aside and knit the sleeves. 

Sleeve: Cast on 36-40-40-44 (44-48-48-52) sts on double pointed needles size 3 mm in blue and knit Rib for 2-2-2-2 (3-3-3-3) cm. Change to double pointed needles size 3.5 mm and garter sts 2 rows. Continue in M.4, at the same time inc 2 sts mid under arm a total of  4-4-6-6 (12-12-14-16) times on every 11th -13th -9th -12th (6th  -7th -6th-6th) round = 44-48-52-56 (68-72-76-84) sts. When piece measures 17-19-21-26 (27-31-34-38) cm cast off 6 sts mid under arm = 38-42-46-50 (62-66-70-78) sts.

Back and front pieces + sleeve: Place the sleeves on the same circular needle as the jersey where you cast off for armholes = 220-240-260-284 (324-348-388-420) sts on needle. Insert a marking thread in all transitions between sleeves and body pieces = 4 marking threads. Continue the pattern but stocking st the first st on either side of the marking threads (= 2 stocking sts in the transitions between jersey and sleeve).

Raglan decreases: Read the entire section before knitting it! There are 8 dec per round (1 dec on either side of the 2 Stocking sts). Knit 2 rounds before starting the dec. Now dec 1 st on each side of the marking threads: 12-14-16-17 (22-23-22-22) times on every other round, thereafter 1 st 6-5-4-5 (4-5-10-13) times on every round (a total of 144-152-160-176 (208-224-256-280) dec sts for the raglan).
Decrease as follows before the 2 stocking sts: 
From the RS: K2 tog. 
From the WS: (does not apply until after the neck dec): Slip the next 2 sts off the needle, twist and place them back on the left needle (one after the other), purl the 2 sts tog into back of st. 
Decrease as follows after the 2 Stocking sts: 
From the RS: Slip 1 st as if to knit, K1, psso.
From the WS: (does not apply until after the neck dec): P2 tog. 

At the same time when there are 6-6-6-6 (8-8-8-8) rounds of raglan decreases left cast off for neck mid front (for all sizes) and for back split (for the 3 smallest sizes) as follows: Put the front middle 20-22-24-26 (28-30-34-36) sts on a thread for neck and complete the piece back and forth on needle. At the same time cast off the middle 6 sts on the back for split on the 3 smallest sizes. Shape the neckline by putting sts on the thread on every other row as follows: The 3 smallest sizes: 2 sts 2 times, the 5 largest sizes: 3 sts 1 time, 2 sts 1 time, 1 st 1 time (a total of 28-30-32-34 (40-42-46-48) sts are put on the thread for neck at the front). After the last dec there are 42-52-62-74 (76-82-86-92) sts on needle and the jersey measures approx 33-35-39-41 (45-48-53-55) cm.

Assembly: 
The 3 smallest sizes: Pick up approx 10 sts along the split at the left back on needle size 3 mm. Purl 1 row (seen from the RS) and then knit Rib for 2.5 cm. Cast off. Repeat along the right side of the split but cast off for one buttonhole after 0.5 cm approx 1 cm from the top - 1 buttonhole = cast off 2 sts and cast on 2 new sts over the cast off sts on the next row. Cast off. Slip all the neck sts on to double pointed needles size 3 mm and pick up a further 6 sts over each split edge on the back. K 1 row at the same time dec 12 sts evenly over the cable part at the front and decrease 12 sts evenly over the cable part on the back (back = cast off 6 sts on each side of the split) = 58-70-82 sts. K1 row from the WS, now knit 3 cm Rib – at the same time when the rib measures 2 cm cast off for 1 buttonhole over the other buttonhole. Cast off in Rib. Sew the opening under the arms and sew in the button on the back split.
The 5 largest sizes: Slip all the neck sts onto double pointed needle size 3 mm. K 1 row at the same time dec12 (12-12-15-15) sts evenly over the cable part on the front and decrease 12 (12-12-15-15) evenly over the cable part on the back = 84 (92-100-102-110) sts.
